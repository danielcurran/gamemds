# 8. Lufia's Database

Perhaps the title of this section is more appropriate for Fortress of Doom, but this is indeed a database of weapons after all! Here you'll find several lists of dueling arms, defensive armours, and some rather Bobbie dazzlin' Rings and Jewels (Rocks)....well, I guess this section was quite incomplete before, but i've put some work into this place and guess what, no more boring tables for me to produce! If there's some BAD things which i've missed, please e-mail me, and give me a nice description, read the Special Thanks section! I treat all of the contributors with unlimited respect and crediting!

Before we get into the charts, i'll be pleased to mention various abbreviations

in this section, these are for elements because I would hate to keep using the

words, Strong vs Flying Enemies on a frequent basis instead I could use Square

Brackets with an "F" in the middle, just like what grade I got for my GCSE in
