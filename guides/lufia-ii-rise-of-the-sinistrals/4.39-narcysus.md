## 4.39. Narcysus

**MAIN GOALS** Let the Girls get Kidnapped! (By a Flute noise???) Buy the latest gear, weapons, armour, et cetera!!!

Upon entering Narcysus, you will find loads of cute girl's, this place will be empty as soon as you enter the main area of the village. The girl's will be kidnapped by what seems to be a flute noise, but for some reason, Selan isn't affected; because she's married. When the girl's disappear, head for the equipment store and buy the latest weapons and armour, for the boys get three long swords, three plated cloth's and three plated caps; which should cost you a pretty penny. For Selan, you should get a war rapier, a Turban and some Tecto Gloves; Selan doesn't need new armour because she's got the Magic Bikini, which is fairly good armour already. You might want to sort out your party formation too, let's put Dekar first, Maxim second and Guy Third, leaving our lovely lady friend fourth; because of her lower defense power. When you've got all the desired equipment, you should rest at the inn, and head for the Northern tower, known as the tower of sacrifice.
