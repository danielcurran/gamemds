# 7. Capsule Monsters

Monsters, some of them Kill us, some help us...the ones who scrap with us are

our enemies, however....we have an exception to this rule...you know! the one

where "All" Monsters are "Bad" and the game which strikes to mind is, Lufia II;

hiding within the colourful landscape we call "Lufia World" there are several

"Capsule Monsters" who are here to aid Maxim and Co throughout the adventure!

@ Brief Lecture!

Many of us would be confused at the term "Capsule Monsters" although if you are a Pokémon Fan, then these type of creatures will be familiar to you! With these fiends in your team, you are most likely going to fell better, it's like having a fifth ally, which CANNOT be controlled by YOU! As these creatures level up; techniques will be gained, feeding them will help them grow into larger forms and battle experience will increase their status. Let me introduce the little buggers to you....starting with....

```
@@@@@
JELZE     Neutral (Non-Elemental)
@@@@@
The Normal Standard ball of cotton wool; Jelze is a fairly average capsule
monster with some strong Physical attacks, to begin with, Jelze has a high
Guts rating; so he will run away only on RARE occasions.....his offensive and
defensive stats are good, and he is fair when it comes to magic resistance; I
would have him at a high level; you will get him as a lump of fluff, but when
he reaches his master level, he will become a Gold Fox; which has some really
cool physical attacks...give him a shot on your team, he's worth a go!
```

Location: When you have completed the tasks around Sundletan, enter the small shrine leading to Alunze and move anti-clockwise around the mountain range; keep going and you should find a lone tree, enter this tree; walk over to the water and claim "Jelze" for your collection!

```
@@@@@
BLAZE     Fire
@@@@@
Blaze can engulf his enemies in flames, he is strong in some places and weak
in others, his main weakness is Guts! Blaze will run when things get hard and
dangerous; but he is both offensive Physically and Magically which is always
a Bonus....keep feeding Blaze so he evolves into his awesome "Fire Bird" form
which is real cool! He is quite Defensive and he has average magic resistance
compared to some monsters; he's a good choice however!
```

**Location:** During the tasks of Clamento, enter Ruby Cave and find an armoured

Dog in a hidden place, that's Blaze and he will join the team when you find him! Do NOT miss him..this monster is valuable!

```
@@@@@
GUSTO     Wind
@@@@@
Gusto is capable of travelling the winds with high velocity, he is the Capsule
monster of Wind! His main status would be his Intelligence and Speed, he is the
fastest of all capsule monsters and he has some "Blow Away" attacks...like his
Cyclone wave and Electro Bolt techniques! I would have him around for the quick
battles where speed plays a large role...if you are fighting Daos...Gusto is a
bad choice as he will die within one blow of Daos's "DARK FRY"...However; he is
useful and should not be missed!
```

**Location:** Inside the Tower of Sacrifice, when you get a choice of three doors;

take a peak inside the middle door and Gusto will be waiting for the Pick-Up, he will join and you have him forever!

```
@@@@@
SULLY     Soil (Earth Elemental)
@@@@@
Sully is very much like GUY and DEKAR, he is strong physically and he has the
intelligence of a Decomposed Turnip...however he is an essential monster to be
obtained, you NEED to get hold of him! Sully would be the favourite of everyone
who owns this game, because unlike the others....Sully can take loads of pain
and he never runs without a good reason...he lacks speed and magic resistance
however, which can be a major downside against fast enemies! DO NOT MISS THIS
MONSTER; he is the real deal!
```

**Location:** Flower Mountain, he is fairly hard to miss, and he is well worth the

bother....he is a must find monster and he would be a worthy monster for your collection!

```
@@@@@
ZEPPY     Water
@@@@@
Zeppy lacks speed a little and his other stats like strength and intelligence
are average....he still rules when it comes to Magic Resistance and HP however;
he is a useful monster to have around but he lacks powerful magical techniques;
instead he has a whole hoard of Physical attacks....overall he's a good choice
for your team; you had better keep Zeppy in his Level 4 form so he can use his
Magic attacks, the "Lightning" elemental ones!
```

Location: Some cave with a screwed up name! You will need the submarine and the Warp Spell or item! Cast "Warp" for 8MP and return to Parcelyte, sink underwater and look for a hole in the seabed...now move around until you've reached one of the whirlpools, some contain Fruits and one of them will lead you to ZEPPY! So you now have the RED FISH!

```
@@@@@
FLASH     Light (Sacred Elemental)
@@@@@
The Luminous Angel of monsters, she is Flash...the BRIGHT SPARK...due to her
abnormal intelligence stats. Flash would be one of my favourite monsters, she
can use some awesome healing spells and her physical status gets better when
she eats more...or evolves into her larger forms! She is well worth snagging
as she will play a major role when it comes to healing, she can even surpass
Selan in healing with her higher intelligence! Her HP's are the only problem;
Flash is limited and she's got the least HP of all capsule monsters; but do
not let this fool you, Flash is by far "one of the best" capsule monsters in
the game..so GIVE ER' A TRY!
```

Location: You can get your mitts on Flash if you visit the North Labyrinth during the tasks set within the Bound Kingdom....You Know! The help Prince Alex and Dekar Thing...you will need your hook to reach this monster, when you have her....you should have a suitable familiar for the "White Witch" Selan!

@@@@@

DARBI Dark (Shadow Elemental) @@@@@ Very! Very! Mysterious, I like him, even though he is Gutless...and his Defense would also be bad! He has some negative status techniques....the usual, Confuse and Poison spells, AND, instant death attacks; which are always handy...if you need some monster to have single round battles with your enemies then Darbi is the ideal choice; he is still lame and he has poor Magic Resistance...including his Guts; but if you need a tough o' darkness to kill your foes; call Darbi's number and watch him in action!

**Location:** Darbi is located in a small shrine on "Crescent island" so finding

him should not be too much of a problem, when you get your ship he is accessible; so don't hold back when it comes to locating him!
