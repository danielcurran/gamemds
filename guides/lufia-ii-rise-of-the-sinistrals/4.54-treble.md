## 4.54. Treble

**MAIN GOALS** Purchase a "Circlet" for Lexis and Selan! Find out about Professor Kirmo, a friend of Lexis!

Well this is a small village with no main errands to run, you can purchase a circlet from the equipment store; and you can speak to a little boy when you enter this town. He speaks of a bloke named Kirmo, and it seems that the guy he mentions is a friend of Lexis; the man he mentions is also working on a submarine; and he's in the town of Portravia to the south, and to give you another reason to head south, the villagers have seen a strange light, South of Treble, and near the town of Portravia, so walk south of Treble and go to the large town below, it is fairly easy to find after all...'.'
