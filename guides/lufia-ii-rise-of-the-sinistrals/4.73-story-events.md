## 4.73. Story Events

**MAIN GOALS** Get Lexis to remodel the Excerion, with the new engine! Reach Narvick using your new Airship!

Looks like your in Portravia, so check out Kirmo's Lab and speak to Lexis, he wants to begin his work straight away; we hear strange noises during the ship upgrade, when it is all done, Lexis wants you to give the new vessel a quick test, so he can see it in working order...so test the ship, use it to reach a town on the Tri-force group of islands you seen earlier, remember the islands are located a little further in the western direction of Portravia..and North of Preamarl...head for the bottom island with a Town; enter this town and you will notice that this place is called Narvick...and if you have anything more to do outside of this place, the now would be a good time to do some of those things...you are approaching the ending stages of the game as from now!
