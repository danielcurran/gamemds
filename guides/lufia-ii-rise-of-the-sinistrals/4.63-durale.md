## 4.63. Durale

**MAIN GOALS** Upgrade Equipment and Spells; Get the ZAP spell in Particular! Find out the Whereabouts of the Tower of Truth, and follow Iris!

- **Well, you will like the spell shop in this town, that's for sure, this shop**
  - sells the "Zap" spell, also known as the strongest Holy Magic spell in the
  - game, it's major downside is the fact that Selan cannot use it; instead one
  - elf can use this and that's good ol' Artea...The other spell in this place
  - can only be used by Selan and Maxim, so poor ol' Artea will just have to make
  - do with the Zap Spell. The Zap spell costs 65,000GP, yeah that's a lot o' GP
  - but you can afford this!

Spells should all be purchased, lets get onto the weapons, first off, Maxim should have a Silver Sword and Guy would fight well using a Heavy Lance; as for Selan and Artea, I would get Selan a Silver Rod and I think Artea should stick with "Arty's Bow" because it hits all enemies on the screen. Armours are next, and I wouldn't bother with the Crystal Mail or Crystal Robes, your best bet is the Crysto Helm for Maxim and Guy, and the Crysto Beret for Arty and Selan...and finally you should buy a Gauntlet shield for the whole team. When your done shopping, walk around town a little and most villagers will speak of a young woman heading west, and in this case she's off to the Tower of Truth...So! let us follow her!
