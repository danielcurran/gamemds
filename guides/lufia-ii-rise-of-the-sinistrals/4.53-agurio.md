## 4.53. Agurio

**MAIN GOALS** Bury the residents of this town! Speak to Iris and find out what happened!

What the Hell!!!! Amon really has done a number in this town, and who is on the scene, it's Iris....Does this woman have anything to do with this, she is looking rather suspicious don't you think? Iris looks upset, or she isn't too proud of herself for not reaching the village in time to stop Amon; she wants Maxim to quit his journey, and to be fair she suggests he should live a peaceful life in the town of Parcelyte. The conversation turns into a lecture on energy waves, and according to the Legend there's a sword that can amplify these waves; the legendary DUAL BLADE. Iris goes on some more about this Dual Blade, and finally the villagers get sent to the ground, and they can Rest in Peace along with any other souls out there...'.'

After the Funeral, Maxim and Co. will decide to find the Dual Blade, and Iris will leave with a strange remark to Maxim's question; we find out who she really is later in the game, but it doesn't matter right now; so head out of this town and head for Treble, which just happens to be a small village South of the Ferim Kingdom.
