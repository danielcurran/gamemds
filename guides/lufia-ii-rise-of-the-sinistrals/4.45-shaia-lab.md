## 4.45. Shaia Lab

**MAIN GOALS** Find the owner, Lexis Shaia! Make your way through this strange maze! Make Contact with Jyad, so he will build a ship for you!

This is the Lab of Lexis Shaia, and the place is full of traps, we have to go through the right doors or we are in trouble! Okay, let's begin; head through the first door and then follow on through the right door in the next room; in the next room make your way through the Left door, then cut down the vines on the Northern wall to reveal a hidden door. Through the door is a staircase which leads to the main room; inside Lexis is waiting, so immediately talk to him, and learn that his engine is not compatible with any ships; in this case the engine is too strong, and only Jyad could make a ship strong enough to make use of this engine. He's also got a strange device called a Kymograph; it reads how strong everyones energy is; and in this case "GUY" gets a score of five, Selan gets eight and Maxim goes off the scale!

You need to find Jyad, but where could he be; if you must know, he's back in the town of Aleyn; so lets use the "Warp" spell! When you reach Aleyn, go to Jyad's house, he will be happy to help you, and he asks for you to wait for him in Treadool; now warp back to the Shaia Lab and talk to Lexis; he also wants you to wait in Treadool; so use the warp spell and head back in town!
