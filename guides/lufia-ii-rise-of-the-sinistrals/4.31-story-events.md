## 4.31. Story Events

**MAIN GOALS** Receive some shocking news, regarding Selan's Hometown! Get Dekar and Guy to Join your party! Get Prepared for battle! -> Uncurse the "Deadly Sword" - '1,250G'

It seems that Gades came to the castle while you were at the Labyrinth; and he only spoke to the King, which is pretty understandable. The King will tell you some bad news, by the looks of it; Gades has decided to destroy Parcelyte and the Bound Kingdom; this is not good, as you may have already guessed. The girls aren't in your party for this mission; so it looks like its up to Maxim and Dekar. Whats this? its your ol' friend GUY; his sister has recovered and he wants to help you slaughter Gades, which means you now have three warriors to play with. When the Girl's leave for Parcelyte, be sure to purchase some new equipment for Guy especially, and I suppose Dekar could also use another weapon, probably the Thunder Ax which you obtained from the Labyrinth; also you may want to give Guy the Deadly sword, when its been uncursed. When you have the desired equipment, its time to attack Gades, so get prepared for a real battle, make sure you have loads of cool equipment equipped, and build Dekar up to level 25 or so; on the monsters outside the castle. When you have reached the desired levels; you should head for the ancient tower, which is located east of the Bound Kingdom.
