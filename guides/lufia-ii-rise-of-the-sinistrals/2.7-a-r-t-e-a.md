## 2.7. A R T E A

Elven Swordmaster, and wielder of Luminous Magic Spells; he joins Maxim and his comrades when a Sinistral takes the life of his friend. His deceased friend has left her daughter all alone, but when it comes to this lil' child, Artea treats her like his own. Artea is a Cross between Robin Hood, and Cecil from FF4; and this seems to be good, because he fights like a Paladin, with the Holy Magic spells and stuff; He is also known as "Arty" by some, tis' simply a nickname commonly used on the Lufia Status screen! He can handle swords, bows, Rods and even Heavy Armors; but his strength is limited. He's the most agile character; and he is more intelligent than the likes of Maxim and Guy; his physical stats are bearable, and he's a good choice for an ancient cave team! He does not come close to Selan even though he can use "Fry" and "Zap" which are two cool magic spells; still, you have to give this bloke a chance, he will do you proud after all, and his abilities are not at all disappointing!
