## 2.5. G U Y

The Best friend of Maxim, always supporting him and occasianly tries to rival Dekar. He has Blond hair, a cute girlfriend named Jessy, and a fit sister named Hilda, who is taken ill near the start of the game. He dares to challenge one of the sinistrals before anyone else, but loses and it should also be known that he will always be in the team near the games ending, along with Artea and Selan. He is more agile and brainier than Dekar, but he's always cracking sad jokes; which make Selan fell pissed; because of this, him and Selan have some petty squabbles, usually stopped by Maxim. He can equip Heavy Swords and Axes like the "Almighty" Dekar, a true ally of the Doom island team!

Second strongest warrior, suppasses Maxim in strength and HP, like Dekar he is unable to use Magic spells, hence we would be expected to use IP Techs instead! Like Dekar, the game can be hacked to give him spells as well as some MP, which he doesn't have either, still he is one of my prefered characters!

**Final Equipment:** Sky Sword, Bolt Shield, Legend Helm, Old Armor, Rocket Ring &

Black Eye.
