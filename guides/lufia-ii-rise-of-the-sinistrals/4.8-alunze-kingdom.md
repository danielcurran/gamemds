## 4.8. Alunze Kingdom

**MAIN GOALS** Purchase new equipment! Gain some info on the King's Coronation! Play about in the casino! *NOT ESSENTIAL* Raise Maxim and Tia's Levels up to 10/11!

This village has quite a few things to keep you occupied; first of all, you should buy some new equipment. This shop offers some good stuff; you should purchase all the cloth stuff and a short sword for Maxim; as well as a Mini Shield and Long Knife for Tia. When you've finished shopping; equip all your new armour and weapons, and head for the Inn. You should rest to restore all your HP and MP, then you should go down the stairs. Down here, you will find a Casino; you might as well have a little go on the slot machine; coz unlike games like Pokémon, you will actually win quite often. When you've finished wasting your money; you should proceed to Alunze Castle.

Before you head for Alunze Castle, you may want to gain a few level; Maxim should be around Lv12 and Tia will be fine at Lv10/11. This will also give you the chance to gain more Gold, and one of the Eagles you fight, may drop an Eagle Stone. When you've reached your desired level, head for the Castle of Alunze; and prepare for the Kings Coronation!
