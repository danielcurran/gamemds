## 4.59. Story Events

**MAIN GOALS** Return to Eserikto and give Milka the Tragic News! Acquire your new Submarine from Lexis in Portravia!

When you get back to Eserikto, you will run into Milka, Artea delivers the news to her and it looks as though she takes it quite well. Again, we have a stupid typo..Instead of Guy's name being GUY, Selan calls him GAI! Milka will run off so she can grieve in peace; so you can now control your party, if you don't have the spells available in this place, then now is the time to get them..otherwise exit this town and head back to Portravia; by using the warp spell. When you reach Portravia, enter Kirmo's Lab and speak to Lexis. After he gives the Submarine to you he will get onto the Sinistral Subject, and you will find out the whereabouts of Amon..It seems he has gone under the sea and he's heading for a town called Barnan; so let's get to that town as soon as possible. To reach Barnan, sail in the southern direction, until you hit the deep waters; which will be represented with the Darker Blue Colour; then sail in the western direction, you should see a large town; this is Barnan!
