## 4.44. Treadool

**MAIN GOALS** Learn about an old geezer name Lexis Shaia! Check out the vessel for sale, and who it belongs too!

Treadool is another one of those port towns, and this is also a good place to pick up some great weapons, let's start with the blue haired dude, who should be near the entrance of this town; he wants to sell you a ship; and if you want to gain some info on who the vessel belongs to; by all means go along with his sale. We find out it belongs to an old geezer named Lexis Shaia, you will also find out that he's a Genius Inventor; and that he owns his very own laboratory; that's nice, but we need to keep him in a good mood if he wants to sell his vessel; before you leave town, you should buy some new equipment!

Time for some serious weapon upgrades, Maxim needs a Rockbreaker, Selan needs a Morning Star and Guy needs a Bronze Ax. When it comes to armours, you will need some serious cash; the amount of armours, shields and helmets available for your party, will set you back a few quid; now be sure to purchase all the items on my shopping list below...!

2 × Spike Shield - 2 × SilverHelmet - 1 × Glass Cloche 1 × Slash Shield - 1 × Silk Toga - 1 × Light Jacket - 1 × Silver Armor -

If you like that latest stuff, then get a big wallet; but one things for sure we have to find Lexis; so rest at the inn and head North a few paces, we come to a small cave, go inside and don't move till you start reading the upcoming paragraph; it is a small maze after all!
