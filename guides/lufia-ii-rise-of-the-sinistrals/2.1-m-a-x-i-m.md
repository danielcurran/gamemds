## 2.1. M A X I M

The Red Haired Hero, master swordsman and monster hunter is quite a character; he can use various swords and magic spells, hence allowing him to cause severe amounts of pain to his foes. Like all RPG Heroes, Maxim has a specific task in Life, he has to rid the world of evil, and obliterate the four sinistrals who appear to be against humans ruling the world. On his adventure he'll find lots of treasures and befriend various people to aid him in this struggle. One will become his wife, the two will have a child; and name him Jeros. The main cause of going on this adventure is a woman named Iris, she helps guide him on his travels, and assists him in certain cases, NOT in battle!

Although his skills are average, some are above normal, his Strength and Health stats are quite reliable and despite him being the Hero, he is unable to equip HEAVY swords and axes, he is limited to Light Swords and Rapiers. He will be in the team forever, if you don't like him, i'm afraid you've got a problem! Also he can't use Fire Magic spells, which would be a MASSIVE problem...still, give him Champion and Valor and he's cool!

Final Equipment:- Dual Blade, Pearl Shield, Pearl Helmet, Pearl Armor, Dragon Ring & Evil Jewel (Uncursed).
