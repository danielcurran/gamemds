## 4.5. Sundletan

**MAIN GOALS** Purchase new equipment! Get caught up in an earthquake! Raise Maxim's Level up to 5/6!

When you arrive in Sundletan, you will get caught up in an earthquake, which doesn't actually cause any harm to you, or the man standing by. Sundletan has had these earthquakes frequently; but the only clue you get to the cause, is from the old man in the Northern part of town.

The General store, sells some good equipment, you should purchase a Dagger & some boomerangs. The Magic shops sells a couple of spells that can be used by Maxim; for some reason, Maxim cannot use any Fire elemental spells; which is not too cool. Still, you should buy, the Strong and Droplet spells; so Maxim now has some useable magic.

Last of all, you should gather some information from the people around town; and if you need to, you can go to the inn; if your skint; you can still rest for free, which is strange, infact the inn keeper doesn't even say anything, about your poverty; it's a good deal I must say. When you leave the village, you may want to gain a few levels; Maxim would be able to cope, if he was at about, level 5. This shouldn't take too long, considering the experience pts available around the area. After gaining a few levels, you should head a few steps to the North, from Sundletan; where you will find the Lake Cave.
