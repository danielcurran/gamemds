## 4.48. Story Events

**MAIN GOALS** Present the flower to Leefa! Get Lexis to Join your team! Receive Jyad's new vessel!

Rare and Beautiful, this is the most wonderful flower in existence, and how will the Lovely Leefa react to your discovery. When Leefa wakes up; she will be so amazed by the flower, that she will offer you some money; but Selan is so kind and gentle, that she wishes to give the flower away, as a present. Leefa is so happy and promises Selan a flower when the plants grow, so you can be sure of something in return. Now Leefa has your flower, you should be looking for Lexis, he can be found near the docks and he's waiting for Jyad; when the Ship Builder arrives, Lexis will join your team.

Now, sail to Dankirk; it can be found south of Treadool, there's another area called Auralio, and a place called Forfeit island in the same region. Dankirk is simply a castle with an in-built town, just like Dekar's Hometown, the Bound Kingdom. I am also going to give you more directions, Forfeit island is far west of Dankirk, and Auralio is a few waves south; it would be a good idea to remember this as you need to visit one of these places pretty soon!
