## 4.46. Story Events

**MAIN GOALS** Meet the Local Flower Girl, Leefa! Head for Flower Mountain to the South!

Hmmmmm....back in this place for the second time, there's a cute girl near the town's entrance, she is selling flowers; talk to her and she will offer you one for one piece o' gold; that is cheap and as a bonus, the girl will faint, causing both Maxim and Guy to catch her. We will take her home and let her rest, she wakes up soon after, and she wants to pick the flowers from the Southern Mountain, she will be knocked out by Selan's Drowsy spell and some kids will get worried, Selan explains that the spell won't harm Leefa, and your party leaves the house in search of the flowers. So rest at the inn, head a few paces east of the village and head south, you will soon come to Flower Mountain!
