## 4.9. Alunze Castle

**MAIN GOALS** Locate the Castle Basement! Watch some Clowns in action!

Alunze Castle, is home to a pathetic army of soldiers; who by the looks of it cannot do their job properly. First of all, just keep on heading forward, you should reach the Throne room, very soon. Inside you will find the king, and a few other people; including the Prince and Princess.

The crown seems to be a fraud, so one of the soldiers attempts to take it for examination; but he is soon halted in his tracks. The guys with the crown are thieves, they will put on a little show; and it won't take long for them to steal the crown. After the thieves have left, you can talk to the King; he is not from the same planet as normal people; so he doesn't have a clue what you need to do, when your crown gets nicked.

The clowns have disappeared into the basement, so look for a balcony with two girls walking around; and head though the Right door, until you reach a Guard who appears to be Ko'ed. Keep going and head into the basement; you will find a useful tool here, which can help you to blow things up!
