## 4.41. Karlloon

**MAIN GOALS** Raid the Stores, Buy weapons, armours and spells! Here the Villagers speak of a super being to the NORTH!

Raiding those stores would be a good idea, this is Karlloon, where you should be doing some serious shopping. This is a small town, and there's only a few houses around the area, but the shops really do make life a pleasure to live! Let's begin with Karlloon's General store; Maxim would be best suited with a Multi sword, coz it can attack twice in one turn; Selan should be using the cutter whip; and all the lads should be using the latest defensive gear; so make sure you purchase three plate helmets, a metal mail, an Iron plate and a Silvo Shield, you should equip those on the appropriate characters and head for the Magic Store!

There's loads of spells in this place, you should be getting the Absorb spell first; it's handy if you need to regain some Magic points. Secondly, the Bolt spell would be cool; and you might as well get the last of those spells, if funding isn't a problem. There's very little stuff to do in this place after shopping, but you could talk to some of the villagers; there's rumor's that a super being is lurking about in the Northern Shrine, so that explains where you need to be heading next. You should rest at the inn if your HP's are low; then you can makes tracks for the Northern Shrine, in search of either Idura; or one of the Sinistral's, who knows what lies beyond!
