## 4.16. Story Events

**MAIN GOALS** Meet Guy's Girlfriend, Jessy! Obtain Camu's Treasure!

Items -> Fire Dagger, Camu Armour, Pearl Brace

Upon entering Tanbel once again, Hilda will need some rest; and Guy will want to help Maxim destroy the Super Beings known as the Sinistrals. In this scene you will meet up with Jessy, who is no other than Guy's Girlfriend, and for some reason, Tia takes a liking to her, simply because, well I don't know coz its a girls thing. Anyway, Guy decides to join you in your fight against evil or should I say, the Sinistrals.

When Guy finally joins, head back to the tower where you rescued Hilda. First of all, progress to the top floor, and into the boss room; where you defeated Camu. This room appears to contain three chests, which hold some really cool treasures; the first treasure is a Fire Dagger; this should be equipped by a swordsman, so this time, let Maxim have this treasure, as Guy is the only one who can equip the second treasure, which is the Camu Armour. The last of the treasures, is a Pearl Brace Shield, this should only be equipped by Tia. When you've obtained the treasures, head back to the bottom floor. You will find a door, which you never visited before, as it used to lead to a dead end. This time the pillars seem to have fallen, this will give you the chance to visit a pretty little village named Clamento; which is a few paces North, from what seems to be the towers main exit!
