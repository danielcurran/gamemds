Whats up with the Level 99 stats...??? -------------------------------------- Your characters gain stats at random values, because this happens, a character will NEVER have the same stats at level 99 when if you beat the game more than once...BUT your characters will always have stats which are similar to the previous game. If Maxim has an intelligence stat of 150, within the next game; this stat could be 148 or 153 or even 155; which is nearly the same, but being a few numbers out, this stat is NOT the same within each time you gain the Maximum Level of 99...Still, I can give you some rough values, and if you were ever interested in which character has the BEST stats, start reading as of now and stop if I'm driving you insane!
