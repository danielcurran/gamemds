## 4.12. Alunze North Shrine

**MAIN GOALS** Find out about the key to Tanbel! Meet up with a girl called Hilda!

The North Shrine is located North of the Alunze Kingdom; when you arrive, you will hear that the key to Tanbel has been stolen. The thief seems to be a boy named Abel; you can find this lad at the cave west of this shrine; where some monsters also lurk. Here you also meet up with a girl named Hilda, who does appear to be rather distressed by all this, she also has a brother who gets really worried when she disappears for large periods of time. After summing up the events here, you should head west, till you reach the south-west cave!
