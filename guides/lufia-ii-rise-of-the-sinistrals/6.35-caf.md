## 6.35. Café

Funny enough, this place does not sell beverages, the sprites in this place are here to give you some extra tips on the ancient cave. This is also a museum in some respects, if you get your hands on a few Iris Treasures, this is where you can get them displayed...otherwise this is a normal building with nothing to offer...which makes it rather sad!
