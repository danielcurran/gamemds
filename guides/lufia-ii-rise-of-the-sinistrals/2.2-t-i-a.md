## 2.2. T I A

The Humble Shopkeeper, amateur magician and childhood friend of Maxim. She is one of the most equippable characters in the game, she is able to use rods and whips to cause damage, including a few spears and knives. Being Humble is her major role, she needs protecting due to her carelessness. She assists Maxim a little, and she buggers off back to Elcid never to be seen again. I suppose I can like her skills?

She has as much strength and endurance as a new-born child, and her magic stats are above average. She is almost as quick as Artea, and she can use near enough the same spells as Selan! She uses Whips and Rods more than Knives, and those come with some useful IP Techniques, overall I would call her one of the good characters, I still prefer Selan!

Final Equipment:- Snow Sword, Dark Mirror (Uncursed), Brill Helm, Ruse Armor, Dia Ring & Gold Eye.
