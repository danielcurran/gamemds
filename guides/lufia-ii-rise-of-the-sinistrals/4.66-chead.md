## 4.66. Chead

**MAIN GOALS** The Normal Deal, Weapons and Armours for Sale! Find out the whereabouts of the Elder!

Chead is a small town, which can only be accessed underwater; by the use of a Submarine. Now! Let's begin with the usual practice...let us go to a weapon & armour store; if you go inside, you will find some cool weapons for everyone to equip; Let's purchase a Buster Sword for Maxim, a Great Ax for Guy and two Zirco Rod's for Selan and Artea. Any of the armours would be fitting, and if you are looking for a problem, then you might want to know that the armours provided in this town, can not be equipped by MAXIM!

Apart from the usual weapon store, you could try visiting the Inn, just to gain a few HP's here and there, you may also want to purchase some new items such as Ex-Boomers, Ex-Potions and some Mind Gourds; although you these will make very little difference to battling stronger monsters, this late in the game, when you've obtained all the latest items; you should head South-West until you come across a mountain, called "Dragon Mountain" and this place is home to a scorching surprise.
