Q: Who are Capsule Monsters, what do they do and what do I feed them?

A: Capsule Monsters are like a fifth ally in battle, but you cannot control them...as someone has used some kind of "Berserk" spell on them! In total seven of these monsters exist, these are Jelze, Blaze, Zeppy, Sully, Flash; Gusto and Darbi. To make these monsters grow....you should feed them all kinds of items...check the Capsule Monsters Section for more details!

Q: How do I defeat Gades first time round?

A: This is always on the Gamefaqs messageboards, people give them a simple answer, like build you team up to Level 40 or so...however all you need to win this battle is a little speed...Jet Helm's are a good choice and the Speedy Ring! Check the strategy! Levels 22-24 would be fine!

Q: Does Tia ever rejoin the team?

A: Hell, it's a shame she does NOT re-join the team, she was quite a Magician after all. Luckily we have Selan to fill in the Gap, she is stronger, more defensive and much cuter, so she's the BEST character in one persons mind! Tia can be used in GIFT MODE, this is a "Gift" given to Lufia veterans who beat the Game twice in a row; this also gives Dekar and Lexis back to your team, so all characters used in the game can be used in the Ancient Cave.

Q: I've found 7 Dragon Eggs, so where is the Eighth one?

A: Well, that is a fair enough question, the 8th Dragon Egg can be found inside the Dungeon North of Dankirk, it's hidden in an area with three chests, each chest contains a treasure and one of those treasures is a Dragon Egg.....you had better check my walkthrough for more information!
