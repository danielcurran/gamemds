## 5.2. Forfeit Island

**MAIN GOALS** Get your mitts on the DRAGON SWORD! Find the Casino known as MARKAO! Gain access to the VIP Room!

Woahhh! Lets get our hands on a serious weapon, and lets make it a one that hurts dragons. Okay, this is one of my favourite places in the game, it's a place called Forfeit Island, and it's home of a casino called "Markao" where you can obtain the strongest sword in the game. Markao, is full of Minigames and most importantly people and prizes. You may want to know how to find the casino, so I will just say it can be found inside the Bar/Pub where you can buy some of those healing drinks. Upon entering this place, you'll notice a lot of girls, the guys are mostly people who run the slot machines, so if there's any ladies out there, then you can feel at home...'.'

The blonde haired chick near the entrance says she likes "STRONG" gambling men. Heh, she looks like one of those elf people, perhaps shes from the same clan as Artea. The games in this place should be explained in detail, but the sprites in this casino, can't give you too much of an explanation for each of the games, so here is where I come in! Please read on and enjoy these mini games, and make sure you come out of this place, STINKIN' RICH!

Hmmm....I did mention the VIP Card, you can get this from Prince Leon at his home office, in the town of Auralio! This Card is used to access the VIP Room in the back of Forfeit Island's Casino; and it means you can now play at the Five Card Stud table with the upperclass people; you certainly are a VIP when you access those rooms. Prince Leon will give this card to you only if you speak to him twice, it's well worth the bother, believe me!!!
