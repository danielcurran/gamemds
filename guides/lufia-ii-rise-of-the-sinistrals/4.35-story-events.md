## 4.35. Story Events

**MAIN GOALS** Acquire Dekar and Guy! (Hmmmph...Like you have a choice!) Make a journey to the Port Town known as ALEYN!

Okay, so you've beaten Idura, but sadly he is still roaming the place; you need to reach a port town called Aleyn; and that town can only be reached via the shrine, east of the Bound Kingdom, as well as being pretty near the tower where you destroyed Gades. Inside, you will find two familiar blokes, one has a Radical Haircut, and the other is a Blonde haired Lad. Congratulations, you get the cookie, it is indeed Guy and Dekar; and they don't want to be left alone, they want some action, so lets get them to join your party, and the choice is not yours, because your stuck with Guy till the end of the game. With the "BIG" boys in your team, you can do some enormous amounts of damage to your foes, this is cool, but that means you are stuck with only two magic users at this time in the game. You have to reach Aleyn, so proceed into the Warp Portal, and get going....!

On the eastern landmass, you should keep on heading east, enter the town when you reach it, and prepare for a little change in the music. The music is now really cool, plus it will be like that for every port town you enter, Aleyn in this case is the very first one, but others do exist. Now lets get on with a few errands around this port, and be sure to check out all main aspects of the town, you will find some good stuff here.
