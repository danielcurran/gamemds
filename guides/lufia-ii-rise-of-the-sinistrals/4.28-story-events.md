## 4.28. Story Events

**MAIN GOALS** Obtain another Dragon Egg! Make tracks for the Bound Kingdom!

Items -> Dragon Egg

When you enter Merix once again, you should know of a Girl named Ugene; who was on the look out for her father; which is the bloke who repaired the small bridge, that leads to the Bound Kingdom. You will find this girl in the South eastern house of the village, and she will give you a Dragon Egg, but only if you speak to her father. After taking the Girl's treasure, you should buy the excess spells, which you couldn't afford before. Then you should head off to the "Bound Kingdom"!

The Bound Kingdom is located North-East of Merix, and you will need to visit the Northern cave once again. Upon entering, you should head straight across the bridge, and up the unexplored staircase to the North. When you've been thrown onto the map screen once again; you should head for the castle to the North; that particular castle is no other than the "Bound Kingdom"!
