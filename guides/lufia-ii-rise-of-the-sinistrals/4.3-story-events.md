## 4.3. Story Events

**MAIN GOALS** Have a romantic meal with Tia! Find out about monsters! Raise Maxim's Level up to 3/4!

So that concludes the training, head back to Elcid and go see Tia, if needed; you could even kill off a few monsters to begin with; she is inside her shop as always, go see her, and watch her do her plain ol' housewife routine!

Tia has been waiting for you, she has been slaving in the kitchen; in order to cook you and her, a romantic meal. It won't be long before, both Maxim and Tia, decide to stuff their faces; soon Maxim and Tia will both agree on how bad, this Phantom Fish tastes. Maxim, will decide to mix a bit of Lemon, with the fish, it is now finally, edible. Tia will make some Tea, and Maxim will soon be on his way; but outside, a disturbance among the villagers, is taking place. It seems that the cave leading to Sundletan, is saturated by a hoard of monsters; which means that no-one can get there. Well, you can guess whats going to happen next; Maxim, is a monster hunter, this means that its his Job the clear out the cave. Which will make the journey to Sundletan, much easier for the villagers; so exit Elcid and proceed to the cave to the North.
