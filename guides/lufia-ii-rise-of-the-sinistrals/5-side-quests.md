Items -> Brill Helm, Light Jewel, Dragon Ring

¤ The Ancient Cave, is a "Gades" sized dungeon, famous for having 99 floors; in this cave, you will find various red and blue chests, as well as a whole load of enemies. If you decide to draw a map of this place; then your not in look; as this cave changes its layout, everytime you enter the damn place. You will also be relegated back to level one, upon entering this cave; which doesn't pay off if you don't find any decent equipment straight away. This could be the hardest dungeon in the game; you must find items to survive; but to start off with, you will be given ten potions - which are the ones that heal around 30 Health points. You should also know; that the ancient cave has some strict rules for you to follow, these are listed below......'.'

```
ººººººººººººººººººººººººººººº
  ± Rules of the Ancient Cave ±
  ººººººººººººººººººººººººººººº
```

» You are NOT allowed to take items into the cave; you will be given 10 potions to start you off; after the potions are in your fair hands, you are expected to survive on the items scattered around inside the cave!

» You ARE allowed to take the items retrieved from "BLUE CHESTS" out of the cave; red chest items will be sent to the 'Cleft of Dimension' never to be seen again!

» You can only escape this dungeon by using the "Providence" Item; found on the 20th floor. You will only receive one of this item, everytime you take a look around the cave; so I wouldn't advise feeding this to a capsule monster!

» You ARE allowed to Keep, all the Iris treasures found inside the cave, you will have no use for these; so why not take them to a proper museum; found in the town of Gruberik!

¤ Have you read the rules? Well, when you have, you might as well read the next few paragraphs; I have included a simple boss strategy for the Ancient Jelly; as well as a few helpful survival tips. Okay; first of all; potions are like Gold Dust; you may not be able to find a healing spell; such as "stronger" or Champion. Look out for any treasure chests, you should explore each of the floors completely; and hope for a Blue Chest occasionally. The Blue chests may contain items such as the "Sizzle Sword" or the "Ruse Armour", so you may find some useful gear; and you should also remember that Blue items can be taken in and out of the cave.

¤ To keep you levels high; you should attempt to defeat every single monster in your path; avoiding them will waste valuable experience points; and you may not be as prepared for the monsters on the upper floors of the cave. I personally would kill every monster in site; so I end up with a team the same level as the number of the current floor, so when you reach the final floor; you should be at level 99.

¤ You should enter the cave with a suitable team; e.g. Maxim, Guy, Selan and Dekar. As you can see, you have two magic users and two blokes with really high attacking stats; still, if you have Artea in your team; you will be even more prepared, as you do manage to get the "Fry" spell pretty quickly inside this cave. When you've obtained the "Providence" Item; you should avoid feeding your capsule monster, you wouldn't want to give him caviar on an empty stomach now' would you?

¤ Before you read the strategy, you should know that this place can take over six hours to complete, and ages if you fight against all the monsters. If you manage to reach the Final Floor, a.k.a. the 99th floor, then you are obliged to kill the Ancient Jelly, it's going to be a short battle. This boss can be hard, you'll need to defeat him within three rounds of attacking; you should be able to do this if you have the Myth Blade equipped, in this case have it equipped on Maxim...! LETS BEGIN!

```
-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-
  # Elite Boss: ANCIENT JELLY #  HP: 10,000      Weakness: Nothing!!!
  -=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-
```

¥ This is one HARD battle, and I suggest all characters have their IP on 100% or you will regret this. Guy should be equipped with the Gades Blade, and Maxim should have the Myth Blade; Selan or Artea should have something that increases attack power, if needed equip a Mini Shield for it's Battle Lust Ikari technique. As long as Guy and Maxim have the desired weapons, you can cope with this battle, now lets concentrate on winning. To begin, have Maxim use the "Battlefury" attack to cause over 2,000 damage, Guy should use the "Octostrike" from his Gades Blade, and Artea should use Battle Lust, or some other attack boosting spell or item on Guy. Selan should cast the Thunder spell, and that's the first turn over. On average you should have done over 6,000 damage, and that's a good start; on the next turn, have Guy and Maxim use normal attacks, and the two magic users Artea and Selan should use some level 3 magic spells, of which Thunder seems to be the most effective. This should be repeated on the last turn, and if your lucky, the Jelly will die; leaving you satisfied, and worthy of taking the Key to your next destination the "ANCIENT CAVE" where you will find a few treasures that can be of use to you, now lets use the Providence item to continue, also, I failed to mention the cool music on this floor, it's just like the music from the Prologue of Lufia: The Fortress of Doom; although this has a very different style, so I have to admit the tune's are pretty different, but who cares, I personally prefer the Lufia 2 version of this tune.....!

¤ Alas, the Ancient Jelly has been defeated, and we have used the Providence item; so it's now time to claim some treasures; behind the reception desk is a locked door, the key you just obtained will open that door, inside you can take three new treasures, there's a Brill Helm, a Dragon Ring and a Light Jewel, that's pretty cool and you may revisit the ancient cave again, if you need more treasures, if you are interested, here are some of the items which I found in Blue chests, this is not a complete list of every item, but it is giving you some idea about what's on offer in this place, and I found the Engage Ring too; so I can get the Dragon Blade without paying 500,000 coins; and that just about covers this Sidequest for the time being; good look with the rest of the game, plus the other Sidequests; and make sure you manage to kill the Egg Dragon too!!!

-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=
  # Elite Boss: EGG DRAGON #     HP: 65,500      Weakness: Nothing!!!
  -=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=
```

¥ Flames of doom, and the Phoenix Resurrection in one, this is one tough Dragon as he can use both Holy and Fire elemental attacks...he even has a fair share of devastating Physical attacks. Now let's begin by describing his attacks; first off, he has an attack called "Sunny-Side Up" and to be fair; and this attack can cause major damage to your whole party; in perspective, this has to be at least twice as powerful as the Zap spell! "Sunny-Side up" is a Flame attack...so if you have the Fire Gear I mentioned before, you will be able to absorb most of the damage from these attacks. The other attack that hit's all party members, is his "ZAP" spell, this is a Holy Spell as you know, and it is fairly effective against a unprepared team..in my case even this spell did over 200 damage to Guy...and to be honest most of my characters were knocked you quickly, apart from Maxim and Artea. After those attacks, the Egg Dragon could use a very strong physical attack called "Scrambled Egg" and this alone only hits one ally, but if their defense is somewhat poor, then you've got a problem! The last of his attacks just confuses people, and to be fair; I have to say this is the most annoying of all...as you may recall, Confusing can be a major problem, due to the fact that it makes your allies attack each other; and if someone like Guy hits Selan, she will be mortally wounded, or she may even fall...not a pretty site. Now! let's win this battle, make sure Maxim is the one who casts Valor; as he should have more HP than Artea and Selan...you should equip Artea with the Gorgon Rock as he will attack first, Guy should attack normally, and Selan should keep Maxim's MP's up and use spells like Trick, Courage and Shield! Follow that simple pattern, until the Egg Dragon is loosing below a thousand HP to your "Battle Cry" or "Ax Attack" IP techs and just allow Artea to attack normally, if needed, Guy could also chip in with the healing, by using some healing Ikari techniques. If the Battle Cry technique starts to do 1,000 or below, this will mean the Egg Dragon has less than 7,000 Hit points left, and if that's the case, then you have almost won this battle...and one last thing to end with! DO NOT! under any circumstances use Offensive Magic spells, these will do too little damage to the Egg Dragon and to be fair, offensive magic attacks are a load o' Trash in this battle!

¤ When the Egg Dragon dies, you will receive 60,000 experience points & 60,000 Gold Coins...and just so this battle has not been a complete waste of time; the Dragon also gives you the Egg Ring and the Egg Sword. The sword isn't up to much, but the Egg Ring rocks...it will boost all your stats to Max, which is 999 points for anything apart from the GUT Stat...which caps at 199; and this means you can equip the Egg Sword, without worrying about it's extremely low attack power! You can even use armours like the "Seethru Silk" so you can use some good healing IP Techniques! What a BONUS!
