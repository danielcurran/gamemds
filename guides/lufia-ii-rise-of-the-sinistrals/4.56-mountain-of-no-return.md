## 4.56. Mountain of no Return!

**MAIN GOALS** Catch up with the Elf Child and retrieve the plans! Run into a fellow swordsman from the Elven Village!

Items -> Aqua Sword

The Speedy Lil' Elf Girl ran up this mountain, so let's find her, to begin the search go up the second lot of steps and into a small chamber with two horse statues. Push them so they meet eye to eye and continue, in the next chamber things get a little more complicated, this time we have several of these statues to deal with! There's also a normal statue, so begin by moving the ordinary statue, three paces to the left and five paces upwards to block of two forces. Now move the left statue in the center of this room; one pace to the right, then another pace upwards...move the right statue four paces to the left and two paces upwards to permanently block of one of the forces. You are then obliged to move the ordinary statue, one pace upwards to block off two more forces, and you can apply the final part of this puzzle, by moving the last horse statue two paces upwards to block off the final force..! Then you may continue through the door...'.'

Now go up the staircase which follows, and continue on this one way path till you reach some monsters; use fire elemental attacks and spells to make simple work of them and head through the door which follows. Once again, follow the one way path until you reach another room full of horse statues; this puzzle is a little more complicated than the second room, so you had better read my solution carefully...If it's Readable...'.'

This took me a good half hour to work out, so here goes, begin with the horse a few paces south of the entrance, and head right until the forces stop your movement. Move the very first horse one pace downwards, and it's neighbor 7 paces to the right; to block the force given off one of the statues. Now move the horse to your left, four paces down, and the one opposite, one pace down; so both horse statues are facing each other; now open the chest to get a Aqua Sword, then equip the newly acquired weapon on Selan. Now walk back to the very first statue and move it one pace downwards, the path will be clear, so you can now head south and then east until you get blocked again; this time you need to move the first statue you see on this path upwards so it touches the statue above. You can then head right a few paces and move the next horse one pace to the right, and to finish off; head downwards and push the final horse as far to the right as possible and head through the door, where you will be on the mountainside, with the elf child you been looking for. Milka decides to run away as soon as you confront her, but she is caught by some Gargoyles, which means she's in trouble....Let's help her....

**Boss:** 4 × Gargoyle · **HP:** 1,000 each

Milka explains why she nicked the plans and Lexis promises a few things to her. Lexis says he will plant flowers in every open area of the town, and he will modify the machines to stop ooze leaking out; and when all the flowers grow, he will bring Milka a large bunch of flowers. Milka is taken in by this, and Lexis walks off so he can start his research, soon after this, two elves come, it seems that a ball of light has come to the Elven village of Eserikto; Artea will warp Maxim and co. to that place, only to find that an elf lady named Karyn has been kidnapped; we have a good idea who the Kidnapper is too; it can only be Amon, the Sinistral of Chaos; After a little bit of thought, Artea decides to join your party, when it comes to the status screen, this Elf is known as Arty. He starts out at level 38 with some of the best spells available; such as Firebird, Ice.Valk; Dragon, Thunder and Valor...'.'
