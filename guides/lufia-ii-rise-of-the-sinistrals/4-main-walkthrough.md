# 4. Main Walkthrough

After looking through your old junk in your attic; you should find various Snes games and a console to play them on. Out of all your games, select Lufia II and shove it inside your Super nintendo. Watch the short intro, regarding all main characters in the game; then start a new adventure. Inside this FAQ; I will use the default names for the characters, so I suggest you name the Hero, Maxim!

¤ Well, this is Elcid a peaceful humble village...home of Maxim and Tia, and a place to get acquainted with the game's basic moves! All you will find here is a mass of conversations and some nice spells within the magic store; I recommend the "Strong" Spell when you get enough cash. Attempt to leave town as of now and an old man will invite you to his training dungeon, accept the invitation, this means you have to exit the village! Once your on the MAP screen, enter the dungeon south of Elcid.

```
¤¤¤¤¤¤¤¤¤¤¤¤¤¤¤¤¤¤¤¤¤¤¤¤
± The Training Dungeon ±
°°°°°°°°°°°°°°°°°°°°°°°°
MAIN GOALS
¤ Complete all training tasks!
¤ Gain the Reset Spell!
```

¤ The first lesson is to watch enemy movements, it is known that monsters will only move when you do; because they will feel provoked. Practice sizing up a monster, by using the arrow buttons on your control pad; when your ready, go get that Jelly. Maxim, only has normal attacks, and no spells at this moment in time, but you should win this battle with ease.

« Remember -> When in a monster saturated room, don't allow the monsters to surround you; or you'll be sorry!

When the instructor has said his piece, follow him into the next room; ready for the second lesson.

¤ The second lesson involves firing arrows to stun enemies, to shoot an arrow, press the "Y" Button on your control pad. Enemies can't draw you into battle when you've stunned them, currently, Maxim only possesses the Skill Arrow. Shoot the arrow at the Jelly to stop its movement for a while, when you've stunned it, you might as well kill it off too.

« Remember -> Once an enemy is paralyzed by an arrow; you can bump into it, without it drawing you into battle.

Once you've dealt with the Jelly, you can freely progress into the next room; ready for your third lesson.

¤ The third lesson involves firing arrows once again, but this time, the arrows are used to trigger a switch. Inside this room, you will notice a switch near a broken bridge; which needs to be activated. After firing an arrow, into the switch; you will be able to proceed across the bridge, that you've repaired. After crossing the bridge, progress through the door and keep going till the instructor halts you once again.

¤ Lesson four involves moving large obstacles onto switches, which can in most cases, activate them. You can move these obstacles, using the "A" button and Arrow Buttons, on your Controller. You can see a statue, and a switch, which means you can practice, by activating it.

« Remember -> In certain situations, switches can only be activated by large obstacles; in other words, something heavier than yourself!

After activating the switch, you can now proceed through the door; but as you go through the door, the instructor will halt you. He wants to teach you some spell, which is called the "Reset" spell; this will allow you to set all the obstacles in a room, to their default locations, pretty cool, huh!

¤ That last few things that you need to know, are that you can jump off cliffs; that have arrows on the edge. Which you should do in this room, in order to make it over to the door; soon after that you will come to a room with more than one monster inside. First of all, you must destroy all the monsters; you should do this in a few seconds. Once the monsters are dead and buried; the locked door will open, you can now proceed to your next lesson.

¤ Lesson Five, involves chopping down bushes with your sword; you can sometimes find switches under bushes and tall grass. Inside those bushes, you will find a switch, which will open the door to the next room. To swing your sword, you must press the "B" button; when you've had enough practice, proceed through the door and be prepared for the next lesson.

¤ The sixth lesson, involves throwing obstacles; which can be done by pressing the "A" button. You may pick up items, such as pots and vases; then put them onto switches to activate them. To change your throwing direction, hold the "R" button, and use the arrow buttons to point in the right direction. This will become very useful through the game; so by all means remember what you learnt' in this lesson. Anyway, to complete this task, put the first pot on the right switch, to open a sliding wall. Then grab the other one, and place it on the left switch; the door to the left side of the room will open, and you may safely exit the training dungeon!

¤ So that concludes the training, head back to Elcid and go see Tia, if needed; you could even kill off a few monsters to begin with; she is inside her shop as always, go see her, and watch her do her plain ol' housewife routine!

¤ Tia has been waiting for you, she has been slaving in the kitchen; in order to cook you and her, a romantic meal. It won't be long before, both Maxim and Tia, decide to stuff their faces; soon Maxim and Tia will both agree on how bad, this Phantom Fish tastes. Maxim, will decide to mix a bit of Lemon, with the fish, it is now finally, edible. Tia will make some Tea, and Maxim will soon be on his way; but outside, a disturbance among the villagers, is taking place. It seems that the cave leading to Sundletan, is saturated by a hoard of monsters; which means that no-one can get there. Well, you can guess whats going to happen next; Maxim, is a monster hunter, this means that its his Job the clear out the cave. Which will make the journey to Sundletan, much easier for the villagers; so exit Elcid and proceed to the cave to the North.

ITEMS -> Life Potion, Escape, Antidote, Power Potion, Hide Armour, Magic Jar

¤ Upon entering the cave, you will see a man; speak to him, and he will explain that a woman went into the cave alone. After hearing that news, you should go inside the first room; where you will find a bridge.

¤ First of all, proceed across the bridge, and head along the path to the right of the door facing you. You will eventually come to another door which can be entered, to obtain a few items. Inside you will find two doors; you can find an Escape item, in the Right door, and you will notice that the left door is locked. The left door, contains a Life potion; you can open it by placing the pots onto the two switches. When you've taken both treasures; backtrack into the previous room, and press the switch; which can be found under a bush, the only one in this small room to be exact. After pressing the switch; you will have gained access to the door below, which will take you into a room; thats saturated with bushes, with only one monster to dispatch.

¤ This room like I said before, is covered in bushes; which need to be trimmed, with no other than your trusty sword. When all the bushes are trimmed to size you should hug the left wall, to reveal a chest containing an antidote. After getting the treasure, proceed through the door to the right; you will be in a room with more pots and a mushroom. First of all, clear the bushes to reveal two switches; place one of the pots on the Left switch, and head into the new door, you will be in a room with four monsters and some stone blocks.

¤ If your HP is low, now is a good time to use a potion; you should shift the blocks around till, you manage to reach the staircase in the middle, but you will still need to kill all four monsters; which shouldn't be too hard. The next room, contains some bats; finish of the first bat and take the valuable Power potion from the chest. From your current position, head right till you get to a switch; which can be activated by a good whack from your sword. Now that you've activated the switch, you need to backtrack to the room with the unvisited door; so it's up the staircase and outside the room with all those blocks that you moved.

¤ After coming back to this room, you must now activate the switch to the far right of the room; which can be found inside the three bushes. When you stick a pot onto the switch, you will have triggered the opening of the Right door; which till a few seconds ago was locked. To proceed further into the cave you must head into the right door; and prepare to fight more monsters. The room has two doors inside, the one you want is the left door, so head through it and down the staircase that follows.

¤ Upon entering this new room; you must head across the short bridge, which in this case, leads you to another staircase. To the left of the staircase, you may hug the wall and follow the path till you finally reach, what looks like another staircase. Down that particular staircase, you will find a Magic Jar and a Hide Armour, which should be equipped on Maxim. After taking some nice treasures; head back up the staircase, and backtrack to the other staircase.

¤ When you finally head down the staircase, you will some healing pads; you may also save your game. After freshening up, continue along the path and proceed up the stairs to you right; you will be in a room, with a Lizard Man; who has decided to nick the key. Walk over to him, and be prepared for a boss battle.

**Boss:** Lizard Man · **HP:** 80

¤¤¤¤¤¤¤¤¤¤¤¤¤¤¤¤

¥ Lizard Man, do you need a strategy for this lame creature, tis' simple, you are around levels 3-5 and you have a Small Knife or Rapier equipped, you need to kill him, so use normal physical attacks and use a Potion when Maxim's HPs drop below fifteen. Keep repeating this, watch him drop dead and take 100GP and 125 experience points...this is fairly O.K for such a Simple Boss.

¤ One Dead Lizard Man equals a nice new key, you can go to Sundletan now..after your cozy chat with the lovely Iris...some Oracle Chick who is able to see the Future. Watch this scene, Maxim will learn that he is some kind of Hero who is going to save the world, could this have something to do with this Red Light that appeared...or is this some strange girl wanting a little adventure in her sad life...looks like it's up to you...!

¤ Sundletan, is the next stop for our Hero, so lets go use the escape item. In an instance you will be outside the cave; now would be a good time to get the "Strong" spell! However, this is can be purchased in Sundletan..to reach your next destination, re-enter the cave, enter the first door, walk across the bridge and enter the door above you; inside, avoid the Mushroom, use your Key and head for Sundletan!

¤ The General store, sells some good equipment, you should purchase a Dagger & some boomerangs. The Magic shops sells a couple of spells that can be used by Maxim; for some reason, Maxim cannot use any Fire elemental spells; which is not too cool. Still, you should buy, the Strong and Droplet spells; so Maxim now has some useable magic.

¤ Last of all, you should gather some information from the people around town; and if you need to, you can go to the inn; if your skint; you can still rest for free, which is strange, infact the inn keeper doesn't even say anything, about your poverty; it's a good deal I must say. When you leave the village, you may want to gain a few levels; Maxim would be able to cope, if he was at about, level 5. This shouldn't take too long, considering the experience pts available around the area. **Boss:** Catfish · **HP:** 200

¤¤¤¤¤¤¤¤¤¤¤¤¤

¥ First of all, the Catfish attacks with two spells; mainly "Flash" and "Mega Quake", of which Flash really hurts. When your HP gets below 50; you should always heal, that nasty Flash spell can do 40+ points of damage. If you are going to win this battle, don't bother with the "Droplet" spell; it seems the Catfish has good defense against that type of Magic. First of all, you should keep attacking with Physical attacks; when he uses that horrid "Flash" spell, you should cast "Strong"; which should heal around 30-40 HP. After around 8 normal hits, the Catfish will be "Cat food". When the Catfish is defeated you will receive; 500 experience and 500 Gold, which isn't too bad.

¤ The Catfish has decided to stay underwater for a while; so I guess Sundletan won't have any more Earthquake troubles. All you need to do in this area, is complete a few story events; but first of all, find your way out of this cave and head back to the village of Sundletan!

After preparations have been made; speak to the blue haired dude, near the village entrance; so you can find out that a pretty young girl went looking for you at Lake cave, and you should know by now, that Lake Cave is home to a hoard of Monsters.

¤ First of all, you need to find this young girl; she can be found in the Lake Cave, in the room where you solved the archery puzzle; when you had to fire arrows at some monsters behind spikes. When you arrive in that room; you can see some monsters attacking a girl; who just happens to be Tia, and Maxim's would be "Girlfriend". Still you must perform your heroic duty, and kill the monsters, which in this case are a Goblin and a Skeleton. After you demolish the monsters; Tia will explain why she came looking for you.

¤ It seems that Tia thought you were going away from her; and by the looks of it, Tia is really upset about this. Maxim explains about the Red Light, and how he's known swordsman ship ever since he was born. Tia at the end decides to come with you, but this reckless wench runs straight into another battle. When Tia joins your party, she will be at Level 7; which should be about two levels below Maxim. She can use the "Spark" spell, and she also comes with the "Drowsy" and "Strong" spells too.

¤ After Tia has joined, head back to Sundletan and purchase the "Droplet" and "Poison" spells for Tia. If needed you can buy the "Drowsy" spell for Maxim; but at the moment 1000G is a large chunk of your money. Why you've got the chance, you should buy Tia a new weapon; the Whip is the best choice, as its got the "Flame Blast" Ikari technique, and Tia will take a lot of damage in battle, because of her weak defense.

¤ The next part of your quest, requires a journey to the Alunze Kingdom; still you might as well gain a few levels, before making tracks. When you do gain a few levels; head in the North-west direction, and you will come to a small shrine; which will lead you over the mountains. To reach Alunze, head across the patches of desert area; till you finally come to a small bridge, you can then head east a little, which will take you to the Alunze Kingdom.

When you've finished wasting your money; you should proceed to Alunze Castle.

¤ Before you head for Alunze Castle, you may want to gain a few level; Maxim should be around Lv12 and Tia will be fine at Lv10/11. ¤ The crown seems to be a fraud, so one of the soldiers attempts to take it for examination; but he is soon halted in his tracks. The guys with the crown are thieves, they will put on a little show; and it won't take long for them to steal the crown. After the thieves have left, you can talk to the King; he is not from the same planet as normal people; so he doesn't have a clue what you need to do, when your crown gets nicked.

¤ The clowns have disappeared into the basement, so look for a balcony with two girls walking around; and head though the Right door, until you reach a Guard who appears to be Ko'ed. Keep going and head into the basement; you will find a useful tool here, which can help you to blow things up!

ITEMS -> Bomb, Headband, Coat, Light Knife, Secret Fruit, Miracle, Dragon Egg, Crown

¤ The Basement is riddled with all sorts of monsters; but sadly, you don't get to fight a boss here. Inside the first room, you will find a door and a weak wall; which can be destroyed by a bomb, a short while later. Go through the door and place the pot onto the switch; this will cause another door to open leaving you on a one way path, once again. The next room contains two blocks and two tiles; first of all move one of the blocks onto the first tile; this will cause the gap between the walls below to widen. The other block can now be moved through the gap, and onto the southern tile; which again will cause another door to unlock itself!

¤ Head through the door, and follow the one way path, till you come to a Blue Chest, containing the bomb item; which can be used to blow things up. After obtaining the Bomb; head back to the weak wall you found before, and blow it to smithereens; you can now proceed further into the hideout of the weirdos.

« Remember -> Bombs are a wonderful tool to have; but they do explode after you take Six steps; or if you swing your sword six times. You can plant bombs by pressing the same button as you would use for arrows; in this case the button you need to press is "Y".

¤ Upon entering the room, head right and down, till you see a vase; pick up the Vase and take it along the path to the left. Activate the switch, and go back to the entrance of this room; this time, head right, till you finally crash into a wall. First of all, ignore the chest above you; and head South to find another chest, containing a Headband. Backtrack upwards a few steps; and head left at the first turn, then South to reach a staircase and a monster. Head downstairs, into another room, which has a weak wall and a door. If you for some reason need some major first aid, head through the door; after healing your HP's and MP's, you should blow up the weak wall.

¤ The room following on, contains some monsters; you can either avoid them, or you can destroy them. After making your choice, go through the next door, and you will come to another puzzle, involving Vases and Switches. First of all; put the vase onto the upper switch, to open the Northern door. Enter the door and take the Escape item from the chest; head back out, then put the vase on the lower switch; to open the Southern door.
