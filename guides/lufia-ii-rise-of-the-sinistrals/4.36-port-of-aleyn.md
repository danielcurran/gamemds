## 4.36. Port of Aleyn

**MAIN GOALS** Purchase some stronger magic spells! (Lv2 spells!) Learn of a bloke named Jyad! (The Ship Builder!)

Yes! Your here, your inside the port town of Aleyn, and the music has indeed changed, just like I said. While your in this place you should check out the magic store, you can buy some cool level two magic spells, and another handy spell called "Rally" which restores your fight power, for those of you who don't know what fight power is, just think of "Rally" as a revival spell.

Also inside this town, you can get some new weapons from the General store; in the west area of this town. I suggest you get Selan a Long Staff, and Guy a Hand Ax, you should stick with your current armour, unless you want a small upgrade, which is not essential. Okay, when you've finished shopping, speak to the town's people, you will learn of a man named Jyad, and he seems to be at the North Mountain, it seems that someone has destroyed all the ships, and that's not good for a port town, Jyad is a ship builder, if you get hold of this Guy, he will be helpful to you, as you will get the chance to visit some cool places soon. So head out of town, and North till you reach some forest area, keep moving around that area, till you reach the mountain, and be ready for a little challenge, you are going to climb it....!
