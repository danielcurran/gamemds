### 8.1.23. Petrify

Characters with Petrify set are considered 'dead', and four statues gets you a Game Over. Petrified characters keep their HP score, but can no longer act, nor can they Cover for other allies. You cannot target Petrified characters with conventional attacks; only those that are specifically designed to be able to hit characters with Petrify can be directed at them. Monsters with Petrify set are just killed, and cannot be healed. Petrify lingers after battle.
