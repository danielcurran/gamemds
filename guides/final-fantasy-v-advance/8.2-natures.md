## 8.2. Natures

Natures are creature types which are used to determine a weapon's or spell's possible effect on the target. Natures are typically fixed; they can never be removed and can never be set with the exception of the Dragon Kiss attack, a Mix coming from a Dragon Fang and a Maiden's Kiss, which sets both the Heavy and Dragon nature on whatever target it's used on.
