## 7.22. !Combine COMBINE-LINK

All !Combine effects are multi-target and unblockable. Two items are needed to produce a !Combine attack: one kind of ammo and one kind of non-ammo. The only effects that truly differ next to power are those coming from the Gold Needle and the Dark Matter.

Those attacks that inflict a status ailment alongside damage have a static 75 % chance that the status will be inflicted
