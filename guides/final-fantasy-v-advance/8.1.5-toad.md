### 8.1.5. Toad

Toad nullifies Defense, Magic Defense and Evade %. Battle Power is set to 3 (also for Throw and Goblin Punch purposes). All secondary abilities are blacked out except for !Black. Only the Toad spell may be used from the !Black menu. Monsters under the effects of Toad may also use Toad spell. Toad also affects the sprite of the affected out of battle. Toad lingers after battle.
