## 7.10. !Release

The stats of the monster caught are used when determining damage, not that of the !Release user. Elemental boosts from his or her equipment IS factored in. You can never learn Blue magic from spells used by Released monsters.

**Note that in the Effect:** description, I don't give a description when the

attack performed is a spell available through other means, as those attacks are explained elsewhere.

(Strong) Attack is an unnamed move that you will only very rarely find in an opponent's AI Script, and even then always as a counter to something; the game uses it as a move that kills a character regardless of your level and defense. The used damage formula is:

(Monster Attack * 8 + (0..(Monster Attack/8)) - target's Defense) * Monster Attack Multiplier

This is similair to a monster's normal physical attack damage formula, only Monster Attack is multiplied by 8 in the (Strong) Attack formula. Versus a target with no Defense, (Strong) Attack functions like a 800% damage physical attack.
