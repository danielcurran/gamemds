### 8.1.8. Paralyze

Paralyzed creatures cannot act, nor will they Cover for other allies. The duration of Paralyze depends on the attack setting it. A Paralyzed target cannot avoid physical attacks, not even with the help of Elven Cloaks, Main Gauches, or Aegis Shields. Paralyze wears off automatically.
