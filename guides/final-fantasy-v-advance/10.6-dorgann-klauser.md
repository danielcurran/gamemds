## 10.6. Dorgann Klauser

Dorgann Klauser was one of the Warriors of Dawn, the legendary group of four warriors that managed to defeat Exdeath. While the Warriors of Dawn were forced to seal the creature away, Dorgann was the only one who decided to stay on the alien world; he gave no reason to his comrades, but found himself married with a son not long after. Dorgann Klauser lost his wife over ten years before his own death, alongside his son Bartz when they were traveling the world. His dying wish was that his son would see more of the world, a wish his son honored to his day.

In the Dawn of Souls remake of the original Final Fantasy, 'Dorgan' was a default name for the Monk character.
