## 7.2. !Animals ANIMALS-LINK

The game picks a random number between the character's level and 0, and produces an attack accordingly:

0 = Mysidian Rabbit (does nothing) 1- 4 = Squirrel (ST non-elemental attack, misses against Floating targets) 5- 9 = Bee Swarm (Mt non-elemental attack, random weak damage) 10-19 = Nightingale (MT curative attack, removes Poison and Dark) 20-29 = Flying Squirrel (MT attack that sets Paralyze on all opponents) 30-39 = Falcon (Deals 75 % current HP damage, misses vs. Heavy targets) 40-49 = Skunk (MT attack that sets Poison and Dark on all opponents) 50-59 = Wild Boar (ST non-elemental attack, misses against Floating targets) 60-99 = Unicorn (fully heales all character's HP)

iOS/Android: Bee Swarm sets Poison in this version.
