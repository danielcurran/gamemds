### 8.2.3. Magic Beast

The Magic Beast nature is just there to check for a certain type of creature for the purposes of certain attacks.

- The Beastmaster's !Calm command was supposed to set Stop only on creatures with a Magic Beast nature; instead, it can set Stop on all targets except for Magic Beasts. - The Artemis Bow and the Beast Killer Whip deal twice as much damage to creatures with a Magic Beast nature.
