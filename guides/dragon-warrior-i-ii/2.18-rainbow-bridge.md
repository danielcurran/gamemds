## 2.18. Rainbow Bridge

| Magic Key |  |

| Token of Roto |  |

Go into Radatome and withdraw your Stones of Sunlight and the Rain Cloud Staff. Go into Radatome Castle and SAVE YOUR GAME. Travel to the Holy Monolith south of Rimuldar.

**Holy Monolith**

Go down the stairs. Speak to the Old Man. He'll recognize us as the descendant of Roto now and says it's time for sun and rain to meet. Hero will hand over the Stones of Sunlight and Rain Cloud Staff to him. The Old Man will offer it to altar and a necklace will descend from a light. The Old Man will tell us to take the Rainbow Drop. Do so by examining the Necklace. RAINBOW DROP acquired! This isn't the place to use it. Exit the Monolith.

**Overworld**

Travel back to Rimuldar.
