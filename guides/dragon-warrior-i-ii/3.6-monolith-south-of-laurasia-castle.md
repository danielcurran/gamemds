## 3.6. Monolith South of Laurasia Castle

<!-- MIXED -->

There's an Old Man near a campfire here as well as a locked Gold Door. Speak to the Old Man to find out he has been waiting for us. He'll tell us that there are 2 kinds of Keys. The Golden Key and the Silver Key. He wants us to find the Silver Key first. He'll tell us we can find it in a place called Lake Cave which is west of Sumaltria. That's all we can do here for now. Exit this place.
