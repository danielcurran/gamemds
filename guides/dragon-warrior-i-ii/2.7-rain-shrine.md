## 2.7. Rain Shrine

<!-- MIXED -->

Go down the stairs Speak to the Old Man to find out he knows we're on a journey to defeat the Evil King Dragon. He wants to test our strength. He must be one of the Wise Men Descendant. Speak to him again. He'll tell us about an item called Silver Harp. It belongs to Garai! If we bring back the Silver Harp and show it to him, he'll give us the Staff of Rain. Exit this place.
