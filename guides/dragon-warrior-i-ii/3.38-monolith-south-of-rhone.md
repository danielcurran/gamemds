## 3.38. Monolith South of Rhone

<!-- MIXED -->

Cast Stepguard then go up the stairs. Speak to the Old Man to find out the path to enter Rhone is found west of this Monolith. Go back down the stairs. Go through the hidden opening south of the Traveler's Gate. Speak to the Demon to find out he won't harm us. The monster will tell us where the Water Crest can be found. We've already found it so his information is useless. Let's kill him...we can't do that unfortunately. Exit this area. (Step on the poisonous swamp).
