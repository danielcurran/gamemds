## 3.32. Monolith North of Beranule

There are 3 Traveler's Gate in this Monolith. Speak to the Old Man to find out that the Temple of Hargon will appear peaceful at our arrival. But that's an illusion. We need to seek the help of the spirit. Speak to the man to find out we can reach various locations with the Traveler's Gates here but we need the Gold Key. We have the Gold Key so we have nothing to fear. The Left Gate takes you to the Monolith West of Sumaltria Castle. The Middle Gate takes you to the Fire Monolith. The Right Gate takes you to the Monolith West of Moonbrook Castle. Step on the Middle Gate.
