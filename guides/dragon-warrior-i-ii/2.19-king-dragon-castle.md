B1F

Cast Radiant. Unlock the Door then go south then west to the end. Go south then go east to find a fork - northeast/southeast. Go northeast. Continue on east to find a staircase going down. Go down it.

B2F

Cast Radiant. Go west then north to reach a fork. Go north. Go east then north to reach another fork. Go east then south. Go south to the end and go down the stairs west.

B3F

Go west then north and open the 2 chest to find a MEDICAL HERB and 500-503 GOLD. Return to the entrance of this floor and go east then north to find a staircase going down. Ignore it. Go west to find another staircase going down. Go down it.

B4F
