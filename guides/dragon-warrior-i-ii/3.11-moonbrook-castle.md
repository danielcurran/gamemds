## 3.11. Moonbrook Castle

| Monsters Roaming Around: | | - Armor Centipede (EXP - 14) (GOLD - 30) | | - King Cobra (EXP - 9) (GOLD - 9) | | - Smoke (EXP - 18) (GOLD - 40) | | - Living Dead (EXP - 40) (GOLD - 25) | | - Metal Slime (EXP - 1015) (GOLD - 90) | |______________________________________________________________| The Castle is small. Walk around to know the layout. The Castle is in ruins and 3 Flame Spirits can be found wandering around. Speak to the one in the southern room to find out he's a confused soul crying out for help. :( Speak to the one in the northwest corner room to find out in a land east of here, we can find a place with 4 bridges, and a small swamp. The Mirror of Ra is there. The 2 treasure chests here are locked. Speak to the Spirit near the Throne. He's the spirit of the King of Moonbrook. He'll let us know that his daughter, Linda, was inflicted by a curse, and turned into a dog. He wants to see her again. That dog at Moonpeta must be Linda. That's why it was following us around. Go down the stairs in the courtyard or what's left of the courtyard. To reach the courtyard, you need to go through the opening at the northwest corner of the Castle so you'll be at the outer area of the Castle. From there, go south to make it to southwest corner of the Castle. Follow the path into the Courtyard. Down here, speak to the fallen Soldier to find out we can undo the curse inflicted on Linda if we use the Mirror of Ra. The Solider will pass away leaving his spirit behind reminding you about the Mirror of Ra. Get Hero to Level 13 Get Rolando to Level 11 At Level 10, Rolando will learn RETURN. The Return spell in this game will take you to last place you saved your game. The last time you saved your game was at Moonpeta right? Exit the Castle. Cast RETURN to be teleported to Moonpeta instantly.
