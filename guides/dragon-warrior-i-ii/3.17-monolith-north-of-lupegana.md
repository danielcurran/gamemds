## 3.17. Monolith North of Lupegana

<!-- MIXED -->

Go down the stairs. There's a Gold Door here, a Traveler's Gate and an Old Man. We can't reach the Traveler's Gate because of the Gold Door. Speak to the Old Man to find out there's a man on an island somewhere that has the Golden Key. This NPCs in this game should really learn what Direction means. Exit this Monolith. Cast RETURN so you can get back to Lupegana. Get aboard your Ship in the Sea.
