Get aboard your Ship nearby. From the continent Zahan is on, travel 15 paces north then travel all the way WEST until you reach an island surrounded by Pointy Mountains. It's a Desert Area with a Single Tree in the middle. Search that Tree to find the LEAF OF THE WORLD TREE. This Island is the World Tree Island. Remember it. We can Revive a fallen party member with the Leaf of the World Tree. You can only hold 1 at a time. If you used up your 1. Return her to grab another one. For now, DO NOT USE THE ONE YOU JUST GOT. SAVE IT UNTIL I TELL YOU WHAT TO USE IT FOR.
