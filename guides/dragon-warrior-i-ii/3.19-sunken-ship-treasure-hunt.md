Our next mission is for obviously find the Sunken Ship Treasure. Get aboard your Ship. You left it near the bridge east of Lupegana. Remember? Sail back to Lupegana and go into it.

**Lupegana**

In Lupegana, go to the Town's Port, speak to the Old Man so he'll let you get on your Ship. Sail through the north opening then sail east to get to the Overworld.

**Overworld**

1 pace = 1 click on the D-Pad From the exact place your Ship is (southeast of Lupegana), sail 2 pace east, 8 pace north and then 2 pace west. From there, sail DIRECTLY north until you see a sparkling object on the sea. Look carefully. It's a sparkling blue object so it blends with the sea color but you should be able to see it. Sail on top of it and search that spot to find the Sunken SHIP'S TREASURE. Sail back to Lupegana.

**Lupegana**

Make your way to the Merchant that told you to bring him the Ship's Treasure. Speak to him. He's the Merchant that owned the Ship that sank. Now that he has the Ship's Treasure, he won't go bankrupt. As a reward, he'll give us his family heirloom, the ECHOING FLUTE. Our next mission is to find the Golden Key.
