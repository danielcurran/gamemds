This Village is in Ruins. This must be the Village that was destroyed by the demons. From the entrance of the Village, go south to the end then go east to get to the southeast corner of the village. There's a Shop Counter here. Stand north of the Shop Counter and go 4 paces north into the poisonous swamp to encounter a Boss.

From where you are, go directly east to the tree and search the ground to find the ARMOR OF ROTO!! This is the strongest Armor in the game. Make sure you equip it. Get to Level 16 then leave this Ruined Town. There's nothing we can do for it.
