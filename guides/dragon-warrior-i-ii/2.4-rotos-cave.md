## 2.4. Roto's Cave

<!-- MIXED -->

B1F

Woah! It's dark!!! Use the Torch you got from the Chest at Radatome Castle to lit up the Cave. That's much better.... His floor is a bit huge but there's nothing to be afraid of as there are no monster spawns. On this floor, make your way to the southeast corner to find a staircase going down. Go down it.

B2F

On this floor, just follow the rather linear passage until you reach a Tombstone. Examine the Tombstone. Something is written on it. This grave belongs to Roto! Three items are needed to cross to the Island of Evil which can be seen from Radatome Castle. Roto collected those items, crossed to the Island of Evil and defeated the evil King. He then entrusted those items to three wise men in the Kingdom. Their descendants are protecting those items and are awaiting our arrival. When an evil demon rises once again, we need to gather those items and use them to defeat the Evil demon. That's all. Backtrack and exit the Cave.
