## 2.13. The Princess' Love

<!-- MIXED -->

| Item Checklist: Princess' Love |

**Overworld**

Get to Level 13. At Level 13, Hero will learn RETURN. Go back to Rimuldar and Max out your Magic Keys. Cast RETURN to get teleported back to Radatome Castle.

**Radatome Castle**

Speak to everyone in the Castle to get different reactions now that the Princess has been rescued. Go to the Throne Room. Speak to the King. He'll thank us for rescuing his daughter. Laura will give us an item. The PRINCESS' LOVE. With this item, Princess Laura will tell us how many Experience Points is needed for us to Level as well as directions to get to Radatome Castle. Princess Laura loves Hero. She'll ask Hero if he fantasize about her. Answer with a YES to get a kiss. After she returns to her throne, you'll gain control. Speak to her whenever you want free kisses. Speak to the King and SAVE YOUR GAME.
