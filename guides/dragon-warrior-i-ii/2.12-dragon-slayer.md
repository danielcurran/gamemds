## 2.12. Dragon Slayer

<!-- MIXED -->

Go into Swamp Cave north of Rimuldar.

**Swamp Cave**

| Monsters Roaming Around: |  |
| --- | --- |
| - Ghost (EXP - 4) (GOLD - 8) |  |
| - Mage (EXP - 8) (GOLD - 16) |  |
| - Giant Scorpion (EXP - 16) (GOLD - 25) |  |
| - Meda (EXP - 14) (GOLD - 21) |  |

Go east, north, west then go north. Go north for a while then go east the south to find a marking on the floor. From the marking, go east then south to find a Door. Unlock it with your Magic Key. Go east to find a Dragon guarding a Princess. Heal up then speak to the Dragon.

| Sleep and Stopspell doesn't work on the Dragon. Cast Firebal |  |
| and hack the Dragon with your Steel Sword. If your HP is low, |  |
| just cast Heal. |  |

Speak to the Princess to find out her name is Laura. She's the Princess of Radatome. We did it! If we hadn't rescue her, she would have been the bride of the Evil King Dragon. Answer her question with a YES. We will escort her back to the Castle. Hero will carry Laura in his hands and then Laura will give him a kiss. Cast Outside.
