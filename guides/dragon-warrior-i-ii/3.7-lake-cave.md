B1F

From the entrance of the Cave, go into the room south and open the chest to find an AGILITY SEED. Use all Stat Growth Items on Hero. Leave the room then go west to the end then go south to find a body of water. Go through the dark opening southeast of the body of water. Go south and open the chest to find a LOTTERY TICKET. Leave the hidden room. From the body of water, go through the dark opening west. Go through the dark opening south. Go south to find a staircase going down. Go down it.

B2F

Go south to find 2 dark openings. One west and one east. Go through the east one and open the chest to find a NUT OF LIFE. The West dark opening leads to a dead end. Continue on south and open the chest west to find an ANTIDOTE HERB. Continue on south until you reach a fork. Go west and go through the dark opening. Open the 2 chest west to find a WING OF THE CHIMERA and 238-245 GOLD. Return to the fork east. Go east this time then south through the dark opening. Go through the dark opening south. Examine the Key on the floor to find the SILVER KEY! The Old Man didn't lie! We have our first Key!!! Get Hero to Level 10 Get Rolando to Level 8 At Level 8, Rolando will learn STOPSPELL. Backtrack and exit the Cave.
