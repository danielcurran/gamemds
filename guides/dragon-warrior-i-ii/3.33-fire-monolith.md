## 3.33. Fire Monolith

<!-- MIXED -->

| Item Checklist: Sun Crest |

Use the Echoing Flute to hear a melody after you've heard your melody. The Crest is definitely here but where? There are 3 Traveler's Gates here. Ignore them for now. Search the spot right of the Northeast Tree to find the SUN CREST. As for the gates: The Left Gate takes you to the Monolith North of Lupegana. The Middle Gate takes you back to Monolith North of Beranule The Right Gate takes you to the Holy Monolith. Exit the Fire Monolith.

**Monolith North of Lupegana**

Unlock the Gold Door and speak to the Old Man to fulfill his dream of traveling through a Traveler's Gate. :) Return to the Fire Monolith by stepping on the Traveler's Gate.

**Fire Monolith**

Step on the Middle Gate.

**Monolith North of Beranule**

Step on the Left Gate.

**Monolith West of Sumaltria Castle**

We're near Sumaltria Castle now. Exit to the Overworld.

**Overworld**

Travel to the Monolith South of Laurasia Castle.

**Monolith South of Laurasia Castle**

Unlock the Gold Door here and step on the Traveler's Gate.
