## 3.35. OPTIONAL: Hermit's Hideaway

|  | ITEMS / ELEMENTS | ENEMIES |
| --- | --- | --- |
| • Resistance Ring     • Energizer Suit | There ain't any! |  |

In this world, the hideaway isn't just a pile of smoldering organic matter. Search inside for a [RESISTANCE RING] and an [ENERGIZER SUIT]. Razzly will also be in here if you rescued her on the 'save Kid' path way back when, but she can't rejoin yet. That ring reduces the time status effects last, while the other automatically recovers HP to full at the end of battle.
