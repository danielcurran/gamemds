You can still visit Viper Manor, which has been mostly abandoned since the

break-in. In the basement jail area, search a bucket for the [PARLOR KEY].

This allows you to open the locked door on the ground floor to get a [DAEMON

**CHARM] accessory.**

| INVITATION: Luccia | INNATE: Black |  |

Remember to loot that rooftop terrace for a [SIGHT SCOPE] accessory, too! And just for the heck of it, you can find a secret entrance in the library tower. Search behind the desk to find a downward ladder. There's a BeachBum that hands over the [PORRE'S FURNACE FRAME], and can change party members' names after that. A throwback to the Nus of yore? Hmm. There is also a single [SILVER EARRING] on the desk in Luccia's Lab.

**REMINDER:** All areas on El Nido can be explored now, so if you haven't been to

the bluffs/Shadow Forest yet, do so now.
