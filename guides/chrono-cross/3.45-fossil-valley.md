You're forced to go through here again, although not much has changed 'sides the enemies. Luckily, there is one more thing you can do here. If you managed to go out of your way and get Draggy before, his Lv. 7 tech is obtained in this area. With him in the party, go up the ladder to where the exorcisms had to be performed, in the dragon boneyard. After some scenes with his mother, he'll receive the 'BigBreath' tech. It attacks all enemies with the spirit of his mommy! ...Of course, if you don't even have the Big Egg Draggy hatches from, you can get it here as well (I think).

Other than that, ditch this dump and make for Termina.
