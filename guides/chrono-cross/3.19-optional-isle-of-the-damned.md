It's enemy-less for the most part. Check the evil-looking hole in the NW corner to find [MIXED BONES] for Skelly. The lower-right cave has a simple [@EYEBALL] component inside. The only other screen has some Airframes in it, which are incredibly powerful for this point in time. If you can fight 'em, it may be well worth it...they can do 140+ with PowerDive, though. Fight at your own risk. (There are no other treasures around here for now). ____________________ ________________
