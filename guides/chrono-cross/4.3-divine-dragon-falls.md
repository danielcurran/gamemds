## 4.3. Divine Dragon Falls

<!-- MIXED -->

|  | ITEMS / ELEMENTS | ENEMIES |
| --- | --- | --- |
| • ChronoCross | There ain't any! |  |

With the Tear of Love and Tear of Hate in hand, go to this area (a waterfall near Arni Village) and enter the main cavern. If you haven't been here in a while, you'll probably have to have Steena with so she can appraise what the pedestals are for. After that, put the Tear of Love and Hate in their holders to create the legendary lost element [CHRONOCROSS]! This item can be used in battle to recharge all used elements _besides_ the one who uses it.
