## 5.11. Key Items

Key items, or items that can't be disassembled or thrown away, have storyline significance or have specific functions. You can check your current ones by pressing the square button on the field or going under the 'Items' tab in the main menu. These are all the ones I've found, so if I forget any or mess up the descriptions, tell me and I'll fix 'em. Thx!
