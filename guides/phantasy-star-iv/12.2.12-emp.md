### 12.2.12. EMP

EMP abilities use an electro-magnetic pulse (EMP) to disrupt or entirely stop any electrical signals within the target, and are therefore only useful for temporarily stopping or utterly destroying mechanical beings.

Techniques: -

Skills: SPARK, HIJAMMER

Combos: CircuitBrk

Weapons: -

Effective Against: All Machines

Ineffective Against: Biomonsters, Darkspawn, Evil Beings
