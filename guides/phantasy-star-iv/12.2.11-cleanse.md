### 12.2.11. Cleanse

Cleanse abilities use the power of light to remove all traces of Darkness from a target, and are therefore only useful in destroying weaker creatures of the Darkness.

Techniques: -

Skills: HOLYWORD

Combos: PurfyLight

Weapons: ELSYDEON

Effective Against: All Darkspawn and Evil Beings (not bosses)

Ineffective Against: Biomonsters, Machines, all bosses
