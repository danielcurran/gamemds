The Land Rover is the first of the three vehicles acquired. Its primary function is to allow the party to cross quicksand, although it can traverse any open land in the game. It is a very useful means of transportation once it has been acquired, as it can cover large distances quite quickly, and is also easier to do battle with. It is not as powerful as the Ice Digger, and probably won't be used at all after this new vehicle is acquired.
