### 7.1.1. The Ranch Owner

**Recommended Level:** 12+ · **When Available:** Immediately · **Commission Awarded:** 5000 MST

This is a request from a ranch owner at the village of Mile.

"The sand worms at my ranch have become so big that I'm at my wit's end. Please help me..."

That is the message.

After accepting this job, teleport to Mile and speak to the man directly opposite the entrance to the village. The ranch owner will tell you that he can no longer look after the dangerous Sand Worms, so he asks you to deal with the problem by whatever means necessary. Make sure you are fully healed and the game is saved, then walk through the small gate and wander around the ranch until a battle starts. You will now fight a Sand Worm.

Use your best skills and techniques available, as you will only need to defeat one of these beasts. This Sand Worm will only attack individual members of your party, so just heal whenever a character gets low on HP and keep hacking away. After a short while it should go down.

Return to the rancher and he will lament the loss of his dream, but also realise that it had to be done. He sends the commission to the guild, so return to Aiedo and pick up your hard-earned cash.
