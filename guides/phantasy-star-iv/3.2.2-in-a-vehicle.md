### 3.2.2. In a Vehicle

Even while travelling in a vehicle you may encounter hostility. Only random battles begin while in a vehicle, as they only ever take place when you are outside. Battles in vehicles are quite different from those on foot. Combat starts the same way, by the screen splitting and (different) combat music beginning. You will then see the battlefield from the view of the vehicle, with the dashboard at the bottom of the screen. The menu on the left also has different options, and these are described below:

ATTAC - Fire at an enemy with the vehicle's standard weapon. You must select a target if more than one.

OPTIN - Choose a special weapon from the list. Those marked in red have been exhausted, and cannot be used.

RUN - Exactly the same as on foot.

The combat then takes place in pretty much the same way as it does on foot, except that damage inflicted by enemies drops the vehicle's SP, not the characters' HP. If the vehicle's SP drops to 0, the battle is lost and the game is over. All lost SP is restored at the end of battle.
