### 12.2.2. Water

Water abilities make use of intense cold and freezing to cause chilling damage to targets. They can be in the form of physical ice shards or highly decreased temperatures.

Techniques: WAT, GIWAT, NAWAT

Skills: -

Combos: Blizzard

Weapons: -

Effective Against: Motavian Biomonsters, Slimes, Sandworms, Gy-Laguiah

**Ineffective Against:** Dezolisian Biomonsters, Bits, Rens, Ghosts, Flamers,

Armour Mechs, Knights, Patrollers, Rip Fiends, Zio
