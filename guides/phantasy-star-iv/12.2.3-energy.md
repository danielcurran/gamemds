### 12.2.3. Energy

Energy abilities make use of power in its raw form, projected in either a beam or a blast. They are not affiliated with any particular element, and so are impossible to resist.

Techniques: TSU, GITHU, NATHU

Skills: RAYBLADE, MEGID

Combos: ParadinBlw

Weapons: -

**Effective Against:** All Darkspawn and Evil Beings (not Spearmen or Mages),

Dark Force, Profound Darkness

Ineffective Against: -
