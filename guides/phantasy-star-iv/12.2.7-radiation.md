### 12.2.7. Radiation

Radiation abilities make use of nuclear reactions and non-material energy to cause internal damage throughout enemies. They are usually in the form of rays, beams or blasts.

Techniques: -

Skills: FLARE, ASTRAL, LEGEON, POSIBOLT

Combos: Triblaster

Weapons: All LASR, PLSM, LACO and GUARD weapons, PULSEVULCN, PHOTNERASR

**Special:** A PLSM-FIELD automatically makes its paired weapon radiation-

based while equipped, regardless of its original affinity

Effective Against: -

Ineffective Against: Rip Fiends
