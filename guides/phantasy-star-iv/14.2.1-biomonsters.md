### 14.2.1. Biomonsters

Biomonsters are hostile creatures that are the product of genetic mutation, unusual environmental conditions and the presence of the Darkness. They are varied in their appearance and nature, but all share similar properties. They are mostly susceptible to certain status effects and elemental damage, although certain biomonsters will have different weaknesses and resistances to some elements. Most biomonsters are encountered while outside or in the earlier dungeons.
