### 12.2.1. Fire

Fire abilities make use of intense heat and combustion to cause burning damage to targets. They can be in the form of physical flames or highly increased temperatures.

Techniques: FOI, GIFOI, NAFOI

Skills: FLAELI

Combos: Fire Storm, ShootinStr

Weapons: FLAMESWORD, NAPALMSHOT (DYNAMITE is fire-based)

**Special:** A CRMCSHIELD automatically makes its paired weapon fire-based

while equipped, regardless of its original affinity

**Effective Against:** Dezolisian Biomonsters (not Helex), Slimes, Rappys,

Undead, Winged Plants, Lizard-Men, Lashiec

**Ineffective Against:** Right Blades, Bits, Sentries, Rens, Ghosts, Servitors,

Armour Mechs, Knights, Rip Fiends, De-Vars, Sa-Lews
