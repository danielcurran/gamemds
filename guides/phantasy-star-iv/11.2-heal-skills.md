## 11.2. Heal Skills

There are only five Heal Skills, so there isn't much point splitting them into groups. None of the Heal Skills has direct Technique counterparts, so they are all extremely useful. They include android healing, healing of all characters (including androids), HP-based healing, and TP restoration.

ATARAXIA - Usable by Raja. ¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯ A unique and invaluable Skill that restores a target's TP by 1/4 of its maximum value. Only works on organic life. Can only be used in battle.

MEDIC PW - Usable by Demi. ¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯ A fairly useful Skill throughout the game, as it revives and heals all organic characters' HP by 1/4 of their maximum value. Only works on organic life.

MEDICE - Usable by Kyra. ¯¯¯¯¯¯ This Skill is quite helpful in the Eclipse Torch section of the game, as it heals around 170-230 HP to one character. Works on any character.

MIRACLE - Usable by Raja. ¯¯¯¯¯¯¯ Highly useful in the later stages of the game, this Skill heals all characters by about 130-180 HP. Works on all characters.

RECOVER - Usable by Demi and Wren. ¯¯¯¯¯¯¯ This unique Skill performs a self-regeneration process on the android, restoring between 270-340 HP to itself. Works on the android that uses it.
