## 6.6. Walkthrough Summary

In case you didn't want the exact guidelines to get through a particular area of the game, I have compiled this summary to outline the different stages of the game. This isn't entirely free of spoilers, but it doesn't give any actual details either. It is also accompanied by the sections that cover each specific area of the game.

Chaz and Alys go to Piata. 1.1.

Find Alys, then speak to the principal. 1.1.

Go down to the basement and Hahn joins the party. 1.1.

Go through the basement and fight the Igglanova. 1.2.

Go back to the principal. 1.3.

Leave Piata and head north then east to Zema. 1.3.

Go through Birth Valley to find the Professor. 1.4.

Leave Zema and go south to Molcum. 1.5.

Rune joins the party. 1.5.

Head east then north to Valley Maze. 1.6.

Go through then enter Tonoe. 1.7.

Speak to Grandfather Dorin. 1.8.

Rune leaves and Gryz joins the party. 1.8.

Go through the basement and get the Alshline. 1.9.

Return to Zema and use the Alshline. 1.10.

Fight the Igglanova, then go into Bio-Plant. 1.10.

Head through Bio-Plant to find the Professor. 1.11.

Rika joins the party. 1.11.

Go north then west to Aiedo. 2.1.

Head east then north and go through Wreckage. 2.2.

Head through Passageway north of Aiedo. 2.3.

Go east then south and into Zio's Fort. 2.3.

Go through Zio's Fort and fight Juza. 2.4.

Fight Zio, then Alys and Hahn leave the party. 2.4.

Demi joins the party and you get the Land Rover. 2.5.

Go northeast and into Monsen. 2.6.

Head northeast and go through the Plate System. 2.7.

Go south then southeast to Termi. 2.8.

Head southwest and go into Ladea Tower. 2.8.

Go up Ladea Tower and Rune joins the party. 2.9.

Go to the top and fight Gy-Laguiah. 2.9.

Get the Psyco-Wand, then Alys dies. 2.10.

Return to Zio's Fort and enter Nurvus. 2.10.

Head through Nurvus, and fight Zio again. 2.11.

Gryz and Demi leave the party. 2.11.

Go into the Spaceport and head to Zelan. 2.12.

Go through Zelan and Wren joins the party. 2.13.

Crash on Dezolis, and Raja joins the party. 3.1.

Go north to Ryuon and speak to Gyuna. 3.2.

Head northwest to Tyler and enter the Hanger. 3.2.

Go through the Hanger and raise the Spaceport. 3.3.

Get into the Landale and fly to Kuran. 3.3.

Go through Kuran and fight Dark Force. 3.4.

Get the Ice Digger, then go north. 3.5.

Go through Mysterious Valley and get the Silvr-Tusk. 3.5.

Head south then northeast to Zosa. 3.6.

Head northeast and go to the Climate Control Centre. 3.6.

Fight Gy-Laguiah, then through to fight D-Elm-Lars. 3.7.

Leave, then head southeast to Meese. 3.8.

Raja leaves the party. 3.8.

Go northeast to the Carnivorous Trees. 3.9.

Retreat, then Kyra joins the party. 3.9.

Go southwest then southeast to Esper Mansion. 3.10.

Meet Lutz, then leave Esper Mansion. 3.10.

Go southwest to Jut, then west to Weapons Plant. 4.1.

Go through, then go back to Gumbious Temple. 4.2.

Witness the theft of the EclpsTorch. 4.3.

Go back to the Spaceport, then fly to Air Castle. 4.4.

Head through Air Castle and fight Xe-A-Thouls. 4.5.

Leave, then go back into Air Castle. 4.6.

Go through Air Castle Basement and fight Lashiec. 4.6.

Get the EclpsTorch, then return to Dezolis. 4.7.

Destroy the Carnivorous Trees. 4.7.

Head north and go into Garuberk Tower. 4.7.

Go through Garuberk Tower and fight Dark Force. 4.8.

Kyra leaves the party, and Gumbious Temple explodes. 4.9.

Head back to Gumbious Temple and enter the hole. 4.9.

Speak to the Dezolisian bishop. 4.9.

Go to the Spaceport and fly back to Motavia. 4.10.

Get the Hydrofoil then head to Krup. 4.10.

Go east onto the island, and Seth joins the party. 5.1.

Head through Island Cave to the top. 5.1.

Go into Soldiers' Temple and get the Aero-Prism. 5.1.

Seth leaves the party, then fight Dark Force. 5.1.

Return to the Spaceport and go to Rykros. 5.2.

Speak to Le Roof, then go to the Strength Tower. 5.3.

Go through the Strength Tower and fight De-Vars. 5.4.

Get the Rings, then go to the Courage Tower. 5.4.

Go through the Courage Tower and fight Sa-Lews. 5.5.

Get the Rings, then return to Silence Temple. 5.5.

Speak to Le Roof and learn the Algo genesis. 5.6.

Head back to Dezolis and into Esper Mansion. 5.7.

Go to Lutz's room and enter Sacred Sword Cave. 5.7.

Get Elsydeon, then return to Motavia. 5.7.

Select the fifth party member. 5.8.

Go to Mile, then head south and into The Edge. 5.9.

Go through The Edge, then fight Profound Darkness. 5.10.

The game is complete. 5.11.
