## 16.3. Dezolis

Dezolis is the third planet of the Algo system, but its size is difficult to determine. Although the party are able to traverse some of its surface, they are trapped on all sides by mountains. This prevents seeing how big Dezolis really is. This aside, Dezolis is the climatic opposite of Motavia, being a very cold planet. Like Motavia, Dezolis has had serious problems with the climate systems, and this causes the terrible snowstorm seen when the party first arrives.

The native humanoids on Dezolis are the Dezolisians. They are green, reptilian humans whose thick skins protect them from the natural cold of their planet. Like the Motavians, the Dezolisians were also hosts for the fleeing Parmanians. Luckily for them, however, the Parmanians found life a little more difficult than their Motavia-bound cousins, so the Dezolisian population has not seen any problems. They are a fun-loving, intelligent race, who are well-known for their forging of the rare metal laconia.

Dezolis is home to a number of villages and one town, fairly equally split between Parmanians and Dezolisians, and also houses a fair few secrets. The game moves onto Dezolis shortly after acquiring the shuttle, and is the main area of the game until the snowstorm has been abated.
