# 14. Enemies

A sizeable part of the game will be spent in battle. This section is designed to provide information on every biomonster, machine, darkspawn, and evil being that you may have to face. The first two sections provide lists of creatures by their location and their type. The last section gives a complete alpabetical list of every enemy in the game, along with their statistics and additional notes.
