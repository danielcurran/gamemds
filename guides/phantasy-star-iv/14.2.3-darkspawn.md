### 14.2.3. Darkspawn

Darkspawn are the non-intelligent manifestations of the Darkness, that have come into being due to the weakening of the Algo seal. They are unnatural, unfriendly, demonic creatures that roam areas saturated with the Darkness' spirit. They are usually immune to most status effects, yet are highly susceptible to certain instant-death abilities and light effects. They are often resistant to a certain element or two, yet are usually weak against opposing elements. Most darkspawn are encountered in later dungeons.
