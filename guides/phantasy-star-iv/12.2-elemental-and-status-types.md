## 12.2. Elemental and Status Types

Each attack in the game are based on an element, and this modifies the damage it inflicts on an enemy depending on the enemy's affinity to that element (weakness, resistance or immunity). There are also different types of status effect, some of which may affect certain enemies while others will not. This section looks at each elemental and status type, and each Technique, Skill and Weapon that is based on them.
