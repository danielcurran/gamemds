### 14.2.4. Evil Beings

Evil beings are the followers of the Darkness, working to wreak havoc and destruction in Algo to prepare for the Darkness' return. They are usually humanoid in appearance (although they may be disproportional), yet possess inhuman strength or abilities. They are similar to darkspawn in their resistances and weaknesses, although they act with more purpose and are generally more difficult to defeat. Most evil beings are encountered in later dungeons, and represent some of the bosses in the game.
