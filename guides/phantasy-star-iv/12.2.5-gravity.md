### 12.2.5. Gravity

Gravity abilities make use of localised fields of gravitational force to warp and twist targets. They are only in the form of pockets of such force.

Techniques: GRA, GIGRA, NAGRA

Skills: -

Combos: -

Weapons: -

Effective Against: Rip Fiends

Ineffective Against: Slimes, Ghosts, Undead, Prophallus, De-Vars, Sa-Lews
