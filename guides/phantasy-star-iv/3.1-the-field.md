# 3. Game Mechanics

<a id="s3-1"></a>

## 3.1. The Field

All travelling, exploration and conversation takes place in the field. There are three different field types - outside, town and dungeon. These field types are explained below:

Outside - All travel between towns and dungeons takes place here. You can't examine things when outside, but you can use vehicles once you have acquired them. Random battles will initiate here. You may save the game anywhere outside.

Town - Located around the world are towns and villages. They are safe havens from the monsters outside, and also provide opportunity to rest and shop. Useful information can often be found in towns, mainly by speaking to its inhabitants. There may also be items to find. Random battles will not take place in towns. You may save the game in towns.

Dungeon - Most of the action in the game takes place in dungeons. This is just a general term - dungeons can be caves, towers, castles, or any other enclosed space inhabited by monsters. Most dungeons contain a specific objective, and this is occasionally guarded by a boss (sometimes more than one) - usually the most powerful creature(s) in that dungeon. Random battles will initiate here. You may not save the game in a dungeon.
