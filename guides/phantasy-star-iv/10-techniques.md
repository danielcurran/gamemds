# 10. Techniques

Techniques are special powers that characters and monsters may possess. They work by emitting a form of psionic energy that can have a variety of different effects, based on the Technique being used. This energy drains the user of the Technique, and this is represented in the form of TP.

Most characters in the game have access to some Techniques, although some more than others. Enemies may also have access to a few Techniques as well, but this is not common.

All but one Techniques are learned by characters when they reach a certain Level. The method of obtaining the remaining Technique, Megid, is described in more detail in '7.2. Megid - The Anger Technique'.

The first part of this section lists the different Techniques available to each character, split into the three types of Technique, along with the Level at which they learn the Techniques.

The second part gives a description of each Technique, split into the three different types, along with TP costs and information on who or what can use the Techniques.
