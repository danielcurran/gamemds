Alys is in the party from the beginning, but she doesn't remain for very
long. She is the most famous hunter on Motavia, and has acquired the
nickname of the Eight Stroke Warrior because of her reputation as a skilled
fighter. Nothing is really known about Alys' past, nor even how old she is,
but she and Rune seem to know each other from a long time ago. Alys found
Chaz while he was living as a criminal, and helped him sort his life out.

She is an attractive and strong woman, and she always needs to be on top of
the situation. Alys is also very open-minded, accepting other people's
views but at the same time being very attentive to their intentions. She
knows her talents, and seems to have a very mercenary attitude to using
them for others, as Hahn initially finds out at his cost. However, we later
learn that her reason for fighting is not one based on money, but a deeper
and more noble cause.


There isn't a great opportunity to learn much about Alys, as she falls
victim to Zio quite early in the game. Her valiant sacrifice to protect
Chaz is a sure sign of her nobility.


Alys' favoured weapons are slashers, and she wears mainly crowns and heavy
armours.

Alys' Techniques are rather limited, having access to only fire and force,
and a couple of assistance Techs.

Her Skills include an attack, a sleep ability, and causing instant death.
