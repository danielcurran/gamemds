### 6.2.8. The Furthest Point East

> 🥉 **Algo Legend** — Inspect the Alis Landale statue. _(RetroAchievements · 2 pts)_
> 🥉 **A Memento of our Trip** — Place a particular triangular flag in your house and admire it. _(RetroAchievements · 3 pts)_
> 🥉 **The Romance of Travel** — Place a particular wood doll in your house and admire it. _(RetroAchievements · 4 pts)_
> 🥈 **Beyond the Sand Sea** — Defeat a wild Sand Worm in east Motavia. _(RetroAchievements · 10 pts)_

```
Starting Party: Demi , Gryz , Chaz , Rika
Starting Level: 13-14, 16-17, 16-17, 16-17
After a good night's sleep, leave Monsen and get in your Land Rover. Head
northeast towards the Plate System, then continue past it to the east until
you reach a mountain range. From here, head south through the quicksand and
head into the village of Termi.

Termi is a village steeped in legend, as it is the hometown of Alis, a
legendary heroine from two thousand years ago. Most of the inhabitants are
proud of this, and look after their village well.

By now, you are probably carrying a lot of excess equipment, so go to the
Armour Store and sell the TITNDAGGER, two TITNSLASHRs, SABER-CLAW, two
CRMC-KNIFEs, CRMC-SWORD, STRUGGL-AX, LASR-KNIFE, LASRSLASHR, IMPACTER,
CRMCSHIELD, two LASRBARRIRs, CIRCLET, GRPT-CROWN, HEAD-GEAR, TITN-MAIL,
GRPT-SUIT, CRMC-MAIL and the TITN-ARMOR to free up your inventory.

Next, unequip Rika's GRPT-CROWN and the three CRMC-MAILs and sell these to
the Armour Store as well. You may also have some other redundant equipment
that you won from enemies, so sell all of this too. This, in total, should
net you a tidy sum of at least 26000 Mesetas.

Using this new income, you shouldn't have too much trouble purchasing a
PSY-CROWN for Rika and three PSY-MAILs for Chaz, Gryz and Rika, and still
have made a profit of over 1000 Mesetas from your sales. You may also want
to sell any MONOMATEs and other old curative items that are cluttering up
your item list.

Unfortunately, there isn't a Tool Store in Termi, so if you need to stock
up on any supplies you will need to teleport to another village. You really
only need a few DIMATEs, a couple of ANTIDOTEs and CURE-PARALs, and perhaps
a TELEPIPE and an ESCAPIPE just in case.

Once you are fully prepared, spend a night at the Inn to refresh the party,
then leave Termi. Chaz, Gryz and Rika should now be safely into Level 17,
and Demi should be at least Level 14. If they are not, spend a little time
in the area earning them some experience points until they are.

Rest at the Inn at Termi again if you need to, then get in the Land Rover
and head southwest towards the coast until you reach a large white tower.
This is Ladea Tower. Save your game, then enter.
```
