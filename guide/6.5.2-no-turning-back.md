### 6.5.2. No Turning Back

> 🥉 **Falz into Darkness** — Defeat a rare enemy at The Edge. _(RetroAchievements · 5 pts)_
> 🥉 **Fused Shadows** — Defeat a Twinarms that fuses from Bladeright + Hakenleft in the Rykros towers. _(RetroAchievements · 3 pts)_

Teleport to Kadary and head down to the Spaceport. Don't bother with the
Regen Platforms, and instead head up to the Landale. Select Rykros as the
destination (suitably unmarked on the map), and watch the launch. Shortly
after, a cut-scene begins.

Chaz asks if they are going in the right direction, and Wren confirms that
they are on the correct course. Chaz wonders if it is a structure like the
Air Castle or an artificial satellite, but Wren simply responds that if
they stay on their current heading they will soon leave the Algo system.
Rune insists that they have to keep going, and Chaz remains astounded. At
that moment, the Aero-Prism flashes, and a green planet appears in front of
the Landale. Wren announces this, and Rune notices that it is not apparent
on the radar. Wren informs them that the planet is neither Motavia, Dezolis
or Parma, and that the planet is not recorded in his database. It is Chaz
who realises that it must be Rykros, and so they land on this new planet.

Arriving inside a strange building, the party head out of the Landale and
into a crystal chamber. They walk up to a glowing symbol in the centre of
the floor, and the room disappears around them into a starry deep-space
backdrop. The image of a galaxy appears below their feet, and a hidden
voice welcomes them, calling them Protectors. Chaz wonders what is going
on, and the source of the voice introduces itself as Le Roof of Rykros. It
explains that it remains on the planet to pass on the story of the genesis
of Algo. Chaz notes that he was right about the planet being Rykros, and Le
Roof confirms that it is, stating that it is the fourth planet in the Algo
star system. Wren disagrees, reporting that there are only supposed to be
three planets in the system, but Le Roof says that Rykros is protected by
a powerful and invisible barrier that stops any normal being from seeing or
otherwise sensing it. It continues by informing the party that Rykros'
orbit is highly elliptical, only passing near Algo once every thousand
years. Rune recalls the message the Dezolisian bishop gave, that the Aero-
Prism shows the way when Rykros returns, and adds that he now understands
what it meant by 'returns'.

Chaz gets to the point and tells Le Roof that they have come to Rykros to
find out about The Profound Darkness. He asks what it is, why it is
causing the disasters on Algo, and where it is now, and demands that Le
Roof tells them everything. Le Roof thinks for a moment, then says that he
will tell all to the courageous Protectors who had discovered Rykros (the
party), but adds that they must first perform a task. He informs them that
there are two towers on Rykros called The Courage Tower and The Strength
Tower. He charges the party to go and meet the guardians of these towers,
and tells them that he will truly recognise them as 'Protectors' when they
return and will tell them everything they wish to know. Chaz asks if Le
Roof is testing them, and Le Roof replies that it is absolutely necessary.
Chaz realises that they have no choice, so he announces their departure.
Before the party leave, Le Roof finishes by saying that they must hurry,
as the hand of the Darkness is already closing in. He awaits their return.

After the normal surroundings are restored, you will discover that the
symbol the party was standing on is also a Regen Platform. The party is
automatically refreshed, and you have a new planet to explore.
