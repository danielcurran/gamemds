### 6.4.5. Terrible Triumvirate

> 🏅 **Limit: Xe-A-Thouls** — Defeat the Xe-A-Thouls in Air Castle. Combined level limit: 125, party of 5. _(RetroAchievements · 25 pts)_

```
Starting Party: Wren , Chaz , Rika , Kyra , Rune
Starting Level: 32   , 33-34, 35-36, 32-33, 32
 // DUNGEON #17 ¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯\
|/  Air Castle             \_________________________________
   Enemies: Bladeright, DimensWorm, FrostSaber, Spector, StoneHeads
  Treasure: 1000 MST, TRIMATE, 2000 MST, TRIMATE, 4000 MST,
            SWIFT-HELM, GENOCYCLAW, ESCAPIPE, 8000 MST, SOL-DEW
 \
  \   Boss: Xe-A-Thoul x3
   \______________________________


Go up and through the main entrance to the castle. Continue up across the
next small room and then up all the way to three flickering images of the
witches you met in Gumbious Temple. Examine them, and Kyra will notice that
they are just illusions. Rune adds that are also forming an 'invisible'
barrier (it's amazing how visible these barriers are). Chaz then suggests
that they will have to find the real witches and destroy them.

Go a little down and then right, taking the door in the upper wall along
this small passage. Head up, right and then down, and go straight into the
small room below you to acquire 1000 MST. Walk back up out of the room,
then continue to the right, down a little, right a bit more, and then up
through the next doorway. Continue up until you reach the crack in the
floor, then head left and up through the door.

Back outside, walk straight up and through the door back into the castle.
Head up actoss the small room, then turn right as soon as you pass the
doorway. Turn down at the corner and pick up the TRIMATE from the bottom of
the corridor. Go back up and a little to the left, then continue upwards.
Turn right after the doorway, then go down through the next door and ascend
the staircase to the floor above.

Head up, left at the corner and then down, and continue through the door
out onto the castle roof. Head a little down, then right to the edge. Walk
all the way down the battlement and through the doorway at the bottom to
take you back into the castle. Head down, completely ignoring the staircase
(it leads to a dead end), then go down into the small room to collect 2000
MST. Return back up, walk left, and then go all the way down the thin
corridor, across the small room, and back outside the castle.

Walk down, left, down and then left, and go up through the doorway. Turn
left again, then up along the balcony and go right at the top. Take the
doorway on the right and then go up the corridor, turning left and then
down. Head left, a little up and then left until you are below another door
that leads out onto the roof. You will also see the witch illusions below
you. Ignore this door, as it leads to a dead end, and continue left along
the balcony, a little down, then left and up through the next doorway.
Obtain the TRIMATE from the centre of this room, then go down through the
door on the left, following the corridor and out of the door at the bottom.
Head right, down and left along the balcony, then go through the doorway at
the end to take you back out onto the castle roof.

Go left, turn up and then left, and take the door back into the castle on
the left. Head up across the small room and up the thin corridor, then turn
right at the top. Ignore the stairs for the moment, and enter the small
room below to procure 4000 MST. Go back up and descend the staircase.

Head down and through the door out of the room. Ignore the path to the
right as it leads to a dead end, and instead continue downwards and then to
the right at the bottom. Turn up at the corner, then right again and down
into the next room. Retrieve the SWIFT-HELM and equip it to Chaz, before
returning all the way back round to the stairs. Take them.

Step a bit to the right, then go out onto the roof through the door above.
Head up across the battlements, turning right as soon as possible. Continue
right and take the door back into the castle at the end. In this single
room, pick up the GENOCYCLAW and equip it to Rika alongside the SILVR-TUSK.

Leave the room, then head a little to the left and up across the roof to
the next doorway. Step through and then continue upwards, turning left at
the corner and then heading down. Descend the staircase back down to the
level below, then walk up, right and down until you reach another larger
area. Go over to the left wall and then down into the small room to collect
the ESCAPIPE, then return out of the room and back to the right. Head down
and back out of the castle.

Turn right, down and then continue right all the way until you see a door
in the top wall. Directly opposite this door on the wall below is another
door. Go through the lower door and find yourself in a small chamber. This
is the room that the invisible barrier was guarding, and inside it is a
Regen Platform. Use this if you need to, then head up and out of the room.

Return up to the top door, then go through and head up to find another set
of the witch illusions. Walk left and go up through the next doorway, then
head right until you reach a junction. The right path takes you nowhere, so
go up between the chasms until you reach another junction. Again, the right
passage leads to a dead end, so head left and then up across the steps.
Turn left and then up through the doorway to take you back onto the roof.

Go left and then down along the roof, and descend the stairs at the bottom
(ignore the route to the right as it leads nowhere). Head right and then
up along the passageway, ignoring the stairs to the right for the moment
and continuing up the thin corridor into a small room. Acquire the 8000 MST
that are here, then return back down to the staircase and take them to the
floor above.

Head down along the thin balcony and then head right at the bottom. When
you reach a junction, head north and descend the stairs. Ignore the passage
above as it leads to a dead end, and go down the corridor to obtain the
SOL-DEW at the bottom. Return back up the passage, then go left and take
the stairs again. Go back down to the junction and turn right, then go up
through the doorway at the end. Take the stairs.

In the similar room above, head down and out onto the castle roof again,
then go left until you see a gap in the wall above. The doorway at the top
here leads to the three witches, and you'll want to make sure that each of
your characters is at least Level 34 before entering. It is unlikely that
anyone but Rika is at this Level at the moment, so spend some time in the
Air Castle gaining EXP until the others have caught up. Return to the
Spaceport to heal up when necessary, and save the game as often as you can.
You could even pay a visit to Motavia and test your skill against the
Sand Worms around Monsen, as they provide a lot of EXP when defeated.

Once everyone has fulfilled the Level 34 requirement, restore the party at
the Spaceport, save your game, and then carefully make your way back
through the Air Castle (escape from battles that you feel you may take a
lot of damage from). Once you have returned to the elaborate doorway, heal
any damage that the party may have received and then walk inside.

Walk up and approach the three witches. The party will stop just below
them and the witches will address you. The middle one will laugh, noting
that the party have come. The left one will then laugh, and tell you that
they have no use for anyone but Lutz, and the middle one will add that they
only have one use for him before laughing again. Finally, the left one will
say that they will kill him with their own hands. Battle then begins.

 __________

Unless you are prepared and use the strategy given below, this will be an
exceptionally tough battle, and definitely the most difficult you will have
faced so far. If you are prepared, however, you may find that the battle is
no harder than the others. Even on their own, Xe-A-Thouls are considerably
powerful, but together they can combine their power to inflict a severe
amount of damage on the entire party. The most important things to do first
in this battle are to set up a Barrier to reduce your characters' damage
and to drop the Xe-A-Thouls' number to two.

In the first round, have Chaz use RAYBLADE, Rika use DBLSLASH, Rune use
EFESS, Wren use BARRIER, and Kyra use NAFOI. Make sure all three of the
single-target attacks are aimed at the same Xe-A-Thoul. This should inflict
around 1300-1350 HP of damage on that Xe-A-Thoul. Hopefully, Wren will be
able to set up his BARRIER before the Xe-A-Thouls can unleash their
combination attack, THNDRBLAST. Otherwise, each member of your party will
suffer a great deal of damage. Also, one or two of the Xe-A-Thouls may use
GIWAT or GIZAN, or they may just attack.

In the following rounds, have Chaz use AIRSLASH, Rika use SAR (or GISAR if
the party are very hurt), Rune continue to use EFESS, Wren use BURSTROC (or
RECOVER if he is low on HP), and Kyra use HEWN (or an appropriate curative
Technique if necessary). After your party has unleashed their first attack
or two, the most damaged Xe-A-Thoul will fall. This prevents them using
THNDRBLAST again. From now on, the battle will be much easier.

Keep following the above pattern, ensuring each member of your party stays
above half of their maximum HP, and the witches will be finished in a mere
three or four rounds.
Nothing is said once the battle is over, which is an unusual event after a
major boss battle. The room that the Xe-A-Thouls were guarding above only
contains a Regen Platform, so don't bother going in there. Instead, use
HINAS or an ESCAPIPE to return to the Air Castle entrance, then take the
Landale to a Spaceport. Use a Regen Platform here and save the game.
```
