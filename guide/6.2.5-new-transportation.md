### 6.2.5. New Transportation

> 🥈 **Demi** — Recruit Demi. _(RetroAchievements · 10 pts)_
> 🥉 **This Luster! This Massiveness!** — Discover the Land Rover. _(RetroAchievements · 3 pts)_

```
Starting Party: Gryz , Alys , Chaz , Rika , Hahn
Starting Level: 15-16, 14-15, 15-16, 14-15, 14-15
The party meet Saya in Krup, and take Alys to a bed at the Inn. Her
condition has not improved. Chaz notes that Zio was unlike anything he has
ever fought, and Rika diagnoses that Alys seems to be possessed by an evil
force. Demi explains that Zio built his fort upon Nurvus, and Chaz realises
that Nurvus must be behind the barrier they couldn't cross before. Alys
then mentions that Rune may be able to help, pointing out that there is no
way they can get past Zio's magical barrier with what they have now. Rika
asks who Rune is, and Chaz replies with his opinion, reluctantly agreeing
that they may need his help after all. As Rune went off with Grandfather
Dorin, Gryz decides that they must have gone to a place called Ladea Tower,
located on a small island across the quicksand to the east. While Hahn and
Gryz try to work out how they will cross, Demi suggests that they can use
the Land Rover, an all-purpose, armour-plated car. Apparently, there should
be one located at the Machine Centre to the south. They decide to leave,
although Chaz asks Hahn to stay behind and look after Alys. Hahn isn't too
happy about the idea at first, but he agrees in the end. You are then free
to go.

Head south along the coast, continuing until you stop and view a metal
structure rise from the ground ahead. Chaz is shocked, and Demi says that
they are free to enter. Continue south and enter the Machine Centre.

There are no monsters in the Machine Centre, so simply walk up and take
the elevator to the floor below. Pick up the CONTROLKEY from the left side
of the passage and use the Regen Platform on the right to restore yourself
to full strength. When ready, continue down the passage and through the
door to a huge chamber.

Demi approaches the panel, using the control key to access the computer. As
she stands at the terminal she notes that there are many machines housed in
the centre, although most of them are quite old and will need repairs. Chaz
is impressed, and asks Demi if Zio caught her while she was trying to get
the system back under control. She concurs, but then goes on to state that
getting rid of Zio won't solve the problem. It seems that Zelan, the main
control system for Motavia's environment, is the source of the malfunctions
on the planet, but the fact that Zelan is an orbiting satellite makes this
fact a little useless at present. After this, a huge vehicle rolls into
view. A little while afterwards, the party are outside the Machine Centre
and in their very own Land Rover.
```
