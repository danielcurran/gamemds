## 16.6. Special Locations

```
There are a few areas that can be accessed from the outside, but do not fit
into either category of town or dungeon. They are neither settlements nor
hostile environments. This section details each of these sites, plus their
relevance to the game plot, in alphabetical order.
Dezolis Spaceport - Location: Southwest of Tyler and northwest of Ryuon
                    Treasure: ICE-DIGGER (later in the game)

                    This small structure houses two Regen Platforms, and
                    allows the party access to the Landale through the door
                    to the north of the Spaceport. After Kuran has been
                    shut down, the party acquire the Ice Digger from this
                    Spaceport in order to pass the Dezolis ice walls.
Esper Mansion     - Location: Southeast of Meese and northeast of Jut
                    Treasure: LACO-ROD, RFLC-ROBE, SOL-DEW, 10000 MST 

                    The spiritual home of the Espers is a large building
                    hidden away in a small canyon. It is a beautifully
                    decorated mansion, its purpose being to allow Espers
                    to train and meditate. It is also the resting place of
                    Lutz, as his Telepathy Ball can be found in Lutz's
                    room in the basement of the inner sanctuary. This well-
                    guarded structure is located in the courtyard in the
                    centre of the Esper Mansion. It is protected by a field
                    of magic that prohibits teleportation. Lutz's room also
                    allows access to Sacred Sword Cave later in the game.
Gumbious Temple   - Location: Northwest of Jut
                    Treasure: TRIMATE, MOON-DEW, STAR-DEW, SOL-DEW

                    This huge temple is the centre of the Dezolisian faith,
                    where the devotees of their religion spend much of
                    their time. It is home to a number of priests, led by
                    the Gumbious Temple bishop, who worship the eternal
                    light of the Eclipse Torch. The party first visit this
                    ancient temple to request to borrow the Eclipse Torch,
                    but they are instead witness to the torch's theft.
                    The basement of the temple contains two areas of note -
                    a small treasure chamber and a passage blocked by two
                    Dezolisian guards. After the party have recovered the
                    Eclipse Torch and destroyed the Garuberk Tower, the
                    Gumbious Temple explodes. This allows the party access
                    to the secret room of the temple, where they meet the
                    Dezolisian bishop. He tells the party of The Profound
                    Darkness, and that in order to defeat it they must find
                    something called Rykros.
Machine Centre    - Location: South of Krup and southeast of Molcum
                    Treasure: CONTROLKEY, LAND-ROVER

                    A small structure that was initially buried underground
                    at the beginning of the game, the Machine Centre is
                    raised by Demi after the party decide to get the Land
                    Rover in order to cross the quicksand. It contains a
                    CONTROLKEY that must be used to access the computer
                    terminal through the southern door, and a Regen
                    Platform. The party can then obtain the LAND-ROVER.
Motavia Spaceport - Location: South of Kadary and north of Mile
                    Treasure: HYDROFOIL (later in the game)

                    This small structure houses two Regen Platforms, and
                    allows the party access to their spacecraft through the
                    door to the north of the Spaceport. It is also the site
                    where the five optional characters are congregated at
                    the end of the game. After speaking to the Dezolisian
                    bishop, the party acquire the Hydrofoil from this
                    Spaceport in order to cross the water on Motavia.
Silence Temple    - Location: Centre of Rykros
                    Treasure: GUARDSHILD, GUARDSWORD, TELEPIPE, TRIMATE

                    The Silence Temple is in the centre of the small area
                    that makes up Rykros. It is the only area of the planet
                    that is safe from attacks. It is also home to a Regen
                    Platform, that doubles up as a means of communicating
                    with an ancient being called Le Roof. He gives the
                    party a trial to complete, after which he explains the
                    true nature of Algo and how they will be able to defeat
                    The Profound Darkness.
Zelan             - Location: Artificial satellite in Motavia's orbit
                    Treasure: PLSM-SWORD, PLSM-CLAW, PLSMDAGGER,
                              PLSM-FIELD, PULSE-LASR, CANCELLER

                    The main control centre for the entire Algo system,
                    Zelan is assumed to be the cause of the problems on
                    Motavia at the beginning. After they visit this place
                    and meet Wren, the party discover that it is Kuran that
                    is the problem. The party can obtain the CANCELLER from
                    Zelan, in order to take Kuran out of Stealth Mode and
                    make it visible to their shuttle's radar.
```
