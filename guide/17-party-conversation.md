# 17. Party Conversation

```
Throughout the entire game, you can select TALK (or MUMBL) from the Party
Menu to get the characters to hold a short conversation regarding the
current situation and their next destination or mission. As the nature of
their discussions is not given in the walkthrough, this section details
every TALK conversation that the party can have during the game.


Chaz alone in Motavia Academy (MUMBL)

      Chaz: 'I wonder where Alys is?'


After finding Alys in Motavia Academy

      Alys: 'We better see the principal and get more details!'
      Chaz: 'So he's the one who requested the commission. I wonder where
             he is?'


After meeting the Academy Principal

      Chaz: 'Are you saying that there are monsters in the basement of this
             academy?'
      Alys: 'Hmm... Sounds like there's something behind all this!'


After Hahn joins the party

      Alys: 'Both the principal and Hahn have asked us to inspect the
             basement.'
      Hahn: 'Are you sure you're not just trying to take my money?'
      Chaz: 'Hey, you've already paid, so don't cry over spilt milk.'
      Hahn: 'I wonder if I'll be OK going with these people...'


After defeating Igglanova

      Chaz: 'I wonder what that capsules in the basement are for?'
      Alys: 'I'm going to make the principal tell us! Hey, you! You're
             coming along too!'
      Hahn: 'Who m...me?'


After speaking to the Academy Principal

      Hahn: 'Birth Valley is located northeast of Piata. Let's hurry and go
             to the professor's rescue!'
      Alys: 'Ah... Zio. I don't like the sound of that!'


After finding Professor Holt

      Hahn: 'Oh, no! Professor Holt has been turned to stone!'
      Chaz: 'He'll be all right, Hahn. We can go to Molcum and obtain the
             medicine Alshline.'
      Hahn: 'Molcum... you mean that Motavian village south of Zema?'
      Alys: 'Oh, calm down! Really, you're pathetic!'


After Rune joins the party

      Rune: 'By the time I arrived, it was too late. Sorry.'
      Alys: 'Hmmm...'
      Hahn: 'But, once we get to Tonoe, we'll be able to obtain Alshline,
             right?'
      Chaz: 'Tonoe... now that's north of the village of Krup, right?'


After Gryz joins the party

      Chaz: 'So there's Alshline in the underground warehouse of Tonoe?'
      Alys: 'Thanks for being out guide, Gryz.'
      Gryz: 'No problem. You can leave everything to me.'


After acquiring Alshline

      Alys: 'We've finally obtained the Alshline!'
      Hahn: 'Let's hurry to Zema and rescue the professor and his team.'
      Gryz: 'I'll join you.'
      Chaz: 'Gryz...'


After defeating Igglanova

      Chaz: 'Professor Holt has gone into the depths of Birth Valley and
             hasn't come back since.'
      Hahn: 'After all we did to obtain the Alshline to turn him from stone
             back to flesh.'
      Alys: 'This teacher of yours is such a pain in the butt!'


After Rika joins the party

      Rika: 'We've got to hurry and stop the system that's running out of
             control... for Seed's sake, too!'
      Chaz: 'First, we'll have to rescue this android called Demi.'
      Gryz: 'We'll have to go to Zio's Fort. Just what we all wanted to
             do!'
      Hahn: 'You're talking about that castle beyond the quicksand that can
             be seen from the village of Mile. I guess we'll have to go
             around from the north.'


After Demi joins the party

      Chaz: 'Oh no! Alys is...'
      Rika: 'Chaz, let's do as Alys says and find this person called Rune.'
      Demi: 'We'll need the 'Land Rover' to cross the quicksand. Let's find
             the 'Machine Center.' It's located south of the village of
             Krup!'


After finding the Machine Centre

      Demi: 'The Land Rover is inside the Machine Center! Let's hurry!'


After acquiring the Land Rover

      Chaz: 'OK! We've got the Land Rover!'
      Gryz: 'Now we can cross the quicksand to the east and get to The
             Ladea Tower.'
      Rika: 'And there's supposed to be a person called Rune there.'
      Chaz: 'I don't feel much like seeing him, but I guess we have no
             choice!'


After Rune rejoins the party

      Rune: 'To break through Zio's magic barrier, we'll need the 'Psycho-
             wand.''
      Rika: 'And it's on the topmost floor of The Ladea Tower?'
      Chaz: 'Let's defeat Zio with that wand! And...Alys needs to get
             better quick!'


After defeating Gy-Laguiah

      Chaz: 'We've defeated Zio's underling, but...'
      Rune: 'It seems that the Psycho-wand works well against him.'
      Gryz: 'Let's get it quickly before the next ones come.'


After acquiring the Psycho-wand

      Rune: 'As long as we have the Psycho-wand, we needn't be afraid of
             Zio's power.'
      Demi: 'Let's go to Nurvus in the basement of Zio's Fort...!'
      Chaz: 'Blast! Alys...!'
      Rika: 'Chaz...'


After breaking the barrier to Nurvus

      Rune: 'We've broken the invisible barrier. Now all we have to do is
             get to Nurvus!'
      Demi: 'We can't abandon the situation with Zio, but yes, stopping
             Nurvus is a higher priority!'
      Chaz: 'Yes, that's true...'
      Rika: 'Chaz...'


After defeating Zio

      Chaz: 'We've been able to stop Nurvus, thanks to Demi, but...'
      Rika: 'We've got to go to the artificial satellite 'Zelan' to restore
             the functions of the environmental control system. And Wren is
             there, right?'
      Rune: 'Whatever. Let's just hurry to 'Zelan!''


After Wren joins the party

      Wren: 'The cause of the system's abnormalities turns out to be in
             'Kuran.''
      Rika: 'The artificial satellite 'Kuran'...what did you say was
             happening there?'


After Raja joins the party

      Chaz: 'To get to Kuran, we'll have to find that space ship Raja told
             us about.'
      Rika: 'So that means we're going to the town of Tyler northwest of
             Raja's temple?'
      Raja: 'Before doing that, why don't we take a little break at the inn
             in Ryuon? You'll be able to hear a lot of fun stories, you
             won't regret it, ha, ha!'
      Chaz: 'Great...'


After speaking to Gyuna about the spaceship

      Chaz: 'According to Gyuna's story, the space ship is underground in
             the town of Tyler.'
      Rika: 'I hear there's some secret with the grave...'
      Rune: 'This seems like a planned turn of events. Ha, ha!'
      Chaz: 'Hmm...'


After finding the Landale

      Rune: 'Well, we've found the 'Landale' so we'll just have to go to
             Kuran.'
      Rika: 'Finally!'
      Wren: 'Let's immediately identify reason the environmental control
             system is out of control.'
      Chaz: 'OK. Let's go!'


After defeating Dark Force on Kuran

      Rika: 'We've beaten Dark Force, but Dezolis is not recovering!'
      Raja: 'I've been telling you, the problem lies in the Garuberk
             Tower!'
      Wren: 'In any event, I do acknowledge there's a need to investigate
             Dezolis.'
      Rika: 'Well, what are we waiting for? Let's go!'
      Chaz: 'Hmm...the Garuberk Tower...'


After Raja leaves the party

      Rika: 'Apparently Kyra from Esper has headed towards the Garuberk
             Tower alone!'
      Rune: 'I've heard the forest surrounding the tower is full of
             carnivorous trees.'
      Chaz: 'We can't let her go alone. We've got to go help her!'


After Kyra joins the party

      Kyra: ''Lutz' should know a way to defeat the carnivorous trees!'
      Chaz: 'Hmm...the chief of Esper.'
      Kyra: 'I'll try to ask for an audience with 'Lutz!' Let's go to the
             Esper Mansion! It's southeast of the town of Meese!'


After gaining access to the inner sanctuary

      Kyra: 'Let's hurry to the mansion. We should find Lutz there. Oh...
             it's like a dream...I'm going to meet Lutz...What shall I do?'
      Chaz: 'Er...'


After meeting Lutz

      Chaz: 'So, Rune was Lutz!'
      Rune: 'We'll need the 'Eclipse Torch' to defeat the carnivorous
             trees! We'll have to hurry and head for 'Gumbious Temple' west
             of the Esper Mansion! Chaz! Stop walking so slow! Argh! You
             and your short legs!'
      Kyra: 'Waaa. This is upsetting the image I had of Lutz!'


After the Eclipse Torch is stolen

      Rika: 'Someone has taken the Eclipse Torch!'
      Chaz: 'Who are those guys? What's the 'Air Castle'?'
      Wren: 'At any rate, let me look at it through the sensors of the
             Landale.'
      Chaz: 'Does that mean we should go back to the airport?'


After finding the Air Castle

      Chaz: 'So, this is the Air Castle? Looks foreboding!'
      Kyra: 'Oh. If you're scared, just walk behind big sister Kyra!'
      Chaz: 'Oh, be quiet!'
      Rika: 'giggle'


After defeating Xe-A-Thouls

      Rune: 'We've beaten the trio. Now all we have to do is to find the
             boss and retrieve the Eclipse Torch!'
      Chaz: 'What do you mean boss?'
      Rune: 'Well...'


After defeating Lashiec

      Chaz: 'We've finally retrieved the Eclipse Torch!'
      Wren: 'You did well, Rika.'
      Rika: 'Thank you, Wren.'
      Kyra: 'Now we needn't be afraid of those carnivorous trees
             surrounding the Garuberk Tower!'


After destroying the carnivorous trees

      Rune: 'The holy light of the Eclipse Torch has burnt away the
             carnivorous trees.'
      Chaz: 'That means we can finally enter the Garuberk Tower!'
      Kyra: 'All right! People of Meese, just you wait...'


After defeating Dark Force in the Garuberk Tower

      Chaz: 'We've beaten Dark Force, so what on earth are those pillars of
             fire?'
      Rika: 'It's in the direction of the Gumbious Temple!'
      Rune: 'At any rate, let's go see!'


After acquiring the Hydrofoil

      Chaz: 'We've got a hold of the Hydrofoil.'
      Rune: 'This means, we can cross whatever we want, be it an ocean or a
             lake!'
      Rika: 'Now we'll be able to get to the Soldiers' Temple from Krup.'


After Seth joins the party

      Chaz: 'Oh, great. Now we've got some strange guy tagging along with
             us.'
      Seth: 'Oh, don't mind me.'
      Rune: 'Chaz! Let's hurry to the temple.'
      Chaz: 'Ah...yeah.'


After acquiring the Aero-Prism

      Rune: 'So this is the Aero-Prism.'
      Seth: 'My...quite a curiosity. May I take a look at it?'
      Chaz: 'Oh, here...'
      Rune: 'No, don't, Chaz! Sorry about this, Seth, but this thing is
             very important to us. We can't just have it handled out of
             curiosity!'
      Seth: 'Oh, forgive me.'
      Rika: 'Oh, Chaz! You're just too good-natured!'
      Chaz: 'So, where is this secret of Rykros hidden?'


After defeating Dark Force outside Soldiers' Temple

      Rika: 'Seth, he is such a...'
      Rune: 'I'm becoming indifferent about enemies. We can't just dawdle.
             We've got to hurry to Rykros!'
      Chaz: 'So what is this Rykros?'
      Rika: 'We'll just have to go and find out, Chaz!'


After speaking to Le Roof

      Rika: ''The Courage Tower' and 'The Strength Tower'...'
      Rune: 'We won't be able to hear what Le Roof has to say unless we
             meet the guards of these two towers...'
      Chaz: 'He said it was some kind of ritual... I wonder what that
             means?'


After passing Le Roof's trials

      Chaz: 'Rune... what on earth are you saying is in the Esper Mansion?'
      Rune: 'Just be quiet and follow me!'
      Chaz: 'sigh'


After reaching the Sacred Sword Cave (MUMBL)

      Chaz: ''Meet Elsydeon...' I wonder what that means?'


After acquiring Elsydeon

      Chaz: 'What on earth are you saying has happened in Motavia?'
      Wren: 'The details are unclear. There is nothing other than the
             message 'come immediately'..'


After returning to Motavia

      Chaz: 'This is the final battle! Let's go! We must face The Profound
             Darkness!'
      Rika: 'Yes, Chaz, let's go and do battle together!'
      Wren: 'Operational checks complete. All systems are fully
             functional.'
      Rune: 'So...let's go!'

   Plus- Hahn: 'Now is the time that I can profit from my secret training!'
      or Gryz: 'Just what we're hoping for!'
      or Demi: 'Allow me to join you. Let's go now!'
      or Raja: 'I'll protect Algo -- and drink all I want! Ha ha!'
      or Kyra: 'I'm ready anytime you are!'


On the top floor of Anger Tower (MUMBL)

      Chaz: 'What! Where is this?... Where is everybody!?'
```
