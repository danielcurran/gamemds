# 12. Abilities - General

<a id="s12-1"></a>

## 12.1. Combinations

> 🥉 **Inventory, Attack!** — Use a non-consumable item in battle to affect your enemies, except Psycho Wand. _(RetroAchievements · 2 pts)_
> 🥉 **Inventory, Assist!** — Use a non-consumable item in battle to support your allies. _(RetroAchievements · 2 pts)_
> 🥉 **Our Powers, Combined!** — Execute any Combo. _(RetroAchievements · 3 pts)_
> 🥈 **Combo Strategist** — Execute 3 different Combos in a single battle, and win. _(RetroAchievements · 10 pts)_
> 🥉 **Just As Planned!** — Execute a Combo in a Macro. _(RetroAchievements · 3 pts)_
> 🥉 **Elemental Weaknesses** — Execute Combo: Blizzard on Motavia, or Fire Storm on Dezolis. _(RetroAchievements · 3 pts)_
> 🥉 **Sudden Death** — Execute any instant-death Combo. _(RetroAchievements · 5 pts)_
> 🥉 **Triple Threat** — Execute Combo: TriBlaster. _(RetroAchievements · 5 pts)_
> 🥉 **Ultimate Destruction** — Execute Combo: Destruct. _(RetroAchievements · 5 pts)_
> 🥈 **Macro Strategist** — Execute 4 different Macros in a single battle, and win. _(RetroAchievements · 10 pts)_

```
There are fourteen combination attacks in the game, even though the game
manual says there are fifteen. I will not say that I have been unable to
find the fifteenth, as I have tried every possible combination of Techs
and Skills there is. Quite simply, there are only fourteen combinations.

I haven't combined Alys with any character after Rika, or combined Hahn,
Gryz or Demi with most of the later characters, or combined Raja, Kyra or
Seth with anyone except Chaz, Rika, Rune and Wren, but that is because the
game story prohibits this. Therefore, the only possibilities are that the
manual is simply erroneous and that there never were fifteen combos, the
fifteenth combo uses two characters that cannot be in the party at the same
time (such as the Alys/Seth scenario above), the fifteenth combo involves
some specific equipment arrangement or Level requirement (in which case
there is a fifteenth combo but I haven't found it yet), or there were
originally fifteen combinations but either a required Technique, Skill or
the whole combo was removed (without changing the manual).

Combinations are very powerful attacks that are created by two or more
characters using specific abilities together. You may have noticed that I
have used no combinations in the walkthrough, as they are not essential to
winning battles. If you wish to use combos instead of standard abilities,
feel free to do so. They are usually far more powerful than the sum of
their parts, but their unpredictable success rate makes them a risky choice
in battle. For a combination to work, all of the characters involved must
be set to act in succession. In other words, a combination attack will not
work if any other character or enemy acts between the characters involved
in the combination. 


E.g. Your party consists of Chaz, Alys, Hahn and Rune, and they are
     fighting two Sand Newts and a MonsterFly. You set Alys to use FOI,
     Chaz to use TSU, Hahn to use WAT, and Rune to defend, in the hope of
     creating the Triblaster combination. The following examples below
     demonstrate the results of different orders of actions:
     (C=Chaz, A=Alys, H=Hahn, R=Rune, S=Sand Newt, M=MonsterFly)

     1. R - def, M - att, A - FOI, S - att, S - att, C - TSU, H - WAT
     2. A - FOI, R - def, M - att, C - TSU, H - WAT, S - att, S - att
     3. R - def, M - att, C - FOI, A - TSU, S - att, H - WAT, S - att
     4. M - att, R - def, S - att, CAH - Triblaster, S - att


As you can see, some of the combinatios may be very difficult to get to
work under normal circumstances. However, setting up a Macro will increase
this chance. If, using the example above, you had set up a Macro like the
one below, Rune would still act first, then some of the enemies may attack,
and then it will get to Hahn. Now, as Hahn is normally slower than Chaz,
Chaz should follow him immediately. The same principal goes for Chaz and
Alys. Technically, this should create a Triblaster nearly every time.


Rune - def, Hahn - WAT, Chaz - TSU, Alys - FOI


Now that I've explained how to increase the chance of a combination attack,
I will explain each of the combinations in detail. The method of creating
each combination is given after the combination name, and uses different
symbols to indicate how to create it:

/ - Separates different Techs or Skills that can be used as substitutes
+ - Indicates that the Techs or Skills can be combined in any order
> - Indicates that the Techs or Skills must be combined in the given order


All Combinations affect all enemies on the screen, and it should be noted
that items cannot be used as substitutes for the Techniques and Skills
required (e.g. you could not use a FLAMESWORD in place of FLAELI to create
the Fire Storm combination).
Black Hole - GRA/GIGRA/NAGRA + NEGATIS
(Destroy)    Requires Rune and Kyra

             By combining a powerful gravitational field with NEGATIS'
             life-sapping effect, a black hole is formed that sucks all
             enemies from the screen, causing instant death to all.
Blizzard   - WAT/GIWAT/NAWAT + ZAN/GIZAN/NAZAN/HEWN
(Water)      Requires Chaz/Alys/Hahn and Rune, or Chaz/Alys and Hahn

             One character creates a series of ice shards, which are then
             flung around by multiple whirlwinds to form a tempest of cold
             razors. This Combo is useful early in the game.
CircuitBrk - HIJAMMER + TANDLE
(EMP)        Requires Rune/Kyra and Wren

             This Combo involves Wren sending out an EMP, that is then
             boosted by TANDLE to utterly destroy electrical circuitry.
             Ultimately, it causes instant death to all machines.
CondctThnd - WAT/GIWAT/NAWAT > TANDLE
(Electric)   Requires Hahn then Rune, or Rune then Kyra

             The enemies are covered in water, which is then used to
             conduct the lightning effect of TANDLE directly into each
             enemy. It will come in handy in the later hi-tech dungeons.
Destruct   - DEBAN > MEGID + LEGEON + POSIBOLT
             Requires Rika then Chaz and Rune and Wren

             Rika throws up a shield to protect the party, as the other
             characters combine the power of their most powerful abilities
             to create a devastating explosion. This causes 999 HP damage.
Firestorm  - FOI/GIFOI/NAFOI/FLAELI + ZAN/GIZAN/NAZAN/HEWN
(Fire)       Requires Chaz/Alys/Hahn and Rune/Kyra, or Chaz/Hahn and Alys

             A super-hot blaze is created, which is formed into massive
             tornados by the other character. These vortices of burning
             death are very helpful in the early stages of the game.
GrandCross - CROSSCUT + EFESS
(Light)      Requires Chaz and Rune

             Chaz performs two consecutive strikes, as Rune channels his
             powerful spell through Chaz's blade to wash all enemies in a
             wave of light. This Combo is useful against the Darkness.
Holocaust  - SAVOL + DIEM
(Death)      Requires Hahn and Rune

             Hahn uses his primary instant-death Technique, while Rune
             strengthens the effect of each energy field with DIEM. This
             is almost guaranteed to instantly kill all organic enemies.
Lethal Img - ILLUSION + DEATH
(Death)      Requires Alys and Rika

             While Rika disorientates the enemies with ILLUSION, Alys can
             perform her instant-death Skill with the intention of hitting
             all enemies. This has a very good chance of instant-death.
ParadinBlw - RAYBLADE + ASTRAL
(Energy)     Requires Chaz and Hahn

             Chaz's blade, already infused with energy from the RAYBLADE
             Skill, is further enhanced by Hahn's simultaneous astral
             energy. This is very useful against the legions of the Dark.
PurfyLight - HOLYWORD + EFESS
(Cleanse)    Requires Rune and Raja

             As Rune calls for the heavenly light to strike all enemies,
             Raja uses the prayer of his HOLYWORD through the light to hit
             all enemies, instantly killing enemies weak against light.
ShootinStr - BURSTROC > FOI/GIFOI/NAFOI/FLAELI
(Fire)       Requires Wren then Alys/Rune/Kyra

             Wren launches his blazing missiles on the enemies, while the
             other character enhances them with a fire-based ability. This
             burning rain is very useful against cold enemies.
SilentWave - AIRSLASH + PHONON
(Force)      Requires Chaz and Demi

             As Chaz performs his wide sweep through the enemies, Demi
             channels her PHONON through his weapon to inflict more direct
             damage. This is a generically useful combination.
Triblaster - FOI + WAT + TSU (cannot use advanced versions of these Techs)
(Radiation)  Requires Chaz and (Alys/Kyra and Hahn/Rune, or Hahn and Rune)

             The three characters pool their power to create a beam of
             combined elements that sweeps across all enemies. Useful early
             in the game, this Combo becomes less valuable towards the end.
```
