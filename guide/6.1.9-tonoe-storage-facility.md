### 6.1.9. Tonoe Storage Facility

> 🥉 **Gryz** — Recruit Gryz. _(RetroAchievements · 5 pts)_

```

   Enemies: Abe Frog, Blob, Carrion Cr, Toadstool
  Treasure: 100 MST, MONOMATE, 500 MST, 300 MST, ANTIDOTE,
            TITN-CROWN, ESCAPIPE, ALSHLINE
Head up a little, then right through the gap in the wall. Then, go all the
way up to the top of the room and pick up 100 MST. Return back to the
stairs then take the left opening and follow this all the way to the top to
collect the MONOMATE from the left corner. Go back to the entrance and then
follow the central passage all the way to the top and go down the stairs.

Head left then turn down, and take the first junction to the right. Head
into the first room along the bottom wall and pick up 500 MST from the
lower-right corner. Leave the room, then continue to the right and enter
the next room along the bottom wall. In here, take the 300 MST near the
left wall. Exit the room and go all the way left back to the first passage.
Go down and then right, and take the stairs at the end.

Go left at the junction, then turn up at the corner. Ignore the door to the
first room and the passage to the right, and go into the room that follows.
Pick up the ANTIDOTE from the lower-right corner then go back left, down,
and right along the passage you skipped just now. At the next junction,
go up a little, then right into the room. Retrieve the TITN-CROWN from
here, then equip it to Alys immediately. Go back to the entrance to this
room, then go all the way down to the first junction near the stairs. Head
right here, until you find the next staircase going down. Take it.

Go up then left, and you will see a door against the top wall. This door is
sealed, so continue left into a stone tunnel. Follow the tunnel left, up,
right, and then down into the room behind the sealed door. There are three
chests here. The top-right one is open, so open the one beneath it to
obtain an ESCAPIPE. Finally, open the left-most chest to acquire the
ALSHLINE and begin a short cut-scene.

Alys takes the medicine, after which Gryz requests joining the group, as it
turns out that his father and mother were killed by Zio when his village
was burned down. Chaz points out that they are not necessarily going after
Zio, to which Gryz brings up what Rune said earlier. Either way, Gryz wants
to go, as he is beginning to get bored of staying in Tonoe. Alys agrees,
and Hahn impatiently suggests they leave.

You have two options now - you can use the ESCAPIPE you just collected (or
HINAS if Chaz has learnt it) to leave the basement, or you can make your
way back to Tonoe on foot. Either way, try to get everyone up to at least
Level 9 if they are not already there.
```
