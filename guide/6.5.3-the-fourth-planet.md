### 6.5.3. The Fourth Planet

> 🥉 **Towering Silence** — Investigate the Tower of Silence. _(RetroAchievements · 3 pts)_

```
Starting Party: Wren , Chaz , Rika , Rune
Starting Level: 39   , 41-42, 44-45, 38-39
You are in a building known as the Silence Temple. Head down from the Regen
Platform and out into the corridor. Continue a little further down until
you reach the junction. The exit from the Silence Temple is below you, but
there are a few items to collect first. Head left, up left and then up, and
pick up the GUARDSHILD in the small room that follows. Next, go up, right,
up and then right into another room, and collect the GUARDSWORD and a
TELEPIPE. Equip this brilliant new sword to Chaz immediately, then continue
right, down, right and then down. Procure a TRIMATE from this last room,
then follow the path down, left, down and then finally left back to the
exit of the temple. Go down and out of the Silence Temple.


Enemies on Rykros (in a vehicle or out): CrminHeads, Cula-Bellr, DeathBearr
The traversible area of Rykros is very small, and contains only four areas
that can be entered. You can go to the Strength Tower and the Courage Tower
in either order you wish, as they will both need to be completed. They are
very similar in nature, and each have exactly the same enemies inside. The
major difference between them is the fact that they have very different
guardians, and because I feel the guardian of the Strength Tower easier
than the guardian of the Courage Tower, I have put them in that order in
the walkthrough.

Before you visit either tower, however, it would be best to get a few EXP
to tip the odds in your favour. Spend a little time on the surface of
Rykros getting Chaz up to around Level 43, Rika up to at least Level 46,
Rune up to Level 40 and Wren up to around 40 or 41. Take note that you will
only receive half as many EXP for defeating an enemy in a vehicle than you
would for defeating the same enemy on foot. Return to the Silence Temple
when you are finished to regenerate the party. 
If you wish to follow the walkthrough exactly and tackle the Strength Tower
first, head directly northwest from the Silence Temple until you find a
secluded area containing red rocks. The largest and most central of these
rocks contains a door at the front. This is the Strength Tower. Save your
game outside, then go in. Follow '6.5.4. Proving Your Strength'.
If you wish to try things a little differently and tackle the Courage Tower
first, go directly east from the Silence Temple until you hit a wall. From
here, head due south and you should spot a large purple rock with a door at
the front, just a little to the east. This is the Courage Tower. Save your
game outside, then go in. Follow '6.5.5. Proving Your Courage'.
```
