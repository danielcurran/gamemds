### 6.2.7. A Tectonic Diversion

> 🥈 **Tectonic Tech** — Solve Plate System's earthquake malfunction. (Missed upon leaving Motavia.) _(RetroAchievements · 10 pts)_
> ⚠ **Missable** — must be completed before leaving Motavia (6.2.12). Shut down the Plate System in section 6.2.7 before launching to Zelan.
> 🥉 **Initiate: Phononmezer** — Recover the Phononmezer parts in Plate System. _(RetroAchievements · 5 pts)_

```
Starting Party: Demi , Gryz , Chaz , Rika
Starting Level: 13   , 16   , 16   , 15-16
 // DUNGEON #9  ¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯\
|/  Plate System           \_________________________________
   Enemies: Gunner Bit, Loader, Seeker, Slave, Worker Pod
  Treasure: REPAIR-KIT, CRMC-ARMOR, TITN-GEAR, PHONON (Skill),
            LASR-AXE, LASRBARRIR, LASR-KNIFE, STUN-SHOT
Head up the passage, and turn right at the junction. Head down the short
passage and pick up the REPAIR-KIT from the end. Ignore the passage leading
up as this is a dead end, and head all the way to the left along the
corridor. Go up and use the elevator.

Go left and then up the thin corridor, then go to the right side of the
large room you come to. Head along the passage to the right, then take the
upper path at the junction. Walk up to the upper left corner and procure
the CRMC-ARMOR. As noone else can use this, equip it to Demi. Go back down
and then take the junction below you, continuing to the bottom to get the
TITN-GEAR. Again, equip this to Demi. Head back up and then return to the
large room, Make your way across the room, take the passage on the left,
then head up and then right and take the elevator to the next floor.

Head down and then left at the junction. When you reach the left wall, go
down the passage, ignoring the path to the right, and then turn right at
the bottom. Descend in the elevator. Go left and then all the way up to
the top, then go a little to the right and open the chest.

Chaz states that the chest is full of weird machines, and Demi points out
that they are internal weapon units for androids. She asks for everyone to
look away, then the screen goes black. A few upgrade noises later, and Demi
is fully equipped with her new weapon, Phononmezer.


Demi acquires the PHONON Skill
Return to and use the elevator, the go back up to the passage until you
reach a path to your right. Follow this all the way to the right until you
hit the wall, then head down. Turn left at the bottom and descend to the
next floor in the elevator.

Head a little right and then all the way up to the top of the passage,
then go left. Turn down at the corner and pick up the LASR-KNIFE on the
right. Head over to the left of this area and collect the LASR-AXE. Make
sure you equip this to Gryz, as it is his best weapon so far. Now, go
right, down then right and pick up the LASRBARRIR. With all of these new
possessions, follow the bottom wall to the left and take the elevator down.

Head right, ignoring the junction above you, and continue to the end to get
the STUN-SHOT. Hand this to Demi, then return to the junction and walk up
the passage. Examine the panel on the top wall.

Demi accesses the panel and shuts down the controls for the Plate System.
This makes Gryz quite happy, but Demi points out that it hasn't solved the
problem. They still need to shut down Nurvus, and perhaps even Zelan.

Now you are done here, RYUKA, TELEPIPE or walk out of the Plate System, and
return to Monsen to recuperate.
```
