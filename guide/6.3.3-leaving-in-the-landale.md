### 6.3.3. Leaving in the Landale

> 🥉 **Space Sledding** — Discover the Landale. _(RetroAchievements · 3 pts)_

```
Starting Party: Wren , Chaz , Rika , Rune , Raja
Starting Level: 20   , 20-21, 20-21, 21-22, 25
 // DUNGEON #12 ¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯\
|/  Hanger                 \_________________________________
   Enemies: Gerotlux, Helex, Snow Slug
  Treasure: DIMATE, ZIRC-GEAR
This is a relatively short dungeon for a change. Head down and then left at
the corner, then continue left by the top wall until you come to a small
chamber at the end. Pick up the DIMATE just to the left of the pool, then
go back to the right and head downwards a little way. Head through the
passage to the right and follow it until the end to acquire the ZIRC-GEAR.
Equip this to Wren, then return to the main passageway. Continue heading
down until you reach the bottom wall, then go left and down around the
corner that follows. Continue down and exit through the door at the bottom.

You have found the space ship, and Chaz is quite impressed. Rune takes a
closer look and realises that it is called the Landale. Raja tries to make
a funny remark, but Chaz ignores him and asks Wren about its condition.
Wren replies that it has been preserved almost perfectly, and that it will
only take a little maintenance to be up and running. Chaz tells Wren to
prepare for take-off, and the next thing you see is a Spaceport (identical
to the one on Motavia) rise from the ground just to the southwest of Tyler.

Take the party to the Spaceport and use one of the Regen Platforms, then
check the party's status. You ideally want everyone to be up to at least
Level 22 before taking the Landale to Kuran, so spend some time in the area
around Tyler to acquire enough EXP to achieve this. If you want more of a
challenge and more EXP you can head through the mountains north of Tyler.
The enemies there are more powerful.


Enemies in the area north of Tyler: Biter Fly, Gerotlux, Helex, Red Mole,
¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯  Skytiara, Snow Slug, Snow Worm


Once everyone is up to a minimum of Level 22 and you have made sure that
they are all equipped with the correct gear, you are almost ready to leave.

Get a good night's rest at the Inn in Tyler, and buy a good supply of items
from the Tool Store. You'll want to have at least five or six DIMATEs and
an ESCAPIPE just in case. Don't bother getting any ANTIDOTEs or CURE-PARALs
as you won't need them on Kuran.

When you are completely set, head into the Spaceport, use a Regen Platform,
then head up and enter the Landale. Select Kuran from the destination list
on the right, and watch your new spaceship fly to the next dungeon.
```
