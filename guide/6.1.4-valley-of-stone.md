### 6.1.4. Valley of Stone

```
Starting Party: Alys , Chaz , Hahn
Starting Level: 7    , 4-5  , 4-5
 // DUNGEON #2  ¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯\
|/  Birth Valley           \_________________________________
   Enemies: FlattrPlnt, Xanafalgue, Zoran Bult
  Treasure: CRBNSHIELD, MONOMATE, CRBN-SUIT
Head up and then turn right at the first junction. Follow this path to the
end and collect the CRBNSHIELD. Don't bother equipping it, though. Return
to the junction and then head up, ignoring the passage on your right to
pick up the MONOMATE at the end. Now go back and take the right passage.
Continue along this path, then turn up then left and go downstairs.

Go up, and take the right passage at the junction. Go up at the turning and
collect the CRBN-SUIT. Equip it to Hahn, as he has the lowest DFS at the
moment. Go down then all the way to the left, then turn up at the end. Go 
past the statues and address the stone man blocking the door at the end.
This is Professor Holt, and he will begin a cut-scene.

Hahn seems shocked by the Professor's petrification, and asks if there is
any way to undo what has been done. Alys then speaks of a medicine called
Alshline, that is supposed to turn stone to flesh. She informs them that it
used to be available at the Motavian town of Molcum, far to the south.
Hahn then suggests they set off, and Alys is nice enough to set the price
at 500 Mesetas. Hahn is not too pleased.


500 Mesetas gained
```
