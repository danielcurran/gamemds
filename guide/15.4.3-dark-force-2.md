### 15.4.3. Dark Force 2

```
Met: Garuberk Tower             Recommended Level before fighting:
  HP: 7524                         Chaz: 38-39       Wren: 36
 EXP: 46383                        Rika: 40-41       Kyra: 36-37
 MST: 1                            Rune: 35


In the first round, get Chaz to use his RAYBLADE, let Rika use SANER, have
Rune fire his sole LEGEON, get Wren to set up a BARRIER, and have Kyra use
WARLA. This will set up the party for the following rounds, ensuring Dark
Force acts last, and also scratch the surface of Dark Force's HP total.

Dark Force has a different range of abilities this time, including a Skill
that sends a character to Sleep (EVIL EYE), a very damaging beam that hits
one character (SHDWBREATH), and a powerful hit-all attack (LIGHTSHOWR).
Hopefully, Wren will be able to set up his BARRIER before Dark Force uses
SHDWBREATH or LIGHTSHOWR, or you may find that you receive a crippling
amount of damage (including the possibility of immediate flat-lining).

In the following rounds, keep Chaz using RAYBLADE (or NATHU when he runs
out), let Rika use either SAR or GISAR following a LIGHTSHOWR (don't use
NASAR as it will not be necessary at this stage of the game), or GIRES or
NARES following SHDWBREATH, have Rune use EFESS (or NAWAT once the supply
is depleted), get Wren to attack or use RECOVER if needed, and let Kyra use
NAFOI or provide some additional healing. If the organic members of your
party do not need healing, have Rika add to the offence by using DBLSLASH.

If any of the party fall, follow the usual fast character revival and slow
character healing strategy, or use a SOL-DEW if you need to revive quickly.

Try not to let a character's HP stay below half their maximum total, as
there is the possibility that a single attack from Dark Force may knock
them down. Also, if any of the characters succumb to Dark Force's EVIL EYE,
let wake up naturally (don't use Rune's AROWS as this will waste his turn).

Stay offensive and heal quickly, and you'll find that the battle will be
over in around eight to ten rounds.
```
