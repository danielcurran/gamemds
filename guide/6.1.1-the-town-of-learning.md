Speaking to people will yield a lot of information about the current
situation, although none of it is essential. You'll generally find out a
bit more about the monster threat, a village called Mile, problems with
the planet, and general information about the history of the Algo system.
If you try to enter the academy basement (the middle door on the ground
floor) you will be stopped by a student walking back and forth and talking
to himself, so just head to the principal's office behind the middle door
on the top floor. Address the principal to begin a cut-scene.

The principal explains that the academy basement has been taken over by
monsters, and that he wishes Chaz and Alys to destroy them. When asked how
the monsters got into the town in the first place, the principal takes on
a different tone, shouting that he doesn't know and that they should just
get on with it.

When they leave, Alys tells Chaz that she thinks the prinipal was acting
very suspiciously, although Chaz feels he may just be afraid of the
monsters. Make your way downstairs, then through the door behind the
statue to find yourself at the entrance to the basement. Speak to the
student guarding the stairs to begin another cut-scene.

The student, whose name is Hahn, requests that he goes with you,
explaining that his mentor, Professor Holt, went missing while researching
a place called Birth Valley. He also says that after this event occured,
the principal barred anyone from entering Birth Valley, and then the
monster scare began. He feels there is a connection, and wants to find the
truth, but was waiting for Chaz and Alys to arrive as he didn't want to go
into the basement alone. Alys lets him tag along for 100 Mesetas.
