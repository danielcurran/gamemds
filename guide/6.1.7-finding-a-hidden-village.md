### 6.1.7. Finding a Hidden Village

```
Starting Party: Rune , Alys , Chaz , Hahn
Starting Level: 17   , 7-8  , 6    , 6
 // DUNGEON #3  ¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯\
|/  Valley Maze            \_________________________________
   Enemies: Blob, Carrion Cr, Sand Newt
  Treasure: ANTIDOTE, 300 MST
Go up, turning right then up, ignoring the passage on the left and taking
the door above. Go up and collect the ANTIDOTE by the pool. Return back to
the junction and turn left, continuing to the end before going up and
through the door.

Head up and then left, and take the second turning up. The first turning
just leads to a dead end. Go through the door, and keep going up until you
reach a junction, where you should turn right. Go up at the turning, then
through the door. Head to the top end of the room, round the pool, and
pick up 300 MST before returning to the junction.

Continue left until the end, then go up through the door. Now, simply walk
all the way up to the top and through the door to leave this place. Don't
bother increasing your levels just yet. You'll see why in a moment.

Back outside, head up to the village of Tonoe.
```
