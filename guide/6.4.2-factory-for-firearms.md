### 6.4.2. Factory for Firearms

> 🥉 **Initiate: Burst Rockets** — Recover the Burst Roc parts in Weapons Plant. _(RetroAchievements · 5 pts)_

```
Starting Party: Wren , Chaz , Rika , Kyra , Rune
Starting Level: 26-27, 27-28, 29   , 27   , 27
 // DUNGEON #16 ¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯\
|/  Weapons Plant          \_________________________________
   Enemies: Arthropod, LifeDeletr, Servant, Silvalt, Star Drone,
            Wiredine
  Treasure: BURSTROC (Skill), PLSMLAUNCH, ELST-GEAR, ELST-ARMOR,
            REPAIR-KIT (infinite supply)
As soon as you enter, Rika asks what the place is, to which Wren replies
that it is a weapons plant that Mother Brain built a thousand years ago. It
was soon realised to be an unnecessary facility and was shut down, but
within the last six months it has begun manufacturing automatic weapons due
to orders from Kuran. Chaz asks if they should shut it down, but Wren tells
him that Zelan stopped the production once it was back in control. He sees
no problem with leaving the weapons plant it as it is.

Ignoring Wren's advice, head up across the room and take the elevator at
the top. Go right a short way and descend in the next elevator nearby. Go
left along the corridor and then up at the corner, turning right at the
top. Keep going to the right and then use the elevator in the upper wall.

Head to the left along the walkway, ignoring both the paths above and
below you, and continue to the left until the end. Walk up until you reach
a junction. The conveyor belt above is rolling against you, so take the
one to the right. From the end, head upwards and use the computer terminal
at the top to reverse the belt direction. Go back down and then left across
the conveyor, then take the conveyor above. At the top, go all the way to
the right, ignoring the first elevator, and take the next elevator to the
floor above.

Walk left, down, right, down and then left, and head up at the corner to
open the chest at the end. As usual, Chaz doesn't have a clue what the
things are inside, but Wren informs him that it is the Burst Roc unit, a
long range semi-automatic weapon that fires heat energy bursts. He installs
it. (Notice how he is less bashful than Demi at install weapon units).


Wren acquires the BURSTROC Skill
Return all the way round to the elevator, then descend back to the level
below. Head back to the left, and take the elevator you ignored earlier. In
this next area, go right, down, left, down and then right to the corner.
Walk up and then a little right, and enter the elevator in the top wall.

Head to the right, then step on the platform to be taken down to a small
walkway. Step onto the next platform just to the right, and then get off at
the end. Go to the left and use the elevator. Walk a little down, then
follow the path left, up and then right, and obtain the PLSMLAUNCH from the
end. This is far more powerful than Wren's PULSEVULCN, but only hits one
target, so don't bother equipping it yet. Return back left, down and right
then take the elevator back up.

Go left and use the two sets of platforms to return to the top, then head
left along the walkway to another platform on the left. Take it down, then
walk a little to the left and take the next platform to the bottom. Go
left, then descend in the elevator nearby.

Walk a little down, then go right and up, ignoring the empty chest to your
left and instead turning right at the junction. In the small room that
follows, pick up the ELST-GEAR and the ELST-ARMOR, and equip them both to
Wren straight away. Finally, if you feel you need some more REPAIR-KITs,
examine the computer panel between the two chests. You will obtain a
REPAIR-KIT. Examine it again and you will get another REPAIR-KIT. You can
do this as many times as you want, up until you can't carry any more.

There is nothing else to do in the Weapons Plant, so use HINAS or an
ESCAPIPE, or make your way out of the place on foot. Also make sure to give
the SILVR-TUSK back to Rika once the party are out.
```
