### 6.2.2. The Parmanian Escape

> 🥉 **A Doomed Vessel** — Inspect the computer system in Wreckage. _(RetroAchievements · 5 pts)_

   Enemies: Tracer, Warren286, Whistle
  Treasure: CRMCSHIELD, 1500 MST, DIMATE, CRMC-KNIFE, CRMC-MAIL
You are in some sort of ruined building from the same era as the Bio-Plant.
Head up and then left, and then up again at the turning. Go left at the
next junction then up towards the door. Instead of going through the door,
however, turn right and follow the path down and right to go through the
next door. Collect the CRMCSHIELD and 1500 MST from this room then go back
to the first door again, this time heading through it.

Go up and take the turning on the left. Continue left, picking up the
DIMATE from the alcove along the way, then head upwards and take the
elevator to the floor below. Follow the walkway all the way to the left,
then turn upwards and acquire the CRMC-KNIFE at the junction. Equip this
to Hahn, replacing his TITNDAGGER. Turn right, then go up all the way to
the door, ignoring the path on the left. Go in and retrieve the CRMC-MAIL,
which you should equip to Gryz. Return to the walkway on the left that you
passed a moment ago, and follow this left then up and then left again
before descending in the elevator.

Head left along this passage and then go up and through the door. Continue
up along the walkway, and then all the way to the left and then upwards at
the corner. Go through the door, along the corridor, then through the next
door. Go up and you will see an array of panels. Examine them, and Rika
will point out that she knows what it is, and begin fiddling with the
controls. She breaks the security code, switches on the computer, and
begins a short cut-scene.

An image of some biospheres appears, and Rika says that they are on a ship
that crashed here from Parma a thousand years ago. Just before the
satellite Gaira crashed into Parma in AW 1284, causing it to explode, the
ship had escaped with some Parmanians. Unfortunately, the ship was caught
in Motavia's orbit, and while trapped in space all of it's passengers died.
As the orbit decayed, the ship grew closer and closer to the planet, until
it finally crashed. Rika then finds out that there were, in fact, dozens of
ships that escaped from Parma. Some of them landed on Motavia, some on a
planet called Dezolis, and the others just disappeared into space.

Once you have control again, you may as well leave. Use an ESCAPIPE or
HINAS if you wish. Now, you'll want to get Chaz, Alys, Hahn and Gryz up to
at least Level 12 and Rika to at least Level 9. Pop into Nalya and have a
look around if you wish. This will be useful if you ever want to teleport
here later. Once your done, return to Aiedo to pick any remaining equipment
and fully restore yourselves. When ready, leave Aiedo and go through the
trees to the north to enter the cave behind.
