### 15.3.4. Carnivorous Trees

**Met:** South of Garuberk Tower · **HP:** 240                          N/A · **EXP:** N/A · **MST:** N/A

There are three Carnivorous Trees. Individually, they are not particularly dangerous enemies. The only thing they do each round is attack (sometimes inflicting Poison). However, each time a Carnivorous Tree is defeated it is replaced with a new one. This cycle never, ever ends, and eventually the party will be wiped out. Unlike all other boss battles, you can run from this battle, and this is exactly what you should do. Don't do anything in the first round except escape. There is no point fighting at all, as you cannot possibly win. Just run.
