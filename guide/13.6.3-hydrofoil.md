The Hydrofoil is the weakest of all three vehicles. Its primary function is
to allow the party to cross the water on Motavia, but it can also cross
land (including quicksand). It cannot dig through ice. It is about twice as
fast as the other vehicles, meaning it is the quickest mode of transport
other that teleportation. On the down side, the Hydrofoil is the least
useful in battle, taking more damage and dealing less. This, coupled with
its low SP, makes combat in the Hydrofoil a little risky. Unless traversing
water, it is best to use the Ice Digger.
