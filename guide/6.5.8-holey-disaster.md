### 6.5.8. Holey Disaster

> 🥈 **Towering Anger** — Prove yourself in the Tower of Anger. _(RetroAchievements · 10 pts)_

```
Starting Party: Wren , Chaz , Rika , Rune
Starting Level: 42-43, 45-46, 48-49, 41-42
The next thing you see is Chaz emerging from the cave with Elsydeon. The
rest of the party are still there, and Rune says that Elsydeon promised it
would give Chaz its power and that there was no mistake in his judgment.
Rune then tells Chaz that they should go and save Algo, and Chaz agrees
unhesitatingly, adding that they will protect all life on Algo and free the
world of terror once and for all. Suddenly, Wren reports that there is an
emergency, saying that Demi has informed him that a disaster has just
erupted on Motavia. Chaz proposes that they hurry to Motavia.


Rika rejoins the party

Rune rejoins the party

Wren rejoins the party
¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯

You are in Lutz's room at Esper Mansion. Leave the room and then teleport
back to Tyler or make your own way back to the village. 

Head into the Spaceport. The party will automatically head up, and find
that Raja and Kyra are there to meet them. Chaz asks if Raja is ok now, and
also acknowledges Krya. Raja jokes that a minor sickness like that couldn't
keep him in bed, and asks to go with them. Krya also informs the party of
her wish to accompany them, to which Chaz thanks both of them. The group
then head off to Motavia in the Landale.

Upon arrival, the group are welcomed by Hahn, Gryz and Demi, who were
waiting for them at the Spaceport. Chaz acknowledges them all, and Hahn
notes that Chaz seems to have become stronger since they last met. Gryz
says that he was scolded by Pana, but he came anyway, and Demi reports that
the system is finally stabilized, so she can go with them. Chaz thanks
everybody, and Wren asks Demi what the disaster is. Demi informs the party
that a large hole suddenly appeared north of Piata, and that life around
the area has started dying. She adds that there are no gas or radiation
emissions coming from the hole, but Rune knows it to be a very powerful
Black Energy Wave. He surmises that it must be coming from The Profound
Darkness itself, and that it must have finally opened the door from the
other dimension.

Hahn tells Chaz that they should go to fight the last battle, and Kyra adds
that she would like to go with them too, pointing out that they need taking
care of by their big sister. Chaz announces that, since they are all there,
he would like all of them to go, but there is a problem. Rune adds that
there are only five 'Rings of Stars' to protect them from the power of the
Darkness, and Chaz tells them that they will only be able to choose one
person to take.

Control passes back to you, and before you decide on your final companion
it is worth noting that you will now be able to acquire for Chaz the most
powerful Technique in the game - Megid. If you wish to obtain it, see '7.2.
Megid - The Anger Technique' for more details. This will also allow you to
obtain the final item of equipment for the party.
```
