### 6.1.2. Cleaning the Cellar

> 🥉 **Hahn** — Recruit Hahn. _(RetroAchievements · 1 pts)_
> 🥉 **Academy Infestation** — Solve the monster situation in the academy's basement. _(RetroAchievements · 5 pts)_

```
Starting Party: Alys , Chaz , Hahn
Starting Level: 7    , 1    , 1
 // DUNGEON #1  ¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯\
|/  Academy Basement       \_________________________________
   Enemies: Xanafalgue, Zoran Bult
  Treasure: MONOMATE, 100 MST, ANTIDOTE
 \
  \   Boss: Igglanova
   \______________________________


Go down, then see a little pink monster scurry off to the left. Turn left
and then almost immediately up to go into the room and collect a MONOMATE.
Go back down to the passageway, then continue left and then up past the
bookshelf. Turn left, then go down and into a room to pick up 100 MST.
Go back up then all the way right and down the stairs.

Go down out of the room, then go left and then up into a large room. Head
to the left side and pick up the ANTIDOTE before returning to the passage.
Go left until the end, then down the stairs. You will be in a room filled
with strange capsules. Go up and meet the first boss of the game.

 __________
| BOSS #1  \____________  ____________________
| Igglanova             ||                    |
|¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯|| Recommended Level: | 
|  HP: 300              ||                    |
| EXP: 173    MST: 54   || Alys: 7           /
|                       || Chaz: 2-3        /
| Weak: -               || Hahn: 2-3       /
| Res:  -               ||                /
| Imm:  -               ||               /
|                       ||              /
To defeat the Igglanova quickly, have Alys use her VORTEX skill and Chaz
and Hahn attack in the first round. The Igglanova will spawn a Xanafalgue
every round until there are two on the screen, at which point it will
attack, so make sure that Chaz attacks the Xanafalgue each round and keep
having Alys use VORTEX. Hahn should be attacking whatever necessary
(usually Igglanova) or using RES if anyone needs healing (which shouldn't
happen). Igglanova should go down in four or five rounds.

Alternatively, to gain a lot of experience quite quickly at this stage of
the game, have Alys defend every round and Chaz and Hahn hack away at
Xanafalgues. Let Hahn use RES when anyone gets low on HP, and just keep
going until you get fed up, at which point use VORTEX to finish off the
Igglanova. How long you do this, if at all, is up to you.
Once the battle is over, Alys will ask Hahn why all the capsules are
there, and he will state that he doesn't know. They decide to see the
principal, so return up the stairs and make your way out of the basement.
Go back up to the top floor of the academy and visit the principal's room.
Speak to the principal to begin another cut-scene.

Alys will ask him why all the capsules are there, and after a little
persuasion he tells her. Apparently, the new breed of monster that had
emerged in the area originated from Birth Valley, so Professor Holt went
to investigate. The principal tells Alys that Holt had found evidence of
an ancient and advanced civilization, and he brought back the breeding
capsules to be studied before setting out to Birth Valley again. But this
time he didn't return. Hahn asks why the principal didn't send a rescue
team, so the principal replies that he was visited by a man who called
himself Zio.

A flashback then ensues, where you see Zio warning the prinicipal not to
send anyone else to Birth Valley, on pain of being turned into stone. Hahn
then resolves to go to Birth Valley, and Alys kindly informs him that it's
egg-laying season for sand worms. She agrees to accompany him for a
further 300 Mesetas. Hahn then says that Birth Valley is to the northeast.


300 Mesetas gained
```
