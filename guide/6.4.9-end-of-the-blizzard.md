### 6.4.9. End of the Blizzard

> 🥈 **Purified Planet** — Dispel the evil of Garuberk Tower. _(RetroAchievements · 10 pts)_
> 🏅 **Limit: Dark Force II** — Defeat Dark Force II. Combined level limit: 140, party of 5. _(RetroAchievements · 25 pts)_

```
Starting Party: Wren , Chaz , Rika , Kyra , Rune
Starting Level: 36-37, 39   , 41-42, 36-37, 36
Following Dark Force's second annihilation, the Garuberk Tower shatters
before your very eyes. Rune tell's Chaz that they've done it, and Rika
announces that the storm is over. The sky clears and Rune notes that the
Black Energy Wave has stopped. He also guesses that the patients in Meese
will already be recovering. Kyra sighs that it's over, and thanks the party
for all their help, realizing it was presumptious of her to think she could
do it alone. She decides to return to Meese to help with the work that's
still to be done and bids farewell to Chaz, embarrassing him by saying that
he's like a baby brother. She then says goodbye to Lutz, commenting that he
wasn't as she expected but he lived up to his name. Kyra then leaves.

Chaz is still dwelling on being called Kyra's baby brother, and wonders if
he is still so undependable. Rune reassures him that he has improved, but
that Chaz is still a lot like him. The two of them joke around a little,
when suddenly there is a huge explosion beyond the mountains. Rika realizes
that it is Gumbious Temple. Chaz is confused, noting that they have beaten
Dark Force, and Rune suggests they find out what's going on.


Kyra leaves the party

the blizzard is over, this village has been partially rebuilt. Go in.
```
