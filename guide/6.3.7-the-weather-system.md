### 6.3.7. The Weather System

> 🥈 **Weather Watch** — Investigate Climate Control for any weather malfunctions. (Missed upon the snowstorm ending.) _(RetroAchievements · 10 pts)_
> ⚠ **Missable** — must investigate Climate Control BEFORE clearing Garuberk Tower. If the snowstorm ends, this is permanently missed.
> 🥉 **Alt-Transform-Delete** — Defeat a Lifedeletr that combines from Wiredine + Arthropod in Weapons Plant. _(RetroAchievements · 5 pts)_

You have to fight this guardian as soon as you enter the Control Centre.
Technically, this guy isn't really a boss this time, as the music is just
normal battle music. However, the battle is unavoidable and inescapable, so
I thought it should be categorized as a boss battle. Besides, you only have
to fight the Gy-Laguiah twice in the entire game, and this is his final
chance in the spotlight, so I figured that he should go out in style.

As the Gy-Laguiah is no different from his debut fight, this battle should
be very easy. Don't bother with defensive preparations. Just have Chaz use
GITHU, Rika use DBLSLASH, Rune use NAWAT, Wren use FLARE, and Raja use RES
on the character hit in the round before. Ignore Wren getting damaged, as
the Gy-Laguiah will not be able to kill him before it is defeated.

Without needing to deviate from this pattern, the battle will conclude
after three or four rounds.
Wren will announce that they are in the control system for the Dezolis
climate, and Chaz notes that the monsters have been making a nest. Wren
says that, as they suspected, the centre must be the cause of the abnormal
weather conditions, but Raja is adamant that it is the Garuberk Tower.

Head up the passageway until you reach an elevator, but instead of going in
it, head right until you reach the wall. Walk up and then head right at the
top, turning down at the corner that follows. In the room at the bottom,
collect a REPAIR-KIT, then head back up and left to the elevator shaft in
the centre of the room. Continue up the corridor until you reach the very
top. Turn left at this junction, and then head down at the corner. Continue
down the corridor, all the way to the bottom, and pick up the PULSEVULCN.

Equip this new hit-all weapon to Wren, then head back up and right to the
junction. Ignore the path to your right as it leads to a dead end, and go
back down around the central column. Take the elevator.

Walk all the way down the walkway until you reach a junction at the bottom.
Turn left, ignoring the elevator fot the moment, and walk left and then up
at the corner. Turn right when you need to, then go up to the end of the
walkway to procure 5000 MST. Return down, left, down and right to the
elevator you passed on the way, then ascend in it back to the surface.

Head a little left and pick up the COMPO-GEAR, then equip this to Wren. Go
back to the elevator and take it to the floor below. Turn right to the
junction, then head right and take the elevator here. Walk right in the
small corridor and enter the elevator in the upper wall on the right.

Head left along the walkway, turning up when you reach the corner. Keep
going up, then go left and then up again. When you reach the choice of the
wide walkway to the left and a thin path to the right, take the thin path
and then head up to the end to obtain another REPAIR-KIT. Go back down and
then left, and keep heading left until you reach the hole in the walkway.
Walk up from here and enter the elevator in the top wall.

Ignore the path to the right as it leads to a dead end, and instead follow
the line right and downwards, continuing down until you reach the bottom.
Head right and then up, still following the line, and descend in the
elevator at the top. Now, go down to the junction and turn left, then pick
up the COMPO-ARMR from the small alcove at the end. Replace Wren's old
ZIRCOARMER with this new protective outfit, then return to the junction and
head up, near to the elevator. Next, check your characters' Level status.
Chaz and Rune should be at least Level 27 by now, Rika is likely to be at
least Level 28, Wren should be around Level 26 or 27, and Raja should be
at Level 29. If this is not the case, spend a moment getting those last few
EXP for the characters falling behind.

Go around the elevator to either the left or the right, then walk up the
passage the is above you. There will be a blue-cloaked figure in front of
the computer panels, but don't approach him yet. First, fully heal your
party and check your recently acquired items are all equipped properly,
including replacing Wren's PULSEVULCN with the trusty NAPALMSHOT and
swapping one of Rika's THUNDRCLAWs with her SILVR-TUSK. Also, change the
PLSM-SWORD in Chaz's possession for the FLAMESWORD.
