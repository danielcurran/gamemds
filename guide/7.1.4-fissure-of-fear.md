### 7.1.4. Fissure of Fear

> 🥈 **HG: Fissure of Fear** — Complete 'Fissure of Fear' in the Hunter's Guild. _(RetroAchievements · 10 pts)_
> 🥈 **Limit: Fissure Fract Ooze** — Defeat the Fract Ooze in 'Fissure of Fear' before finding Wren. Combined level limit: 55, party of 3. _(RetroAchievements · 10 pts)_

```
Recommended Level:  19+
When Available:     After shutting down Nurvus
Commission Awarded: 5000 MST
This is a request from Eddie who lives in Monsen.

"My son has fallen into a crack in the earth!
 Please help my son!"

That is the message.


After accepting this job, teleport to Monsen and visit the house directly
north of the crack in the ground. Speak to the woman inside, and she will
inform you that her son, Tallas, was playing outside when he fell through
the crack. She wants to go and rescue him, but tells the party that creepy
groaning sounds have been coming from the crack in the ground. She finishes
by asking the party to rescue Tallas.

Go outside and you will see that the sign has been removed from the fence.
Rest at the Inn if you need to, then save your game and drop down the hole.


  ______________
 // DUNGEON #25 ¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯\
|/  The Hole               \_________________________________
   Enemies: Jr.Ooze, Meta Slug, Zol Slug
 \
  \   Boss: Fract Ooze
   \______________________________


Chaz will be surprised that there is a vast cave underneath Monsen, an Rika
will notice that Tallas is nowhere in sight. The path to the left leads to
a staircase that takes you to the surface outside Monsen, so head right.
Turn up at the corner, then continue right and down. Examine the shoes on
the floor, and Rika will wonder if they are Tallas' shoes. Continue to the
right until you reach a junction. The lower path leads nowhere, so head up
and follow the passage up and a little to the right until you see a large
orange slug. Examine it, and Rika will become concerned about the fate of
Tallas. Chaz will mention that it is bad luck to even think such a thing,
and then the creature will attack.

You could argue that this isn't really a boss battle, as the music is just
normal battle music. However, the battle is necessary to the completion of
the mission, so I thought it should be categorized as a boss battle.
Use your best Skills and Techniques to get the battle over as quickly as
possible, avoiding light Skills as the Fract Ooze is resistant to these.
Apart from its standard attack, the Fract Ooze only uses one Skill called
CELL SPLIT, a hit-all attack that does a small amount of damage. If you
stay on the offensive, it is unlikely you will need to heal at all, and the
battle should be over in no more than two or three rounds.
Once the fight is over, Tallas will be standing where the Fract Ooze was.
He asks if they are on the other side of the planet, and Chaz asks Tallas
if he was really eaten. Rika is glad Tallas is safe, pointing out that he
would have been digested had they not come along in time. Tallas agrees,
then thanks Chaz before running off down the passage.

Use HINAS or an ESCAPIPE, or follow Tallas, heading all the way to the left
to the staircase that you ignored earlier. Go back into Monsen and return
to Eddie's house (see that the sign has been replaced). Speak to Tallas'
mother and she will thank you, saying that she had lost all hope. She tells
you that she will send the commission to the guild, so teleport back to
Aiedo and pick up your reward.
```
