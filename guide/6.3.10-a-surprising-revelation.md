### 6.3.10. A Surprising Revelation

> 🥉 **Kyra** — Recruit Kyra. _(RetroAchievements · 3 pts)_
> 🥈 **Limit: Carnivorous Trees** — Defeat 50 Carnivorous Trees without Raja or Kyra. Combined level limit: 95, party of 4. _(RetroAchievements · 10 pts)_

```
Starting Party: Wren , Chaz , Rika , Kyra , Rune
Starting Level: 26-27, 27-28, 29   , 27   , 27
Address the guards blocking the entrance to Esper Mansion, and they will
see Kyra and let you pass. Walk into the building, and head down the
corridor to the right. Turn up at the corner and then turn right again,
passing the corridor above you and going through the door at the end of the
passage in the upper wall. Pick up the LACO-ROD, RFLC-ROBE, SOL-DEW and
10000 MST from this small room, and equip the RFLC-ROBE to Rune. You can
also equip the LACO-ROD if you wish, but his two SILVSHIELDs will be more
useful in the long run.

Leave the room then go left and up the long passage you passed earlier,
until you come to two doors. Head through the door on the left and continue
up the corridor, turning left at the corner at the top. Follow the passage
a little further left and then turn down to speak to one of the guards
standing in front of the doorway below you. The guard will tell Kyra that
she may not enter without permission. She gets agitated by this but the
guard will remain constant. At that moment, Rune cuts in and demands that
the guards let them pass. The guard begins to refuse but suddenly realises
who he is speaking to. He apologises profusely to Rune, and lets the party
through. Chaz is more suspicious than ever.

Go down through the door to find yourself in a courtyard. Go either left or
right and then down around the large central building, then head up and
walk through the doorway. Speak to the old man on the left and he will
welcome Rune back, saying that he is glad that Rune is safe. Rune says that
the old man can forget the formalities, and that he is going in. The old
man complies.

Walk towards the staircase and the party will stop. Rune will explain that
they are in the inner sanctuary and that, just beyond, there is a chamber
called Lutz's room. Kyra confirms this, and then gets anxious about meeting
Lutz, asking if her clothes are clean and her hair is alright. The party
will then head down the stairs automatically, and approach a small sphere
on a table in the centre of an otherwise empty room. Kyra looks around,
wondering where Lutz is, and a cut-scene begins.

Rune explains that Lutz is not here, having left this world long ago. Wren
adds to this by saying that no human could live for two thousand years, but
Kyra doesn't accept it, telling the party that she saw Lutz with her own
eyes. She also says that she believes in the legend of the Espers and of
Lutz, and that is why everyone is trying so hard. Rune tells Kyra to calm
herself down, telling her that even if Lutz's body is no more, his spirit
lives on. He adds that before Lutz died, he left his will and memory in the
Telepathy Ball, the sphere on the table. Rika asks Rune if he is saying
that the ball is Lutz, but Rune tells her that Lutz is not there now. He
says that if the chosen person appears, the Lutz's will and memory are
transferred into that person. In a grand gesture, Rune points at his own
head, and the party realise that Rune is Lutz.

The old man appears again, and confirms this. He explains that Rune Walsh
is the fifth-generation Lutz, and Kyra is left a little bewildered. Rune
then speaks to Chaz, telling him that since ancient times there has been a
cycle where disasters occur every thousand years, due to a personification
of evil called Dark Force that appears every thousand years. He adds that,
up until now, a courageous person would defeat the evil and bring peace
back to the world. Rika points out that they definitely did defeat Dark
Force on Kuran, but that the abnormal conditions are still continuing.
Rune goes on to say that Algo was shaken a thousand years ago by the
explosion of the planet Parma, upsetting the delicate balance they have
tried so hard to maintain. He surmises that this may be the cause. Chaz
asks Rune if that means that Dark Force is still alive. Rune replies that
doesn't know, but he is certain that the root of all evil is not yet
destroyed, and it must be defeated.

Rune then tells Chaz that he has chosen him as the soldier to fight the
final battle, explaining that he sensed from the start that Chaz had great
potential. In a more light-hearted manner, Rune apologises for keeping it a
secret all this time, and jokingly asks if they'll keep on cooperating.
Chaz, a little confuses, sort of agrees, then Rune announces that they have
to go to the Garuberk Tower. The old man then says that there is a forest
of carnivorous trees surrounding the tower (which you already know), but if
they use the 'Eclipse Torch' they will be able to destroy it. Rune then
states that their next destination is the Gumbious Temple, and the old man
tells them that it can be found in the valley to the west. Rune tells Chaz
to stop looking so dazed, and Kyra expresses her disappointment that Lutz
would be so insensitive. Rune apologises, telling her that he's always been
like that, but it doesn't seem to make Kyra any happier.

After that extensive and quite illuminating cut-scene, you finally have
control of the party again. Ascend the staircase out of Lutz's room (no
form of teleportation works in this room - it must be magically protected).
You can't use HINAS or an ESCAPIPE here as it isn't a dungeon, but a quick
way to get out of Esper Mansion is to use RYUKA or a TELEPIPE and select
EspMansion as the destination. Your next task is now set.
```
