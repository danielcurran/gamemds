## 10.2. Heal Techniques

```
There are four small groups of Heal Techniques. The first group heals an
individual character and the second group heals all characters. Status Cure
Techniques remove various status effects, and Revival Techniques restore a
character from a flatlined state.

RES   - Usable by Chaz, Hahn, Rika, Raja and Kyra.
¯¯¯     Also usable by Tech User.
3 TP
        This is very useful early in the game, as it saves using MONOMATEs.
        It restores HP based on the user's MTL score.
        Can be used anywhere. Only works on organic life.


GIRES - Usable by Chaz, Hahn, Rika, Raja and Kyra.
¯¯¯¯¯   Also usable by SoldrFiend and TechMaster.
6 TP
        A more powerful target curative Tech, helpful later in the game.
        It restores HP based on the user's MTL score.
        Can be used anywhere. Only works on organic life.


NARES - Usable by Chaz, Hahn, Rika, Raja and Kyra.
¯¯¯¯¯
9 TP    The most potent of the group, it proves invaluable near the end.
        It restores HP based on the user's MTL score.
        Can be used anywhere. Only works on organic life.


SAR   - Usable by Rika and Raja.
¯¯¯     Also usable by TechMaster.
12 TP
        A very important Technique in some of the earlier boss battles.
        It restores HP to all, based on the user's MTL score.
        Can be used anywhere. Only works on organic life.


GISAR - Usable by Rika and Raja.
¯¯¯¯¯   Also usable by Dark Witch and Radhin.
24 HP
        An expensive Technique, it should only be used when necessary.
        It restores HP to all, based on the user's MTL score.
        Can be used anywhere. Only works on organic life.


NASAR - Usable by Rika and Raja.
¯¯¯¯¯
36 TP   The most powerful heal-all Tech. Essential in later boss battles.
        It restores HP to all, based on the user's MTL score.
        Can be used anywhere. Only works on organic life.


ANTI  - Usable by Chaz, Hahn, Raja and Kyra.
¯¯¯¯
2 TP    A very helpful Tech throughout the game, especially when there is
        little space to carry ANTIDOTEs. It completely cures Poison.
        Can be used anywhere.


RIMPA - Usable by Chaz, Hahn, Raja and Kyra.
¯¯¯¯¯
8 TP    A rarely used but specifically-functioned Technique, it may come in
        handy when least expected. It completely removes Paralysis.
        Can be used anywhere.


AROWS - Usable by Rune and Raja.
¯¯¯¯¯
9 TP    Exceptionally valuable considering it has no item counter-part,
        this is very useful later in the game. It awakens all characters.
        Can only be used in battle.


REVER - Usable by Chaz, Rune an Raja.
¯¯¯¯¯
12 TP   When MOON-DEWs are hard to come by, this Tech is very useful early
        in the game. It revives a character and restores 1/4 maximum HP.
        Can be used anywhere. Only works on organic life.


REGEN - Usable by Raja.
¯¯¯¯¯
36 TP   Unique to Raja, this valuable Technique revives a character and
        restores all HP, and also removes all status effects.
        Can be used anywhere. Only works on organic life.
```
