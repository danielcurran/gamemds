### 15.2.5. ChaosSorcr

```
Met: Shuttle                    Recommended Level before fighting:
  HP: 460                          Chaz: 19-20    Wren: 20
 EXP: 18380                        Rika: 19-20
 MST: 1200                         Rune: 21-22


A break in the pattern of increasing boss difficulty, the ChaosSorcr is
exceptionally easy to defeat. The ChaosSorcr will either use a laser beam
attack, Flaeli, Hewn or ShadowBind (a target-all Skill that has a small
chance of reducing the Agility of each character). Have all the characters
use their standard attacks, and the ChaosSorcr will go down in the second
round, probably only getting the chance to act once in the entire battle.
```
