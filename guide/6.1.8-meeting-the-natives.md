Go to the Inn and refresh yourself, then unequip Rune completely. He won't
be needing his things for a while. Head to the east side of town, then
enter a hut north of the stalls. Speak to the old Motavian sitting on the
chair in the middle. Different music will begin and he will introduce
himself as Grandfather Dorin. He will ask if you wish to know the secret of
refining titanium. Reply that you don't. Also reply 'no' to whether you
wish to know why the path to the village is blocked by a rock. However,
when Grandfather Dorin asks if you wish to know Alys Brangwin's chest
measurements, say 'yes'. Alys will then punch Dorin, and threatens to punch
him again when he sees Rune and asks for his help. A cut-scene then begins.

Rune begins by requesting an item he had left there and asking Dorin about
Alshline. Dorin replies that they can take whatever they need, and that
it's in the warehouse basement out back. They're about to leave when Dorin
warns them it might be a tad risky, so he asks Gryz to accompany them. He
agrees, and then Rune bids farewell, telling Chaz not to get any silly
ideas about taking on Zio by himself. This surprises the group, as the idea
of Chaz fighting Zio hadn't even been brought up. Alys then thanks Rune,
and he leaves.

As you make your way around the stores, you should buy a TITN-SWORD, two
TITNSLASHRs. two TITNDAGGERs, a TITN-HELM and two TITN-MAIL. It is unlikely
you will be able to afford all these at the moment, as they come to a total
of 4570 Mesetas. Even with selling the STEL-SWORD, two HUNT-KNIVES, two
SLASHERs, CRBN-HELM, LTHR-BAND, two CRBN-SUITs and WHITEMANTL (which you
should do), you'll still need another 3265 Mesetas to buy all this
equipment. If you can't afford it yet, buy the remaining equipment later.
Don't sell the WOOD-CANE, as this weapon can be used in combat as the RES
spell, which saves TP.

Before heading onto the next chapter, go back to Valley Maze and get Chaz,
Hahn and Gryz up to Level 8 or even Level 9, and take Alys up to around
Level 9 ot 10. Once you are done, return to Tonoe and buy whatever
equipment you couldn't buy before, then head past Grandfather Dorin's hut
and around to the west until you find another hut. Enter here, then
examine the trapdoor at the top. Gryz will open the door, after which you
should head on into the basement.
