### 7.1.5. Stain in Life

> 🥉 **HG: The Stain in Life** — Complete 'The Stain in Life' in the Hunter's Guild. _(RetroAchievements · 5 pts)_

```
Recommended Level:  N/A
When Available:     After obtaining the Hydrofoil
Commission Awarded: 50000 MST (-50000 MST costs = 0 MST profit)
This is a request from the village chief of Uzo via letter transmission.

"My two daughters have left the village and have not returned since.
 Please find out where they are..."

That is the message.


Before you accept this job, take note that you will need 50000 MST in order
to complete it. Although you will get this money back, you will not get
anything else.

After accepting this job, teleport to Piata and get into the Hydrofoil.
Head north and then to the west end of the land, then go due south. You
will come to an island, and on this island is the village of Uzo. Drive in.

Uzo          Inn             |    80 MST per person|
                             |                     |
             Tool Store      |TRIMATE   |   400 MST|
                             |ANTIDOTE  |    10 MST|
                             |CURE-PARAL|   120 MST|
                             |TELEPIPE  |   130 MST|
                             |ESCAPIPE  |    70 MST|

Go to the house in the southwest corner of the village, and speak to the
woman at the back of the house. She informs you that their two daughters
made a raft and went to the mainland to do some shopping at the market.
However, that was a month ago, and there has been no sign of them. She is
not even sure whether they made it to the mainland or not. The woman then
explains that her husband makes out that he is stubborn and obstinate, but
he is really more worried than she is. She begs the party to find their
daughters.

There are only two places that have anything like a market on Motavia -
Tonoe and Aiedo. Of course, Tonoe is hidden within a mountain range and
protected by a monster-filled cave, so Aiedo seems like the most likely
location for the girls to be found. Teleport back there.

Head up to the market complex, but don't go in. Instead, head right and
then up around the building. Continue up the path to the river, then go a
little to the left and cross the bridge. Head up through the gate and into
the building. This is Aiedo prison.

In the left-most cell you will see two girls. Speak to the one on the left,
and she will tell you that they came to Aiedo from far away to do some
shopping. Unfortunately, they got a little carried away, were unable to pay
for their rooms, and were subsequently caught. She then finds out that the
party are there at the girls' parents' request, but points out the watchman
that is nearby.

Speak to the watchman on the left, and he will inform you that the girls
have a ten-year sentence. The seem to ask if the watchman can let them out,
but he tells them that he can only do that if they post the girls' bail.
However, the bail is 50000 Mesetas. Speak to the man again and he will ask
if you want to pay the bail. Agree, and the man is amazed by the party's
magnanimity, then he unlocks the girls' cell.

Speak to the girl on the left again, and she will also be amazed. Chaz then
suggests that they all go back to Uzo, so that's what they do. The girls'
father thanks the party, and tells them that he will send the commission to
the Hunters' Guild as promised. He will then express his gratitude once
more. Teleport back to Aiedo, and reclaim your expenses from the guild.
```
