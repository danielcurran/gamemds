Head through the door to the left of the Armour Store, and you'll find
yourself in Hahn's parents' house. Apparently, his father owns the store,
and he is annoyed with his son because Hahn wastes his time studying when
he should be helping with the family business. Now you see why Hahn wanted
to avoid this place.

Now go to the building at the north side of town. Enter, and you will find
yourself in a classroom. Hahn races over to the front and embraces the
teacher. This is Saya, and it turns out that she is Hahn's fiancee. A
conversation ensues between everyone, after which you may leave.
There is little else to do here, so buy any MONOMATEs and ANTIDOTEs you
feel are necessary (and an ESCAPIPE may come in handy too), then rest at
the Inn. When you are ready, leave Krup.


Enemies between Krup and Valley Maze: Crawler, Locusta, Mini Worm,

Head northeast along the coast, until you see a cave entrance to the
north ahead of you. Enter this location, and you will find the party
standing outside of a tunnel blocked by rocks. Rune steps out, and with a
raise of his arm and the word 'Flaeli', a fireball shoots from his
fingers and blasts to rocks to pieces. Rune calls this technique 'magic'.
Save your game, then enter.
