## 16.2. Motavia

```
Motavia is the second planet of the Algo system, and also the largest. It
is composed of roughly equal areas of land and water, the majority of the
land being sand-covered. It is a naturally hot planet, but suffers greatly
after the environmental control systems go awry. With poor soil and
inhospitable climate conditions, the Motavia seen at the beginning of the
game is not an easy place in which to live.

The indiginous sentient beings on Motavia are the aptly-named Motavians.
These blue, fur-covered humans seem poorly designed for life in a hot
climate, but it seems that their fur must act to reflect heat rather than
insulate. Since the arrival of the Parmanians a thousand years ago, the
Motavian population has dwindled, and now there is only one small village
remaining. They are a strong, proud race, and understandably fearful of
their less furry neighbours. They are also noted for their knowledge of
refining titanium.

Motavia is home to a number of towns and villages, mostly Parmanian, and
also houses a fair few secrets. The game begins on Motavia, and is the main
area of the game for a long while.
```
