### 15.5.3. Sa-Lews

```
Met: Courage Tower              Recommended Level before fighting:
  HP: 5499                         Chaz: 43-46       Wren: 40-43
 EXP: 35000                        Rika: 46-49
 MST: 1                            Rune: 40-42


The guardian of the Courage Tower, Sa-Lews is a master of magical combat.
In the first round, have Chaz use CROSSCUT, get Rika to use SANER, let Rune
use LEGEON, and get Wren to set up his BARRIER.

Sa-Lews will use a number of Rune's Skills, including FLAELI, HEWN, TANDLE
and LEGEON. As three of these are hit-all Skills, you may be in for a tough
time in this battle.

In the following rounds, let Chaz continue using his CROSSCUT, get Rika to
use GIRES or GISAR (you may need to use NASAR following some of Sa-Lews
attacks), get Rune to use LEGEON (or NAWAT if this runs out), and keep Wren
attacking (or use RECOVER if he is low on HP).

As usual, have Rika perform DBLSLASH if there is no organic healing to be
done, and revive fallen characters with the fast-slow method, a SOL-DEW or
a REPAIR-KIT (for Wren).

Follow the strategy above, and Sa-Lews will concede in around seven or
eight rounds.
```
