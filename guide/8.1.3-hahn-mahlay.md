Hahn is with the party until Alys falls sick, but he will return again near
the end of the game. He is a regular Parmanian, and the son of a blacksmith
in Krup. He grew up in the village, and began to learn his fathers' trade,
but he soon realised that working as a blacksmith would be a waste of his
true talent. The thing was that Hahn was far more intelligent than most,
and his thirst for knowledge was the cause of his decision to go to Motavia
Academy and become a scholar. This made Hahn's father furious, and he
threatened to disown Hahn if he left. Alas, Hahn's heart was already set,
and he left for the Academy.

During the years that he spent at the Academy, Hahn fell in love with a
school teacher in Krup, a girl named Saya, and they became engaged. A short
while after this, the monster scare takes place, and Hahn's part in the
game begins.

Hahn is a highly intelligent man, capable of reasoning a situation instead
of becoming emotional. He also has a very inquisitive nature, wishing to
understand the world around him, but fortunately he does not take any risks
in this search. This is demonstrated when he first meets the hunters, as he
balances his drive to learn with realising the dangers it may pose. Even
with his great intellect, Hahn is sometimes a little naïve. He trusts
others quite readily, and Alys takes advantage of this fact.


Hahn's favoured weapons are dagger, and he wears mainly bands and light
armours.

Hahn has the widest selection of Techniques, including curative Techs,
water, force and death Techs, and hindrance Techs.

His Skills include divining, evasion, and causing instant death.
