## 1.1. Foreword

```
Welcome to the Phantasy Star IV walkthrough and strategy guide.

Why, you may ask, write a walkthrough for a game that was released ten
years ago? Well, the answer is quite simple. This was the first console-
based RPG I ever played, and I loved every minute of it. After playing the
other games in the series, I came to the conclusion that The End of The
Millennium is the best of them all, and worthy of my time and effort to
fully flesh it out. This guide contains no reference, apart from the one
above, to any of the other Phantasy Star games. Although Phantasy Star IV
does refer to many elements from previous games (it is a sequel after all),
this guide is not written for those games, and any links are ignored. I
may, in the future, compose guides for the earlier games in the series, but
that remains to be seen.

This guide contains everything you will need to make your way through the
game, including all of the side areas. The first part of the guide contains
general playing information, including explanations of the controls, layout
and mechanics of the game, and a section to explains the terms used. After
this is the full walkthrough. I have split the walkthrough into acts, each
covering the main stories of the game, and these are split into chapters
that outline each separate mission. The idea of this is to make finding a
specific area that little bit easier. Finally, there are a number of
sections that go into great detail on the characters, abilities, items,
enemies, places and all the other aspects of the game. This reference area
is about as exhaustive as it can be, and will hopefully answer any specific
question you may have about the game. There is no FAQ section in this guide
at the moment, which is hardly surprising as noone has asked me anything.

As far as its completion is concerned, this guide is almost there. There is
only one thing I have yet to finish, and that is the Character Statistics
section. Once this is completed, I will update this guide. Even without the
last few statistics (which you don't really need anyway), this is probably
the best guide you will find for this game anywhere. If you disagree, and
feel that there is anything that could be improved, please let me know. I
will gladly do whatever is required to make this guide flawless, and credit
those that aid me in the task.

Ready? Then let's begin!
```
