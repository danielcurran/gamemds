### 6.5.4. Proving Your Strength

> 🥈 **Towering Strength** — Prove yourself in the Tower of Strength. _(RetroAchievements · 10 pts)_

```
Starting Party: Wren , Chaz , Rika , Rune
Starting Level: 40-42, 43-44, 46-48, 40-41
 // DUNGEON #21 ¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯\
|/  Strength Tower         \_________________________________
   Enemies: Bladeright, BloodSaber, CrminHeads, Dark Witch,
            Hakenleft, Illusionst, Le-Faw-Gan, Twinarms
  Treasure: MOON-DEW, GUARD-ROD, GUARD-CLAW, GUARD-CLAW, MOON-DEW,
            PALMA-RING, MOTA-RING, DEZO-RING
 \
  \   Boss: De-Vars
   \______________________________


Head straight up, ignoring the paths to the left and right, and stop just
before the staircase. Go along the small passage to the left, just above
the stairs, and pick up a MOON-DEW. Return to the right and ascend the
staircase to the next floor. Go left and take another staircase, then head
down to the bottom of the corridor. Ascend the staircase to the right.

Go right along the corridor, then turn up and then right and acquire the
GUARD-ROD. Equip this to Rune if you want, but I would advise keeping the
shields. Go back left and down, then head left. Walk past the staircase and
go up, left and then up again, and turn right at the top corner. Head up
and then right again, and follow the thin passage at the top to the right
to obtain a GUARD-CLAW. Replace Rika's LACO-CLAW with this final model,
then head back left and down, and return down the stairs.

Head back up and take the next staircase to the floor below. Take the path
down between the staircases, and turn right at the bottom. Go up at the
corner, then take the thin passage to your left to get another GUARD-CLAW.
Don't equip this to Rika, as her SILVR-TUSK is far more useful. Instead,
go back to the right then up through the gap in the wall, and ascend the
stairs to your right.

Walk left to the wall, then go up and left and collect the MOON-DEW from
the alcove, then head back right and then down all the way to the bottom.
Continue left and then up, and take the next staircase on the left. Go
right and down, then climb the final stairs to the top floor.

When you arrive, you will see two Gi-Le-Farg witches attacking a large
fiery being. The witches are dispatched quite easily, and control returns
to you. Heal the characters, then equip Wren's best single-target weapon
(PLSMLAUNCH or PHOTNERASR). Speak to the fiery being, and it will inform
the party that they must pass his test. This begins a boss battle.

 __________
| BOSS #16 \____________  ____________________
| De-Vars               ||                    |
|¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯|| Recommended Level: | 
|  HP: 5499             ||                    |
| EXP: 35000  MST: 1    || Wren: 40-43       /
|                       || Chaz: 43-46      /
| Weak: -               || Rika: 46-49     /
| Res:  -               || Rune: 40-42    /
| Imm:  Fir,Frc,Grv,Lgt ||               /
|                       ||              /
The guardian of the Strength Tower, De-Vars is a master of physical combat.
In the first round, have Chaz use RAYBLADE, get Rika to set up DEBAN, let
Rune use LEGEON, and get Wren to just attack normally (a BARRIER would be a
waste of time, as De-Vars doesn't use magical attacks). De-Vars will either
perform a standard attack or unleash a hit-all Skill called DISRUPTARM.

In the following rounds, let Chaz continue using his RAYBLADE (or NATHU if
this is exhausted), get Rika to use GIRES or GISAR (you won't need more
powerful healing than this), get Rune to use LEGEON (or NAWAT if this runs
out), and keep Wren attacking (or use RECOVER if he is low on HP).

As usual, have Rika perform DBLSLASH if there is no organic healing to be
done, and revive fallen characters with the fast-slow method, a SOL-DEW or
a REPAIR-KIT (for Wren).

Follow the strategy above, and De-Vars will concede in around seven or
eight rounds.
De-Vars will admit that the party is better than he thought, and find this
fact comforting. He will allow you to take some objects, stating that they
are the proof that the party needs. Three chests then appear below.

Collect the PALMA-RING, MOTA-RING and DEZO-RING, and equip these to Wren,
Rika and Rune, respectively. Also, replace Wren's PLSMLAUNCH or PHOTNERASR
with the PULSEVULCN again. Now, use HINAS or an ESCAPIPE, or make your way
out of the Strength Tower on foot. Head back to Silence Temple and into the
main chamber. If you have already completed the Courage Tower, follow the
walkthrough from '6.5.6. Genesis of Algo'. Otherwise, head up and use the
Regen Platform to restore the party, then leave the Silence Temple.

Next, go directly east from the Silence Temple until you hit a wall. From
here, head due south and you should spot a large purple rock with a door at
the front, just a little to the east. This is the Courage Tower. Save your
game outside, then go in. Follow '6.5.5. Proving Your Courage'.
```
