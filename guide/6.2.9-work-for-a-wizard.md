### 6.2.9. Work for a Wizard

> 🥈 **Psychic Nullification** — Recover the Psycho Wand. _(RetroAchievements · 10 pts)_
> 🥉 **Rune's Reunion** — Find Rune again. _(RetroAchievements · 3 pts)_
> 🏅 **Limit: Gy-Laguiah** — Defeat the Gy-Laguiah in Ladea Tower. Combined level limit: 65, party of 5. _(RetroAchievements · 25 pts)_

```
Starting Party: Demi , Gryz , Chaz , Rika
Starting Level: 14   , 17   , 17   , 17
 // DUNGEON #10 ¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯\
|/  Ladea Tower            \_________________________________
   Enemies: Centaur, FlyScreamer, Haunt, Ripper, ShadowSabr
  Treasure: DIMATE, STAR-DEW, ESCAPIPE, FRADEMANTL, STAR-DEW
            1000 MST, PSYCO-WAND
 \
  \   Boss: Gy-Laguiah
   \______________________________


You are in a huge chamber whose sole feature is a staircase. Head up the
room until you find the stairs, then ascend them. Head through the thin
passage to the left and take the first turning above you. Walk to the end
to collect the DIMATE then go back to the passage.

Continue left until you reach the wall, then head up and take the junction
on the left. Keep walking down and to the right, then take the staircase up
to the next floor. Head around the path to the left, continuing upwards
past the alcove to the right, and collect the STAR-DEW from the top of the
passage. Now return back to the stairs and take them back down.

Head left and up to the junction, then return down and right to the area
below the first staircase. Continue along the right-hand corridor, ignoring
the passage above you and turn up along the passage at the end. Go right at
the junction and follow the path down and around to the left to take the
staircase at the end.

You will see Rune a little to the right, so go and talk to him. Rune teases
Chaz, but Chaz hasn't got time for games. He asks for Rune's help, and Rune
notices that Alys is missing. Chaz explains what has happened, and Rune
reassures him that everything will be fine once they get the Psycho-wand.
He goes on to say that the Psycho-wand is the only thing that can break
through Zio's magical barrier, and that he had come to Ladea Tower to get
it. Apparently, it is on the top level, so Rune agrees to help them deal
with Zio once they get it.


Rune rejoins the party
¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯

As Rune is not so powerful compared to the rest of the party now, keep him
at the back of the party, as he is a useful character to keep alive.

Equipment   |¯¯¯¯¯|¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯|¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯|¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯|¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯|¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯|

Follow the passage left and up, past the alcove on the left, and then head
left and down the thin corridor. Turn left at the corner and enter a small
room. Go through the passage on the left, then head up and around to the
right to pick up the ESCAPIPE. Return to the small room and take the
staircase to the next level.

Head all the way down the thin corridor below you and turn right at the
bottom. Continue left and up around the path, then take the stairs to your
left. Head left into a large chamber and retrieve the FRADEMANTL from the
centre of the room. Replace Rune's PSY-ROBE with this new armour, then go
down through the thin corridor to the left, turning left at the bottom and
then descending the stairs that follow.

Head left and then up and around to the right, and take the STAR-DEW and
1000 MST from the small alcove below you. Return back left and down to the
staircase, then go back along the right passage into the large room. Head
to the bottom of the room and take the staircase at the bottom.

The next floor is a lot more decorative. Before taking the steps above you,
fully heal your party. When you are ready, head up and approach the chest
surrounded by a blue shield. Chaz supposes that it is the Psycho-wand, so
Rune steps up and breaks the barrier. At that moment, one of Zio's servants
appears, obviously having been waiting for the Psycho-wand to be released.
The party are not going to let the monster take the wand, so battle begins.

Not a particularly difficult opponent, as both of its attacks only affect
one character. In the first round, have Chaz use GITHU (if he is not Level
17 yet, which he should be, use CROSSCUT), Gryz use WAR CRY, Rika use SANER
(to ensure that Gy-Laguiah acts last in the round), Demi use her one round
of PHONON, and Rune use GIWAT.

In the subsequent rounds, have Chaz continue to use GITHU or CROSSCUT, let
Gryz just attack, get Rika to heal whoever was attacked in the round before
(unless it was Demi), get Demi to just attack, and keep Rune using GIWAT.
If Demi is attacked, have her use RECOVER in the following round. If any of
your party fall (due to a critical strike), let Demi use MEDIC PW and
follow this up with a curative Technique.

If you keep on top of things, there shouldn't be a problem in this battle,
and it should be over in about five to seven rounds.
Walk up and procure the PSYCO-WAND to begin a cut-scene.

They have the Psycho-wand, and Gryz is confident that nothing can stop them 
now. Suddenly, Rune and Rika have a strange feeling. Rune tells Chaz that
they must go to Krup immediately. A split second later, and they're there.
```
