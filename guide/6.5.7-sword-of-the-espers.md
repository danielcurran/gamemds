### 6.5.7. Sword of the Espers

> 🥉 **Seth** — Recruit Seth. _(RetroAchievements · 1 pts)_
> 🥉 **A Hero's Sword** — Find Elsydeon. _(RetroAchievements · 3 pts)_

```
Starting Party: Wren , Chaz , Rika , Rune
Starting Level: 42-43, 45-46, 48-49, 41-42
If you wish to complete the Silver Soldier job at the Hunter's Guild,
assuming you haven't done so already, then you must do so before continuing
the walkthrough. If you do not, the job will be withdrawn.

Back at the Spaceport, use a Regen Platform then teleport to Esper Mansion.
Save your game, then enter the building. Speak to the guards to get by,
then make your way all around the mansion and into the inner sanctuary.
Descend the stairs into Lutz's room, and the party will automatically head
to the top wall behind the Telepathy Ball. Rune opens a secret door in the
wall, then a cut-scene begins.

Rune informs Chaz that the sacred sword Elsydeon awaits him. Chaz asks what
it is, and Rune tells him that it's probably the only weapon that has the
power to destroy The Profound Darkness. Rune charges Chaz to go, alone, and
meet with Elsydeon, to which Chaz asks if Rune means 'meet'. Rune confirms
this, adding that Chaz should find the answers he is looking for.

|/  Sacred Sword Cave      \_________________________________
   Enemies: Phantom
  Treasure: ELSYDEON
Head up to the top of the passage, then turn to the right. Go down the set
of steps that follows, and keep next to the left wall as you continue down
the corridor. Head a little right at the bottom and take the staircase. Go
up the passageway and up the steps to the statue on top of the raised
platform. Examine it to begin a cut-scene.

The statue is holding what Chaz supposes to be the sacred sword Elsydeon.
Chaz then hears a voice in his head which welcomes him, calling him by
name. The voice continues that Chaz is in the place where the spirits of
those who fought to protect Algo return to, and that Elsydeon is the sword
sheltered by those spirits. The voice tells Chaz to take Elsydeon is his
hands, at which point Chaz has a vision.

He sees a woman fighting what appears to be Dark Force, followed by images
of other people involved in the battles against the Darkness, and the Algo
star system. The vision ends, and the voice confirms that Chaz is the one
that Rune had selected, and that he now understands. An apparition of a
girl appears, and tells Chaz that they entrust Elsydeon to his care, adding
that all their thoughts are in the sword. The voice finishes by saying that
they will be with Chaz, always. Chaz tells them not to worry, and that they
can leave it to him.
```
