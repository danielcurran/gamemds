### 6.2.4. Castle in the Sands

> 🏅 **Limit: Juza** — Defeat Juza. Combined level limit: 45, party of 5. _(RetroAchievements · 25 pts)_

```
Starting Party: Gryz , Alys , Chaz , Rika , Hahn
Starting Level: 14-15, 14   , 14-15, 14   , 14
 // DUNGEON #8  ¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯\
|/  Zio's Fort             \_________________________________
   Enemies: Ripper, ShadowSabr, Speard, Tech User
  Treasure: 2000 MST, MOON-DEW, LASR-SWORD, DIMATE, LASRBARRIR
            LASR-CLAW
 \
  \   Boss: Juza, Zio
   \______________________________


Head up through the entrance corridor, and you will see a yellow forcefield
protecting the door to a room in the centre of this large chamber. You can
examine this 'invisible' barrier if you wish, but there is no way to get
past it at this stage. Walk to the left or right around this structure and
through the door at the top. There are two staircases here. The one on the
right doesn't lead to anything at all, so take the one on the left.

Head left and down, then turn upwards and take the staircase down to grab
2000 MST. Return up the stairs, then continue down the passage and turn
left. Drop down the large black hole here, then pick up the MOON-DEW from
the right side of the room. Exit the fort through the door on the left,
then go back into Zio's Fort and make your way back through until you are
back at the hole again. Take the stairs to the left.

Head through the opening to the right and walk through the corridor that
follows. When you are out, walk up to the top of the room and collect the
LASR-SWORD. Equip this to Chaz, then return through the corridor and go
back down the stairs.

Head right, around the bottom side of the hole, then take the thin passage
to the right. Don't bother taking the path below you, as there is nothing
there. Continue along the thin passage, ignoring the hole and the stairs
for the moment, and continue around them and down the passage. Take the
stairs at the very bottom, and then head up to obtain a DIMATE. Go back up
the stairs and up along the corridor, then drop down the hole you passed
a little while ago. Retrieve the LASRBARRIR from the left, then exit the
castle through the door on the right.

Go back to Kadary and rest up, then save and re-enter Zio's Fort. Return
all the way upstairs and back round to the hole you just dropped down, and
ascend the stairs to the right. Take the door to the left and follow the
corridor that follows until you are in another chamber. Pick up the
LASR-CLAW from the top of the room and equip it to Rika, then head down
and around to the left. Walk up the central passage until you are standing
in front of the dark-robed man. Heal up anyone who needs it, then address
the man to learn that his name his Juza and that you will need to get past
him if you intend to see Zio. A battle then ensues.

This guy is pretty tough compared to what you have faced so far. In the
first round, have Alys use VORTEX, Chaz use CROSSCUT, Hahn use WAT, Gryz
use WAR CRY and Rika use DBLSLASH. This will inflict a fair amount of
damage and increase Gryz's ATK for the rounds to come. In the rounds that
follow, use the same attack strategy. Let Gryz just attack each round, and
Chaz use TSU if he runs out of CROSSCUT.

Juza only uses magic, so have Hahn or Rika heal when a character is hit by
a targeted spell. If Juza uses ZAN, CORROSION or FORCEFLASH (especially the
latter), it may be best to let both of them heal, and maybe even get Chaz
to lend a hand if necessary. Although Gryz has the most HP at this time,
give him priority over other characters as he will take the most damage
from Juza. If you are unlucky and one of your characters falls in this
battle, get a fast character to use the MOON-DEW and a slower one to follow
this up with GIRES or a DIMATE. This should restore them back to a decent
level of health.

If you keep the pressure on Juza, and heal when necessary, this battle
shouldn't last more than four or five rounds.
After you defeat Juza, a staircase will appear to the left. Heal up if you
need to (you're not out yet), then ascend the stairs. Go down and around to
the right, and take the next set of stairs up to the top floor. Remove all
of Alys' and Hahn's equipment (you'll see why in a little while), then head
up and approach the unusual-looking object to begin a cut-scene.

Rika identifies the being as Demi, the android you've been looking for.
Alys cuts her free, and then introduces herself. Demi is very grateful, and
uses her Medical Power to heal the party before telling them that she is
responsible for controlling Nurvus. While Gryz and Hahn are awed by her
human likeness, Alys asks if Demi can stop Nurvus from supplying energy to
the system. Demi begins to explain that she had already tried to do so,
when she is interrupted by an unseen voice. Zio appears in his menacing
way, and accuses the group of breaking into his property. Chaz suggests
that Zio looks at his own actions before judging others, although Zio
doesn't seem to see anything wrong with what he has done. He taunts Gryz
a little, before Alys points out that if Zio continues to do as he has
done then the whole of Motavia could be destroyed. Zio seems pleased with
this, pointing out that his god wants there to be no life in Algo, and then
he sees red. He makes a short speech, vowing to destroy them all in the
name of his god, Dark Force, and begins another battle.

You will not win this fight, and you are not meant to. Before you get a
chance to do anything, Zio will use MAG.BARRIR, reducing any magical damage
you inflict to 1 (you won't even hit him with regular attacks).

The simplest way to get through the battle is to let everyone attack each
round (use Macro A if you haven't changed it). In the first round, Zio will
set his hands on fire (no effect), and do nothing in the second round. In
the round that follows he will summon NIGHTMARE, then use BLACK WAVE on
Chaz in the round after that. The battle ends abruptly with a cut-scene.
Alys jumps in front of the wave to protect Chaz, then Hahn and Rika suggest
they retreat. Chaz uses HINAS to escape, and they all appear outside Zio's
Fort. Rika attempts to use GIRES on Alys but it has no effect, so Hahn
decides that they should go back to his village. A RYUKA later, and the
party are back in Krup.
```
