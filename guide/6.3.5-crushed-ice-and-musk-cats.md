### 6.3.5. Crushed Ice and Musk Cats

> 🥉 **Glacier Grinder** — Use the Ice Digger for the first time. _(RetroAchievements · 3 pts)_

```
Starting Party: Wren , Chaz , Rika , Rune , Raja
Starting Level: 24-25, 25-26, 26-27, 25-26, 27-28
A little while later, Wren has done his stuff and tells the party that all
of the maintenance systems for Algo's environment are working properly
again. Everything seems to be sorted out. But wait... It appears that
Dezolis' perpetual snowstorm still hasn't stopped. Chaz asks if their
systems have been restored, and Wren confirms that they have. Raja then
claims that he told them so, and that the demons in Garuberk Tower are
causing the blizzard on Dezolis. He moans that they never listen, and Wren
points out that if the snowstorm doesn't stop then the Parmanians and even
the Dezolisians may perish. They all decide to go back to Dezolis, but
Rune points out that it is covered with walls of ice, and they won't be
able to get anywhere on the surface. Wren suggest that they can use the
Ice Digger, a car that he had purposely built in preparation for any action
required on Dezolis. He then loads it onto the Landale.

Head down to the elevator, use it, then head out of Zelan. Select Dezolis
as the destination, then sit back and wait till the party arrives. Use one
of the Regen Platforms to restore yourselves after the battle with Dark
Force, then go outside.

The ICE-DIGGER is permanently in your inventory, and you can use it at any
point outside. It has the unique ability to clear snow that lays in its
path, and this will come in handy very soon. Apart from that it functions
in exactly the same way as the Land Rover.

Before anything else, equip Wren's PULSE-LASR, replacing the NAPALMSHOT.
Save your game then get in the Ice Digger. Head a little east then north
through the gap between the mountain range above. Continue north until you
reach an ice wall, then drive through the ice northwards, keeping to the
west as much as possible. Once you come out the other side, head a little
further north until you reach a cave entrance. Get out of the Ice Digger
then go into the cave.

|/  Mysterious Valley      \_________________________________
   Enemies: HungryMole, Skytiara, Tech-Plant
  Treasure: TRIMATE, STAR-DEW, SILVR-TUSK
Head up and left, following the passage until you reach a turning to the
right. From here, head right and go past the steps above you to collect
the TRIMATE from the small area near the wall. Head back to the steps and
go up them, then head through the door above and a little to your right.

Continue upwards, keeping close to the right wall, then head right and go
up through another doorway above. Head up the short passage that follows
and turn left at the corner, then pick up a STAR-DEW from the alcove in the
top wall. Continue left and then go down, and head through the door at the
bottom of the corridor. Head left, passing around the protruding wall, and
address one of the cats guarding the next doorway. They welcome you to the
valley, and Chaz is surprised that they talk. They then explain that they
are musk cats, and hence they can talk. The musk cats say that they get a
special feeling from Chaz, and suggest he sees the old man. They then let
the party pass. Go through the door.

The next corridor is filled with several musk cats. If you speak to these
cats you will learn that they seem to have a fond remembrance of you, that
the valley is the only warm place in Dezolis, that no human has ever set
foot in the valley, and that the old man has a treasure. Go through the
door on the far right of the passageway.

Head up the passage into the large chamber that follows and speak to the
huge winged musk cat sitting on the raised platform above. He introduces
himself as the chief of the valley. He notes that there is good in Chaz's
eyes, and that he has a feeling that it is not the first time they have
met. He says he feels safe about giving something to you. It is a treasure
that has been passed down from generation to generation of the musk cats.
He then gives you the Silver Tusk.

Don't equip the SILVR-TUSK to Rika just yet, but do not sell it either.
It is far from the strongest claw in the game, but it is the most useful
against the Darkness. With your prize, use a HINAS or an ESCAPIPE or walk
out of the Mysterious Valley.
```
