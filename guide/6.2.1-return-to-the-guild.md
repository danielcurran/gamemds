Speak to the guy near the entrance and he will oddly compliment Alys before
running off. Now, go across the bridge to the left and then go into the
northern-most house on the left side of town. This is Alys' house, so you
can rest here for free. After that, it's time to go shopping again. There
are two shopping areas in Aiedo - a large market complex in the centre of
town, and an equipment store near the Hunters' Guild. The best equipment
can be found at the stores just south of the Hunters' Guild, so head south
then east across the bridge, then go up and right once you pass the wall.

The best things to buy are one CRMC-KNIFE for Hahn, a STRUGGL-AX for Gryz,
two SABER-CLAWs for Rika, two CRMC-HELMs for Chaz and Gryz, and three
CRMC-MAILs for Chaz, Alys and Rika. This will be very expensive, totalling
24300 Mesetas, although this price will be reduced to 19865 by selling the
TITN-CROWN, TITN-SWORD, TITNDAGGER, BROAD-AXE, two CLAWS, two TITN-HELMS,
two TITN-MAILs and the GRPT-SUIT. As you will have some other things to do
before setting off on the next part of the journey to Zio's Fort, buy what
you can now and wait until later to get the rest. You may have noticed that
to fully optimize the party's equipment you could buy another CRMC-KNIFE
and a CRMC-MAIL, but you will get these for free in a little while.

Outside the shops, head north and enter the Hunters' Guild. This is where
hunters all over the land go to find work and earn money. There are eight
jobs that you can take from here throughout the game, but only one will be
available at the moment. If you wish, you can sign up for this job. See
'7.1.1. The Ranch Owner' for more details.

Before leaving the Hunters' Guild, head to the very east side of the
building then go up the right-most door. You will be in a small theatre
with a stage occupying the top part of the room. Approach the stage to
watch a short dance if you wish, then walk through the door on the left.
Continue through the next left door, then pass to the left above the
receptionist and into the far left room. Pick up the 1000 MST and a TRIMATE
from the top-left corner, then go back.

Another thing you can do while you are in this area is visit the wreckage
of a crashed ship. Exit Aiedo and head a little east and then north. A
little further than the village of Nalya you will see a pile of junk on the
ground. Make sure that Rika is at least Level 4, then enter this area.
