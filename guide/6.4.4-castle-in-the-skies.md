### 6.4.4. Castle in the Skies

```
Starting Party: Wren , Chaz , Rika , Kyra , Rune
Starting Level: 27-28, 29-30, 30-32, 28-29, 28-29
Leave Gumbious Temple, save your game, and prepare for some Level building.
The next dungeon you are going to face is the biggest in the game, and the
most difficult so far. You will need to get all the characters up to an
absolute minimum of Level 32 before you even think of going anywhere else.
One of the best places to go to gain Levels quickly is the Weapons Plant,
as the enemies there provide the most EXP at this stage of the game. Try to
allow Arthropods and Wiredines to use their COMBINE ability, as LifeDeletrs
are very lucrative indeed.

By the time you have finished, Chaz should be at least Level 33 or 34, Rika
should be about Level 35 or 36, Rune and Wren should be at Level 32, and
Kyra should be around Level 32 or 33.

Go and buy all the equipment you may not have been able to afford earlier,
and replace Rika's THUNDRCLAW with the LACO-CLAW that you have yet to
equip. Also, ensure everyone else is equipped properly for the trial ahead.

Once you are ready, use RYUKA or a TELEPIPE to teleport to Tyler, or hop
in the Ice Digger and head to this area, then go into the Spaceport.

The party automatically get into the Landale, and Wren begins his search
for the Air Castle. A moment later, he reports that he has got something,
although it is very faint. Rika asks if Wren can get it on-screen, so Wren
says he will try. An image of a floating castle amidst some asteroids
appears on the screen. Chaz says that it's the Air Castle, and Rune backs
this up. Chaz notes that it has a spooky atmosphere, so Kyra teases him,
asking if he's getting scared. Chaz says her to stop being ridiculous, and
announces that they are going in.

The Algo map screen appears, with a new destination on the list: A.CASTL.
Before you select it, though, cancel from the map and go down through the
Spaceport. Use one of the Regen Platforms to get the party fully prepared,
then return up and into the Landale. Select A.CASTL from the list, and
watch as the Landale cruises through space and lands next to the castle.

Wren says that gravity is normal and that there is a breathable atmosphere,
and notes that it must be an artificial condition. Chaz suggests that
whoever is there must be expecting them.

The Air Castle is, by far, the largest dungeon in the game, mainly because
it is really two dungeons merged into one. As such, the entire excursion is
split into two chapters, each outlining one of the distinct dungeon areas
of the Air Castle.
```
