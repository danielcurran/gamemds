Rune is a useful guide throughout the game, aiding the party for a short
while at the beginning before joining permanently not long afterwards. For
a long time, nothing is really known about Rune. He seems to know an awful
lot about quite few things, and is also an old friend of both Alys and
Grandfather Dorin. Even when it is discovered that Rune is, in fact, the
fifth-generation Lutz, his life story still remains a mystery.

Rune is a wise yet enigmatic man, known for his ability to use a strange
technique known as 'magic'. He is very helpful in the game, providing
information about a variety of subjects, although some of the things he
knows seem to create an air of suspicion around Rune, especially to Chaz
and Rika. He is also considerably powerful, having access to unique spells
and being the best offensive Technique user of all the characters.

His attitude towards Chaz is a confusing one at first, as he seems to enjoy
teasing Chaz for no reason at all. This is meant harmlessly, but Chaz
doesn't find Rune's remarks amusing. Only when they reach Esper Mansion
and Chaz discovers Rune's legacy and true opinion of Chaz, does Chaz really
understand what Rune was doing. It is from this point that Rune and Chaz
begin to become very close friends.

Rune is a mentor, an adviser and a guide. He seems to be quite insensitive
on occasion, but ultimately cares very deeply about everyone. He is also
able to detect the Black Energy Wave, seemingly due to a combination of his
magical prowess and the fact that he is Lutz. On a personal note, I think
that Rune is the best character in the game.


Rune's favoured weapons are rods, and he wears mainly bands and robes.

Rune has a large selection of offensive Techniques and a few others,
including fire, water and gravity, a couple of curative Techs, travel
Techs and a hindrance Tech.

His Skills include powerful offensive and instant-death spells.
