### 6.1.10. Stone to Flesh

> 🥉 **Petrified People** — Save the people of Zema. _(RetroAchievements · 5 pts)_

```
Starting Party: Gryz , Alys , Chaz , Hahn
Starting Level: 9    , 9-10 , 9    , 9
You now need to go back to Zema. You can either use a TELEPIPE or Chaz's
RYUKA Technique to get there, or you can walk back to gain some more
experience. If you intend to walk, make sure you rest at the Inn before
you leave. Once you are at Zema, walk in to begin a lengthy cut-scene.

The party will use the Alshline to restore the residents of Zema and the
research team. Professor Holt is glad to see Hahn, although he has no
recollection of what has happened. He instructs Hahn to go back to the
academy and tell them what has happened, and tells him that he's off to
explore further into Birth Valley.

With this problem over with, the group decide to take it easy for the rest
of the day. Hahn decides to return to Piata, and Gryz resolves to go after
Zio (alone, if he has to). Alys suggests that she and Chaz go back to the
Hunters' Guild in Aiedo, when suddenly there is a scream.

The group rush over to the entrance to Birth Valley to find an Igglanova
blocking their path. You are automatically flung into battle (with fully
restored HP and TP).

her TITNSLASHRs. This battle will be over in a couple of rounds.
The villagers think it is a curse, and Chaz becomes curious about what is
further inside. Hahn realises the Professor is inside, and Alys is kind
enough to join him for the fair price of 1000 Mesetas. It seems Hahn won't
be getting married for a while.
```
