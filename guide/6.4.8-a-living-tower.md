### 6.4.8. A Living Tower

> 🏅 **Dezolian Flame** — Retrieve the Eclipse Torch. _(RetroAchievements · 25 pts)_

```
Starting Party: Wren , Chaz , Rika , Kyra , Rune
Starting Level: 35   , 37   , 39-40, 35-36, 34-35
 // DUNGEON #19 ¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯\
|/  Garuberk Tower         \_________________________________
   Enemies: DarkMaraud, D-Elm-Lars, Ghoul, Hakenleft, King Saber,
            Radhin
  Treasure: TRIMATE, POW-SHIELD, TRIMATE, MOONSLASHR, STAR-DEW,
            MOON-DEW, ESCAPIPE, STAR-DEW
 \
  \   Boss: Dark Force
   \______________________________


You will be inside a stangely organic chamber. In fact, the entire tower is
organic, with different styles on different floors. It can be a little
confusing at first, but it really isn't as bad as it seems to be.

Head up and left across the snowy chamber, continuing up and around to the
right. There is a light flesh-coloured column in the wall. This functions
as an elevator, so use it as such.

There is nothing of any value in the area below you (except clear slime),
so go right and then up and around to the left. Continue left until you see
an eye protruding from the ground. Examine this and the whole floor will
tremble and make an odd noise. Walk up a little bit and collect the TRIMATE
from the right of the small room (you may need to look closely as the
chests are a little harder to spot in this dungeon).

Go back down and then head right. You will notice that touching the eye has
caused an opening to appear in the right wall, so continue through to the
right and then downwards. Head down and right, ignoring the junction to the
left for the moment, then walk all the way down to the bottom to retrieve
the POW-SHIELD. Return up the corridor then take the left turn you just
passed. Enter the elevator in the top wall.

Make your way down, heading straight past the passage to your right, and
continue all the way to the bottom right of the chamber, weaving in and out
of the green tubes. Pick up another TRIMATE, then go back up to the path
leading to the right. Continue right from here, then up, right and down,
then go left at the following junction. Ignore the next elevator you come
to, and head down, right and then up to use the elevator here instead.

This room is a dead end, and filled with green tubes. Go down around the
tubes and pick up the concealed MOONSLASHR from the bottom left. Equip this
to Kyra alongside one of the LACOSLASHRs, then return to the elevator. Go
back down, left and then up, and ascend in the elevator you passed earlier.

Head right, then go up and right along the corridor until you reach a
junction. Turn upwards, then turn left at the following junction to pick up
a STAR-DEW from the left side of the small room that follows. Go out of
this room and continue leftwards along the passage, turning down at the
corner. Head all the way to the bottom of the passage, turning a little
left along the way, then go around to the left and up to another elevator.
Enter it to get to the floor above.

Go right again, heading up and right until you get to a crossroad. Go down
a little from here and pick up the MOON-DEW, then return to the crossroad
and continue upwards (the right path is a dead end). Continue upwards and
leftwards until you reach a goo-plastered chamber. Use the elevator in the
top wall to go up yet another floor.

Walk a little to the left, then follow the passageway all the way around
upwards, left, down and then right until you reach a junction. Turn up into
the small chamber and collect the ESCAPIPE from the top right corner. Go
back down to the junction and continue to the right, heading up and then
left. Pass the first elevator you meet on the wall above, then head around
and then up, and take the second elevator just to the right.

In this small room, go to the bottom left and use the eye switch. This will
open up a room in the floor above. Once you have done this, ascend in the
elevator again and go back round to the one below that you ignored just
now. Take it to the floor below, then head a little down and then left.
Follow the corridor leftwards and upwards, then turn right at the corner
and continue upwards to another elevator. Enter it.

You are now in a large chamber, although the left part of it would have
been sealed before you activated the second eye. Head left to the newly
available elevator, and take it up to the final floor. Head down and then
into the corridor on the left, and procure the STAR-DEW from the bottom
corner before returning to the main passage. The path below is a dead end,
so head up and follow the passage until you see a weird creature occupying
the top wall. Before approaching, heal the party (you may even wish to
teleport out of the Tower, rest and then save first). Once you are ready,
equip the PLSMLAUNCH to Wren and then walk up to the creature.

The party will stop just before the monster, and Rika will ask if it is
Dark Force. Rune will reply that it is, but Wren will report that it is
nothing like anything they found on Kuran. Rika supposes there were two
Dark Forces, and Chaz adds that Raja was right about this one creating
havoc on Dezolis. Rune tells him that it is not just Dezolis, but that Dark
Force is destroying the whole Algo system. Wren informs the party that Dark
Force senses their presence, and it then attacks.

This version of Dark Force is a lot more powerful than the one you met in
Kuran, but so are the characters. Regardless of this, however, the battle
will be more difficult than the first one.

In the first round, get Chaz to use his RAYBLADE, let Rika use SANER, have
Rune fire his new LEGEON, get Wren to set up a BARRIER, and have Kyra use
WARLA. This will set up the party for the following rounds, ensuring Dark
Force acts last, and also scratch the surface of Dark Force's HP total.

Dark Force has a different range of abilities this time, including a Skill
that sends a character to Sleep (EVIL EYE), a very damaging beam that hits
one character (SHDWBREATH), and a powerful hit-all attack (LIGHTSHOWR).

Hopefully, Wren will be able to set up his BARRIER before Dark Force uses
SHDWBREATH or LIGHTSHOWR, or you may find that you receive a crippling
amount of damage (including the possibility of immediate flat-lining).

In the following rounds, keep Chaz using RAYBLADE (or NATHU when he runs
out), let Rika use either SAR or GISAR following a LIGHTSHOWR (don't use
NASAR as it will not be necessary at this stage of the game), or GIRES or
NARES following SHDWBREATH, have Rune use EFESS (or NAWAT once the supply
is depleted), get Wren to attack or use RECOVER if needed, and let Kyra use
NAFOI or provide some additional healing. If the organic members of your
party do not need healing, have Rika add to the offence by using DBLSLASH.

If any of the party fall, follow the usual fast character revival and slow
character healing strategy, or use a SOL-DEW if you need to revive quickly.

Try not to let a character's HP stay below half their maximum total, as
there is the possibility that a single attack from Dark Force may knock
them down. Also, if any of the characters succumb to Dark Force's EVIL EYE,
let wake up naturally (don't use Rune's AROWS as this will waste his turn).

Stay offensive and heal quickly, and you'll find that the battle will be
over in around eight to ten rounds.
```
