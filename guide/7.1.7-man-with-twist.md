### 7.1.7. Man with twist

> 🥈 **HG: Man with a Twist** — Complete 'Man with a Twist' in the Hunter's Guild. _(RetroAchievements · 10 pts)_
> 🥈 **Limit: King Rappy** — Defeat King Rappy in 'Man with a Twist' with no 5th party member. Combined level limit: 110, party of 4. _(RetroAchievements · 10 pts)_

```
Recommended Level:  36+
When Available:     After obtaining the Hydrofoil
Commission Awarded: Nothing
This is a request from Mr.Sekreas of Torinco.

"We are at our wits' end due to a flock of birds who are eating up our
 terraced fields. Please exterminate them..."

That is the message.


Before you accept this job, take note that although you are offered 20000
Mesetas to complete the task, you won't actually receive any money at all.

After accepting this job, teleport to Termi and get into the Hydrofoil.
Now, head southwest to the west coast near the Ladea Tower. From here, go
due south onto a large island. The village of Torinco should be within
sight to the west, so drive in.

Go north up the first few steps, then continue up and cross the next steps.
Head east and enter the house at the end. Speak to the man in here, and he
will introduce himself as Sekreas. He will tell you that a strange flock of
birds has been eating up the fields, and that he knows where the head
honcho bird can be found. He asks the party to exterminate the head honcho
bird, and explains it can be found in a cave to the northeast of Torinco.

Rest at the Inn if you need to, then head out of the village. Go a little
to the north, then east below the mountain range. Save your game just
outside the cave entrance, then go on in.

Head up the passage until you reach a junction. Go right and then continue
upwards, crossing the set of steps when you come to them. Follow the path
up and then around to the left, continuing up until you reach another set
of steps. Ignore the corridor to the right, and head up the thin passage
above. Walk all the way to the top, then stop when you reach a larger
chamber. Heal up all the characters, and equip Wren's best single-target
weapon (PLSMLAUNCH or PHOTNERASR). Now, address the large yellow bird
sitting on the platform above. It won't speak, but it will attack.

The master bird is not too difficult, and won't really require any serious
careful strategy to defeat.

In the first round, have Chaz use RAYBLADE, let Rika use DEBAN, have Rune
use LEGEON, and get Wren to use BARRIER. If there is another character in
your party, let them perform their best attack (avoiding light Skills).

King Rappy will either use a normal attack or its EARTHQUAKE Skill (which
damages all characters). With the defences set up, neither of these attacks
should pose too much of a threat.

In the rounds that follow, let Chaz continue using RAYBLADE, have Rika use
DBLSLASH, get Rune to use NAWAT, let Wren just attack, and have your other
character (if there is one) use their best abilities. 

Obviously, have Wren use RECOVER if he needs to (unlikely), and get Rika to
heal any other characters that drop to low HP levels (also unlikely).

If you keep the pressure on, King Rappy should fly the nest in about four
or five rounds.
After the battle, the boss bird will fly around the characters and down the
corridor. Chaz will tell it to stop, and start to run after it. At that
moment, Sekreas runs into the room and tells the party to stop. Sekreas
tells the party to let the bird go, but Chaz asks why, considering Sekreas
was the one who asked for it to be exterminated. Sekreas agrees, then asks
for the party's forgiveness. Rika demands to know what's going on, so
Sekreas explains. He starts by telling the group that he has always been an
animal lover, and that recently he discovered a new breed of bird. He goes
on to say that he used the cave to rear the birds, but eventually they
began looking for food. The birds went to Torinco and started to eat up the
fields, so the villagers decided to set about exterminating them. He asks
the party if they agree with him that this was a cruel thing to do, but
Chaz asks why, if that was the case, Sekreas commissioned the Hunters'
Guild to exterminate the boss bird. Sekreas replies that he knew a head
honcho bird had started to emerge in the flock, and that it was very
powerful. He (foolishly) thought that even a hunter wouldn't be able to
defeat it, and hoped that this would disuade the villagers from trying to
destroy the birds. Chaz realises that Sekreas had been using them, and
Sekreas admits that he had grossly underestimated their strength. Sekreas
says he is sorry, and consents to the party locking him up in prison or
whatever they want. Chaz can't be bothered with him, and tells Sekreas to
get out of his sight. Sekreas complies, running off down the passage.

Chaz tells the party that he can sort of understand why Sekreas did what he
did, and adds that the boss bird has flown away somewhere, and that it
won't eat up Torinco's fields any more. Rune points out that it could just
go and start on some other fields, but Chaz just says that they'll deal
with that when they come to it. 

Don't bother returning to Torinco as Sekreas will only apologise for what
he has done. Instead, report to the Hunters' Guild and report what has
happened. The receptionist will say that no word has been received from
Torinco, and that action will be carried out to both reclaim Sekreas' debt
and to save face for the guild. Chaz insists not to bother, so the matter
is forgotten.
```
