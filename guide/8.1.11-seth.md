Seth is not really one of the true party members of the game, but he is a
playable character, and therefore fits the sole criterion of this section.

Seth meets the party on the island east of Krup, introducing himself as an
archaeologist. The party are surprised to see him, but he behaves extremely
friendly and in a very polite manner, so they allow him to join the party.
Rune, however, is immediately suspicious of Seth, which is not a good sign.

Apart from the fact that Seth happened to be on the island in the first
place, he earns more suspicion from the dark abilities he possesses. He
also seems very powerful for an archaeologist. All of these suspicions are
confirmed when Seth becomes Dark Force outside the Soldiers' Temple, but
Seth's true nature remains a mystery. It is not entirely certain whether
Seth was a disguise used by Dark Force, or if Seth was really a normal
Parmanian that was possessed by Dark Force. The two schools of thought on
this subject are as follows:

If Seth was just Dark Force's guise, why does he scream in pain before
being ripped apart and revealing Dark Force. If he was merely a mask,
surely you would expect him to laugh evilly or something like that, then
simply morph into Dark Force. Also, he is noted to be 39 years of age.
Surely if he was but a manifestation of Dark Force, he would have no age.

If Seth was a normal archaeologist before Dark Force possessed him, you
would expect that he could be found in some town or village beforehand, or
at least be mentioned by someone somewhere. You'd think, being a scholar,
that he would be known at Motavia Academy. Also, how would Seth have got
to the island in the first place, having no mode of transportation?


Seth's favoured weapons are dagger, and he wears mainly bands and light
armours.

Seth cannot use any Techniques, which is unusual for a human.

His Skills include dark offensive and hindrance spells.
