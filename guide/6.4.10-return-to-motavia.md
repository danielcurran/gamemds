### 6.4.10. Return to Motavia

> 🥉 **Hyper Hovercraft** — Use the HydroFoil for the first time. _(RetroAchievements · 3 pts)_

```
Starting Party: Wren , Chaz , Rika , Rune
Starting Level: 36-37, 39   , 41-42, 36
Walk north and through the door. You will find a secret, underground
worship site. Approach the very old Dezolisian sitting on the raised
platform, and he will speak to you. He will introduce himself as the bishop
of Gumbious Temple, which had stood for two thousand years. Rune asks him
what's going on, and Rika will add that the temple has disappeared without
a trace. Chaz tells the bishop that they've defeated Dark Force, and asks
if that isn't enough. The bishop replies that 'The Profound Darkness'
still exists, adding that it is the root of all evil, causing death and
destruction since long ago. Apparently, Dark Force is merely a tool in this
existence. This doesn't sound good. The bishop continues that Dark Force
had come to Dezolis to find the location of Rykros. Chaz asks what this is,
and the bishop tells him it is something that is beyond the party's
control, and that it is the reason why the Profound Darkness stirs. He
explains that if the Profound Darkness cannot get hold of Rykros, it will
not allow the party to get it.

Rune asks what Rykros is, and Chaz adds that he can't understand how
something could be so important that they must scramble to find it. The
bishop replies that Rykros is the place where all the secret answers lie,
but the party do not understand. He then confesses that he does not know
what or where Rykros is, but he does know that when Rykros returns, the
Aero-Prism will light the way. Rune has heard of this artifact, and tells
the party that the first-generation Lutz stored the Aero-Prism in a place
called Soldiers' Temple on Motavia. Chaz notes that this is in the lake
near Krup, and Wren adds that he will contact Demi to arrange a vehicle
capable of crossing water. Chaz thanks the bishop and tells him that they
will return the Eclipse Torch. The bishop then asks if they will go to
Rykros, and Chaz replies that they will. To this, the bishop adds that he
has done his part, and that it is now up to the party to shape history.

The party automatically board the Landale and fly to Motavia. They enter
the Spaceport, and Demi (calling from Nurvus) welcomes Wren. He asks about
the condition of Motavia, and Demi reports that it is still on hold. She
adds that everything Wren requested is on standby, and Wren compliments her
on her efficiency. Demi is very grateful for this praise, and unveils the
Hydrofoil, explaining that it has been taken from the Machine Centre and
completely overhauled. Wren tells them that they can now cross water, and
Rune proposes that they go straight to the Soldiers' Temple. Chaz asks if
Demi is ready to go, but she tells him that it will still be some time
before Motavia's systems are running normally again. Rika says that it's a
pity, but wishes Demi the best of luck.

You now have unlimited use of the Hydrofoil, and you can use it at any
point outside. It is much faster than both the Land Rover and the Ice
Ice Digger, but it is the least powerful of the three. In addition to this,
the Hydrofoil can cross water. Select HYDROFOIL to enter the vehicle.

Once you have been given the Hydrofoil, the remaining four Hunters' Guild
jobs become available. The first three of these can be carried out at your
leisure, but if you want to complete the last job (and acquire Wren's best
weapon, armour and Skill), you will need to do it before getting ELSYDEON.
See '7.1.5. Stain in Life', '7.1.6. Dying Boy', '7.1.7. Man with twist' and
'7.1.8. Silver Soldier' for more details.

It will probably be a good idea to get these jobs done now, as you really
should get all the characters up to at least Level 38 before attempting the
next section of the quest. You should be aiming to get Chaz up to Level 41
or 42, Rika should be around Level 43 or 44, Rune should be at Level 38,
and Wren should be around Level 38 or 39. If you don't want to do the jobs,
head over to Monsen and take out some Sand Worms to get the EXP you need.

Once you have got the Levels you need, teleport to Krup and jump into your
new Hydrofoil. Head east across the water and get out of the vehicle on the
small island. On top of the island is the Soldiers' Temple, but you've got
to get the party up there first.
```
