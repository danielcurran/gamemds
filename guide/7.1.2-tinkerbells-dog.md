### 7.1.2. Tinkerbell's Dog

> 🥉 **HG: Tinkerbell's Dog** — Complete 'Tinkerbell's Dog' in the Hunter's Guild. _(RetroAchievements · 5 pts)_

```
Recommended Level:  N/A
When Available:     After obtaining the Land Rover
Commission Awarded: 2000 MST (-280 MST costs = 1720 MST profit)
This is a request from the house where Tinkerbell lives.

"Our household pet 'Rocky' has disappeared.
 Please find him..."

That is the message.


After accepting this job, head to the east side of Aiedo. There are two
houses near the south wall next to the river. Tinkerbell's house is the one
on the left, so enter.

Speak to Tinkerbell's father on the left and he will explain the situation,
hinting that the dog is likely to be found outside of Aiedo, then speak to
Tinkerbell's mother near the door and she will inform you that Rocky is
very fat and that he likes sweet things. You can speak to Tinkerbell if you
wish, but you won't get any important information.

Leave the house, and go to the south gate. Step just outside and head left
along the wall. Turn north at the end, hugging the wall all the way, until
you reach a building at the corner. When you go in, you will see that the
room is deserted, so head down the stairs and you find yourself in Naura
cake shop. Speak to the man behind the counter and buy one SHORTCAKE for
280 MST, then leave Aiedo.

Rocky will either be in Krup, Monsen or Termi. In Krup and Monsen, you will
find Rocky to the southeast of the Tool Store. In Termi, he will be to the
southeast of the statue of Alis. Address Rocky when you find him, and the
dog will bark. Chaz will surmise that he is Rocky. Address the dog again
and you will give him the SHORTCAKE. You then take Rocky back to Aiedo.

A second later and your back in Tinkerbell's house. Her father will thank
you, and inform you that your commission will be sent to the guild. Return
to the Hunters' Guild and collect your 2000 MST.
```
