### 13.2.2. Daggers

```
Daggers are made up of short, sharp blades attached to a hilt, perfect for
quick strikes. They are one-handed weapons, and so one can be equipped with
a shield, or two can be equipped at the same time. In the latter case, the
daggers' attack bonuses are cumulative.

Chaz, Hahn and Seth can use daggers.


DAGGER     - 'A knife with a small blade.'
¯¯¯¯¯¯
40 MST       Hahn begins with this weapon.
             Also available at Mile and Aiedo Weapon Stores.

             A poor weapon and the weakest dagger in the game. Exchange it
             for a HUNT-KNIFE as soon as possible.


HUNT-KNIFE - 'A large multi-purpose knife.'
¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯
120 MST      Chaz begins with this weapon.
             Also available at Mile, Zema, Tonoe and Aiedo Weapon Stores.

             Although a weak dagger, Hahn will find two of these more
             preferable to the DAGGER at the beginning of the game.


TITNDAGGER - 'A dagger made of a titanium alloy.'
¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯
240 MST      Available at Tonoe and Aiedo Weapon Stores.

             A durable dagger, it serves Hahn better than the HUNT-KNIFE in
             the early stages of the adventure.


CRMC-KNIFE - 'A knife made of ceramic.'
¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯
1600 MST     Available at Aiedo Hunters' Guild and Tyler Weapon Stores.
             May also be dropped by Speard.

             Hahn's best weapon before he leaves the party on Motavia, this
             is a useful dagger to use before rescuing Demi.


LASR-KNIFE - 'A knife with a small built-in laser device.'
¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯
S            Found in Plate System.
1600 MST
             This radiation-based weapon can only be equipped on Chaz. As
             Chaz has better weapons at the time, sell this dagger.


PLSMDAGGER - 'A dagger with a small built-in plasma unit.'
¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯
S            May be dropped by Siren386.
1800 MST
             Like the LASR-KNIFE, only Chaz can equip this radiation-based
             weapon when it is found. It may as well be sold.


TORNDDAGGR - 'A dagger that has the power of HEWN magic.'
¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯
8300 MST     Available at Zosa and Meese Weapon Stores.

             Although not useful as a weapon, like the two daggers above,
             this dagger can be used as a single-target HEWN spell.


LACODAGGER - 'A dagger made of high grade Laconia.'
¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯
9400 MST     Seth begins with this weapon.
             Also available at Jut Weapon Store.

             Only useful if you choose Hahn at the end of the game, this
             radiation-based weapon is best paired with Hahn's MAHLAYDAGR.


MAHLAYDGGR - 'Dagger that Hahn created with his father.'
¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯
S            Hahn returns with this weapon. (Only usable by Hahn)
7900 MST
             The best dagger in the game. As well as having a good ATK, it
             can also be used in battle as the NAFOI Technique.
```
