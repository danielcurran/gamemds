The Ice Digger is the most powerful of the vehicles. Its primary function
is to allow the party to pummel through the ice walls on Dezolis, but it
can also cross quicksand like the Land Rover. It is just as fast as the
Land Rover, and is far more useful in battle. It has the most SP of all the
vehicles, and also inflicts the most damage. Even after acquiring the
Hydrofoil, you will probably want to use the Ice Digger for most travel.
