### 6.2.12. Upward Mobility

```
Starting Party: Chaz , Gryz , Rika , Rune , Demi
Starting Level: 19-20, 19-20, 19-20, 21-22, 16-17
Demi informs the party that they she won't be able to shut down Nurvus by
normal means, and that she must connect to it directly to access it. She
bids farwell to the group, and then accesses Nurvus. A few seconds later
and the operation is a success, and all of the control systems on Motavia
have been stopped. She points out, however, that without the systems
operational Motavia will soon become uninhabitable and barren, and so all
of the controls must be sorted out individually and reset. Rika then points
out that in order to do that something must first be done about Zelan, the
artificial satellite that is sending abnormal signals. She suggests that
they go to Zelan, and Chaz concurs. Gryz then notes that Zelan is in outer
space, a location that may be quite difficult to reach. This doesn't really
pose a problem, as Demi informas them that Nurvus is equipped with a
shuttle. She tells the group that she cannot go with them, but that they
should seek her master, Wren, who is aboard the satellite. Demi then wishes
them the best of luck, and they depart.

The next thing you see is a spaceport rising from the sand a little to the
northwest of Zio's Fort. A bright, shiny shuttle glints in the sun, and
the party marvel at it for a while. But one of them won't be going. Gryz
can't leave his little sister, and apologises to Chaz, who understands.
You'll also notice that Zio's Fort has disappeared.
```
