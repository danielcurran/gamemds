### 6.3.4. Malign Manifestation

> 🥈 **Dark Satellite** — Resolve Kuran's communications problem. _(RetroAchievements · 10 pts)_
> 🥉 **Initiate: Hyper-Jammer** — Recover the Hyper-Jammer parts in Kuran. _(RetroAchievements · 5 pts)_
> 🥉 **Kuran's Charge Cannon** — Defeat C-Ray Tube + Sat. Minions in Kuran. _(RetroAchievements · 3 pts)_
> 🏅 **Limit: Dark Force I** — Defeat Dark Force I. Combined level limit: 113, party of 5. _(RetroAchievements · 25 pts)_

```
Starting Party: Wren , Chaz , Rika , Rune , Raja
Starting Level: 22   , 22-23, 23-24, 23-24, 25-26
 // DUNGEON #13 ¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯\

   Enemies: CommndBall, C-Ray Tube, FloatMine, Piercer,
            Sat.Minion, Siren386
  Treasure: HIJAMMER (Skill), REPAIR-KIT, ZIRCOARMER, 3000 MST,
            REPAIR-KIT, NAPALMSHOT
 \
  \   Boss: Dark Force
   \______________________________


After the short dialogue, head up the passageway and take the elevator at
the top to the floor above. Follow the platform to the left and take the
door around the corner. Go all the way along the corridor to the left and
then head up and open the chest in the small room. Chaz asks what's in
there, and Wren replies it is an internal weapon unit for androids. He
surmises that it is a Hyper-Jammer, a device that temporarily scrambles
the AI of machines using electromagnetic waves. Wren then installs it.


Wren acquires the HIJAMMER Skill
Go back down and head around the wall section to the passage below the
elevator. Walk down and pick up the REPAIR-KIT, then return to the elevator
and take it down to the floor below. Go right, up and then right along the
long corridor, taking the door on the bottom wall at the end. Continue
right, ignoring the path below you, and head around the bend in the path
to reach another junction. Here, follow the line leading down and go
through the door at the bottom. Now go down, around the central column to
either the left or right, and ascend in the elevator.

Go left or right, then up around the elevator shaft and continue up along
the walkway. Turn left at the corner and keep going to the next corner
leading upwards. Follow the walkway all the way to the top then head right
and use the elevator in the wall above you.

Head left, down, left and then down again, and retrieve the ZIRCOARMER from
the small alcove to the left. Replace Wren's SPACEDARMR with this new
hardware, then return back up to the elevator. Continue right, down, right
and then all the way down past the open chest on the right. Head a little
to the left and the go down and through the door on the right.

Follow the passage all the way to the right, ignoring the elevator for the
moment, and then head up at the junction at the end. Go around into a small
room and collect the 3000 MST, then return to the junction and head down to
procure another REPAIR-KIT. Go back up to the junction again and go back
left along the corridor to the elevator you ignored just now. Take it.

Head right in the next passageway, and keep going along it, turning down
and then right at the end. Pick up the NAPALMSHOT, but don't equip it to
Wren just yet. His PULSE-LASR is far more useful at the moment. Go back up
and all the way along the corridor to the left, and then take the door in
the bottom wall opposite the elevator. Go down and then left, ignoring the
path below you, and then head up at the next corner. Continue up this
platform, shimmying left on the way, and turn left when you reach the top.
There are a few paths branching off here, but ignore all of them and enter
the elevator instead.

Go a little down and then either left or right along the walkway, and keep
going down and then around into the elevator to the floor below. Go around
to the left or the right and then up, ignoring the side branches, and
follow the line at the top of the room. The party will then stop and have
a short conversation. Chaz realises that they are in the main control
centre, and Wren says that he will look for the cause of the malfunction.
At that moment, however, Rika notices something in the shadows ahead.

Make sure all of the characters are up to at least Level 24. This will
likely mean that Chaz and Rune should be Level 25, Rika should be Level 26,
Wren should be Level 24 and Raja should be Level 27. If they are not, spend
a little time in Kuran (not the area you are currently in as there are no
enemies here), and build up your characters until they are. It may also be
a good idea to HINAS or ESCAPIPE out, return to Motavia or Dezolis
Spaceport, and use a Regen Platform to restore your party, then go back
through Kuran until you are back into this area.

Get Raja to heal everyone (except Wren who can heal himself by walking or
using RECOVER), and equip the NAPALMSHOT to Wren. Once you are completely
prepared, head up and approach the strange object covering the computer
terminal. The party stops just before, and Rika feels a great sense of
oppression. Rune recognizes the creature as Dark Force, the being that Zio
had revered as a god. Wren, in his unemotional way, diagnoses that Dark
Force seems to be the cause of the problem, and that they must remove it.
Rune remarks that it may be easier said than done, but Chaz has no fear.
He tells the party to go for it, and so a battle begins.

Dark Force is, without a doubt, the most powerful enemy you will have faced
yet, and it's appearance certainly hints at this. However, if you are smart
in this battle you may find that it is easier than the fight with Zio.

In the first round, let Chaz use GITHU, Rika use SANER, Rune use NAFOI (or
NAWAT if he is Level 26), Wren use BARRIER and Raja use BLESSING. This
should inflict a decent 400-500 HP of damage on Dark Force, and also
prepare you for the rounds to come.

Dark Force will use either FLARE SHOT (an extremely powerful laser attack
that targets one character), PHONONMASR or BURSTROC (both of which are
fairly damaging hit-all spells).

In the rounds that follow, keep Chaz using GITHU, let Rika use DBLSLASH,
have Rune continue to use NAFOI or NAWAT, continue attacking with Wren,
and get Raja to maintain the party with healing Techniques.

If Dark Force uses FLARE SHOT in the previous round, get Raja to use
GIRES on the victim (or get Wren to use RECOVER if he was hit). If Dark
Force uses one of his party attacks, have Raja use SAR. In the unfortunate
event that a character should fall, have a fast character use REVER or a
MOON-DEW (if you have any) and get a slow character to heal them with GIRES
or a DIMATE. If Wren is the flatlined character, use a REPAIR-KIT.

If you are in a situation where Wren is using RECOVER to heal and everyone
else is at maximum HP, let Raja throw out a ST. FIRE, as this Skill hurts
Dark Force a lot.

Use this strategy, keeping your party's HP levels high and continually
pummelling your opponent, and Dark Force should crumble to dust after a
mere six or seven rounds.
Dark Force explodes in a display of light. Chaz tells Wren to get Kuran
back to normal, and Wren efficienly obliges. Chaz then asks Rune how he
knew that the creature was Dark Force, and Rune replies that he has seen
it before. Chaz then asks where he saw it, but Rune doesn't seem in the
mood for answering. Both Chaz and Rika get a little suspicious, but this
is interrupted by Wren approaching and informing them that all of Kuran's
functions have been properly restored. He also tells them that all they
need to do now is to make some final adjustments at Zelan, so they go.
```
