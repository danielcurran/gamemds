Go straight up, turning left along the way. Take the first turning down
when you reach it, and follow it to its end to get the DIMATE. Return to
the top, then continue on to the left all the way to the end. Go down and
ignore the passage to the right and the following one to the left, and
continue down and right to acquire a CURE-PARAL. Go back up and take the
left passage you skipped just now, following it around and down until you
reach another junction. Take the path to the left and continue down until
you reach the exit. You will now be in a different area.

Most people in this village seem to be either under the evil spell of Zio,
or mortally afraid of him (the ruin you passed on the way here being a
village before he came along). There is a church dedicated to him here, and
a possessed man convulsing in front of it. You won't find much of any value
in terms of information here, but there is a weapon worth getting.
Go to the very bottom of town and step just outside the gate. Head to the
left and then up, hugging the wall. Take the entrance to the left, and
enter the right-hand building. There is an opened chest in here, and the
other one contains a LASRSLASHR, which you should give to Alys.

Rest at the Inn if you like, and purchase any more supplies you need, then
leave Kadary. Wander around outside, returning when you need to, until you
have got everyone in the party up to at least Level 14, preferably Chaz and
Gryz up to Level 15. If you have completed the first Hunters' Guild job,
you should already be well on the way to achieving this. Once you have
finished, fully restore your party before going south to Zio's Fort. Save
and then enter.
