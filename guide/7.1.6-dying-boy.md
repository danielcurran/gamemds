### 7.1.6. Dying Boy

> 🥉 **HG: The Dying Boy** — Complete 'The Dying Boy' in the Hunter's Guild. _(RetroAchievements · 5 pts)_

```
Recommended Level:  N/A
When Available:     After obtaining the Hydrofoil
Commission Awarded: 10000 MST (-500 MST costs = 9500 MST profit)
This is a request from Mr. Culvers of Torinco via letter transmission.

"My son has fallen ill. I would like to dicuss a certain plan I have to
 deal with this matter. I will go into more detail after we meet."

That is the message.


After accepting this job, teleport to Termi and go to the Bayamare Gift
Shop to buy an ALIS-SWORD. Leave the village, then get into the Hydrofoil
and head southwest to the west coast near the Ladea Tower. From here, go
due south onto a large island. The village of Torinco should be within
sight to the west, so drive in.

Torinco      Inn             |    90 MST per person|
                             |                     |
             Tool Store      |TRIMATE   |   400 MST|
                             |ANTIDOTE  |    10 MST|
                             |CURE-PARAL|   120 MST|
                             |TELEPIPE  |   130 MST|
                             |ESCAPIPE  |    70 MST|

Go north up the first few steps, then continue up and cross the next steps.
Head east and then north up another flight of steps, then go back west and
ascend the last steps to find a house just to the right. Go in.

Speak to the man standing next to the bed on the left side of the house. He
introduces himself as Culvers, and informs you that his son has become ill.
The doctors said that he had been cured, but Culvers says that the boy is
still convinced that he is going to die and that he can't be cured.
Apparently, it is one of those psychological illnesses. Culvers adds that,
back in the days when there were fewer monsters, they used to take family
trips to Termi. He wishes that they were able to go there now, but says
that it is not possible. However, Culvers thinks that if they were able to
take their son something connected with Termi, that it might restore his
strength. He asks the party if they can buy something from the gift shop
in Termi.

Fortunately, you have already bought something from the shop, so approach
the boy in bed and speak to him. The boy sees the Alis-Sword, and remembers
the heroine that he saw in Termi. The party step away from the bed, and
Chaz stands in the middle of the floor before the boy. Chaz tells the boy
to watch, then performs a few stylish sword moves. The boy is impressed,
and Chaz explains that the sword is filled with the strength and courage of
the heroine Alis. He gives the sword to the boy and tells him to get
better. The boy thanks Chaz, and tells him he is feeling better already.

Speak to Culvers once more, and Culvers will say that it is a miracle. He
thanks the party greatly, and says that he thinks Chaz is the second hero.
After that, he will tell the party they can collect their fee from the
guild. Return to Aiedo, and do what the man said.
```
