### 6.1.5. Rune in the Ruin

> 🥉 **Rune** — Recruit Rune. _(RetroAchievements · 5 pts)_

```
Starting Party: Alys , Chaz , Hahn
Starting Level: 7    , 4-5  , 4-5
You will now be back outside the cave in Zema. There is nothing else to be
done here, so leave the town.


Enemies between Zema and Molcum: Crawler, Locusta, Mini Worm, MonsterFly,
¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯  Sand Newt


Head a little south, then southwest along the coast before heading directly
south between the mountains. You should see the village of Molcum ahead.
However, don't enter it just yet. Spend some time fighting monsters in this
area, returning to Mile when you need to rest. Try to get Chaz and Hahn up
to Level 6, and perhaps get Alys to Level 8. When you are ready, enter.

Molcum is in ruins, all of the buildings having been burnt down. There seem
to be no people about either. Head north through the village until you see
a man dressed in white facing away from you. Approach and speak to him to
begin a cut-scene.

Alys seems to know the man, and calls him Rune. He greets her, and then he
and Chaz have a little squabble. Alys breaks it up and asks what happened,
and Rune replies that Zio burned the village down. It seems he's been up to
a lot recently. Hahn then cuts in by asking how they are going to get the
Alshline, and Rune suggests they go to Tonoe. He then gives directions that
take the party past a village called Krup, although Hahn seems strangely
averse to going into this particular place. Rune then invites himself to
join the group, but Chaz doesn't seem to happy with this. He also gets fed
up with Rune calling him Shorty, but before he can say what his name is,
Rune says that he had already said his name was Chaz (although he didn't).
They then decide to leave.


Rune joins the party (Level 17)
¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯

Rune will be a great asset to the group, being far more powerful than all
of the other characters. He will also give you access to some powerful
spells. As he is much tougher than everyone else at the moment, put Rune at
the front of the party, followed by Alys, Chaz and then Hahn. Also, swap
Rune's CIRCLET and Hahn's LTHR-BAND, as Hahn will benefit more from the
better head protection.
```
