## 15.3. Act III

<a id="s15-3-1"></a>

### 15.3.1. Dark Force 1

```
Met: Kuran                      Recommended Level before fighting:
  HP: 4540                         Chaz: 25       Wren: 24
 EXP: 32600                        Rika: 26-27    Raja: 27
 MST: 1                            Rune: 25


In the first round, let Chaz use GITHU, Rika use SANER, Rune use NAFOI (or
NAWAT if he is Level 26), Wren use BARRIER and Raja use BLESSING. This
should inflict a decent 400-500 HP of damage on Dark Force, and also
prepare you for the rounds to come.

Dark Force will use either FLARE SHOT (an extremely powerful beam attack
that targets one character), PHONONMASR or BURSTROC (both of which are
fairly damaging hit-all spells).

In the rounds that follow, keep Chaz using GITHU, let Rika use DBLSLASH,
have Rune continue to use NAFOI or NAWAT, continue attacking with Wren,
and get Raja to maintain the party with healing Techniques.

If Dark Force uses FLARE SHOT in the previous round, get Raja to use
GIRES on the victim (or get Wren to use RECOVER if he was hit). If Dark
Force uses one of his party attacks, have Raja use SAR. In the unfortunate
event that a character should fall, have a fast character use REVER or a
MOON-DEW (if you have any) and get a slow character to heal them with GIRES
or a DIMATE. If Wren is the flatlined character, use a REPAIR-KIT.

If you are in a situation where Wren is using RECOVER to heal and everyone
else is at maximum HP, let Raja throw out a ST. FIRE, as this Skill hurts
Dark Force a lot.

Use this strategy, keeping your party's HP levels high and continually
pummelling your opponent, and Dark Force should crumble to dust after a
mere six or seven rounds.
```
