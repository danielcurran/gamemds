### 6.3.8. Raja's not Laughing

```
Starting Party: Wren , Chaz , Rika , Rune , Raja
Starting Level: 26   , 27   , 28-29, 26-27, 29
Use RYUKA or a TELEPIPE to go back to Zosa, or pile into the Ice Digger.
Return west, back through the ice wall, then head south and west again, and
drive into Zosa.

Stay a night in the Inn and restock your inventory if you wish, then leave
Zosa and get in the Ice Digger. Head east, keeping to the south this time,
and pass east and south around a snow-topped forest. Just to the east of
this, below a mountain range, is a village. This is Reshel, but at the
moment there is nothing there but Zombies. Enter this place if you wish,
but the only things of value you will find are EXP and Mesetas.

Head a little south of Reshel, then continue east alongside the mountains
until you see another village tucked into the mountain range below. Unlike
Reshel, this village is inhabited, but it is not without problems. Drive
into the village of Meese.

As soon as you enter the village, remove all of Raja's equipment. He won't
be needing it any more. Meese is a Parmanian village, like Tyler, although
speaking to the villagers will reveal that the Meese seems to be overrun by
a terrible sickness. Those inflicted with the illness ofter die within a
month, and then return from the grave as zombies (explaining what happened
in Reshel).

Go to the Armour Store and sell all of the excess equipment you have been
carrying (including Raja's old equipment and Wren's NAPALMSHOT, as by the
time you will be fighting a boss again he will have a better single-target
gun). With the large sum of Mesetas you will have received, you shouldn't
have too much trouble buying two RFLC-MAILs, one for Chaz and one for Rika.
Equip their new armour and sell the old SILV-MAILs, then wander over to
the southeast corner of Meese. This used to be the Inn, but the owner had
made it into a public clinic since the disease outbreak. Go in.

There are a lot of sick people in this place, under the care of a group of
people known as Espers. They have been sent from Esper Mansion, a building
southeast of Meese, to try to help the villagers. Head up from the entrance
and then left along the corridor. Go upstairs, then go right and into the
right-hand room. Speak to the Esper here to learn that the illness started
as soon as the Garuberk Tower appeared, and Raja says that he told them
that the Garuberk Tower was the cause of everything. Suddenly, Raja feels
very dizzy and he collapses to the floor. Chaz thinks he's joking, but
Rika points out that he's got a tremendous fever. They carry him to a bed.

Chaz is still convinced that Raja is fooling around, but Rika chastises him
by saying that it's no laughing matter. Rune and Rika both sense something,
and Rune says that he has sensed it since they arrived on Dezolis, only it
is much stronger here. Chaz asks what they are talking about, and Rune
explains that the cause of the illness is the Black Energy Wave. He
surmises that it is coming from the Garuberk Tower, and that it must be
pretty close. At that moment, an Esper runs up into the room and shouts
that a girl named Kyra has gone to the Garuberk Tower all by herself. Chaz
asks who she is, and the Esper explains that she is a very courageous
person from their group. However, the Garuberk Tower is surrounded by a
forest of carnivorous trees. Chaz decides that they should go and save her,
and asks where the Garuberk Tower is. The Esper tells them that it is to
the northeast of Meese.


Raja leaves the party
```
