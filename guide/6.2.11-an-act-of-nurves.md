### 6.2.11. An Act of Nurves

> 🏅 **The Dark Sorcerer** — Defeat Zio. _(RetroAchievements · 25 pts)_
> 🏅 **Limit: Zio** — Defeat Zio. Combined level limit: 75, party of 5. _(RetroAchievements · 25 pts)_

```
Starting Party: Chaz , Gryz , Rika , Demi , Rune
Starting Level: 18-19, 18-19, 18-19, 15-16, 20-21
 // DUNGEON #11 ¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯\
|/  Nurvus                 \_________________________________
   Enemies: Balduel, Blauzen, Centaur, FloatMine, Greneris,
            Tarantella, TechMaster, Tower, Zios Guard
  Treasure: WAVE-SHOT, REPAIR-KIT, CRMC-GEAR, SPACEDARMR,
            PLSM-CLAW
 \
  \   Boss: Zio
   \______________________________


This is the largest and most difficult dungeon you have faced so far. You
may get dangerously low on HP and TP, so you may need to use HINAS or an
ESCAPIPE on occasion to rest up at Kadary before venturing back in.

You start in a green antechamber. Head up through the door and notice the
usual hi-tech décor. Continue along the passage and examine the panel at
the junction. Rika suggests Demi sends an interrupt command from there,
but Demi points out that the system isn't accepting any commands from the
outside. Apparently, they're going to have to get into its nucleus.

Turn left and head around and up through the doorway. Go up to the end of
this small passage and collect the WAVE-SHOT. Equip this new firearm to
Demi, replacing her STUN-SHOT in the process. Return back to the computer
terminal and take the right path, then head through the door at the top.
Follow the corridor upwards, turning left at the top, then take the top
path at the next junction and follow it to the elevator. Take it.

Head down the passage and turn left at the corner. Walk left until the end
then take the path downwards at the junction. Head along the walkway and
retrieve the REPAIR-KIT at the bottom. Return up to the junction then go
up and descend in the elevator.

Take the elevator immediately to the left, then go down through the short
passage. Turn a little right then continue down the long platform, and
pick up the CRMC-GEAR from the end. Equip this new head protection to Demi,
then go back up and around to the doorway. Go through and take the elevator
back up to the level above. Go right then down, and follow the path near
the right-hand wall all the way to the bottom. Turn left and head down at
the junction, then use the elevator to the left.

Head down and the right, and go through the doorway on the upper wall.
Cross the bridge that follows, then go right and then upwards at the next
corner, following the line on the floor until you reach another walkway.
Continue up this path until you are back in a corridor, then head up at the
following junction. Take the elevator to the floor below.

There will be an elevator to the right, but ignore it for the time being.
Instead, head down and aroung to the left, then take the left passage at
the junction. Keep going left and then turn up at the corner to enter a
small room. The chest on the left is empty, so open the one on the right to
obtain the SPACEDARMR, which should be equipped to Demi. Go back down, then
right and up towards the elevators, and then enter the one on the right
that you ignored earlier.

Go down through the short passageway, then head down and around to the left 
and continue all the way down the long platform that follows. When you
enter a small area with a door on the left and an elevator just below and
to the right, head left and go through the door. Walk across the bridge
and out into a small area, where a walk around to the left will take you to
a PLSM-CLAW. Equip this to Rika alongside her LASR-CLAW and then go back
around and cross back over the bridge. Once you have returned, head down
and take the elevator to the right.

Head down and around to the left, then follow the passage all the way to
the end. If Chaz, Gryz and Rika are not at least Level 19 (preferably Level
20), Demi is not at Level 16 or 17, and Rune is not safely up to a minimum
of Level 21 yet, spend a little time in this corridor until they are. It
would also be a good idea to use HINAS or an ESCAPIPE to get out and
return to Kadary to fully refresh the party, then head all the way back
down to this passageway a little better prepared.

Once all the characters are at the right Level and are completely healed,
enter the doorway to the left of the corridor to find yourself in the main
control centre of Nurvus. Set up the following Macro, in the order given:
Rune:  Use Item  - PSYCO-WAND
Gryz:  Skill     - WAR CRY
Demi:  Skill     - BARRIER
Rika:  Technique - DEBAN
Chaz:  Skill     - CROSSCUT
Head up the walkway towards the main computer terminal, just before which
point the party will automatically stop and Zio will appear.

Zio seems to be a little impressed by your arrival, but informs you that
you shall be going no further. He calls upon his god for power, then the
true battle with Zio begins.

 __________

As before, Zio will set up his MAG.BARRIR at the very beginning of combat,
nullifying all of your attacks. However, this time you have a trick up your
sleeve. Go to the Macro menu and select the Macro you made above. In the
first round that follows, Zio will summon NIGHTMARE, Rune will cancel the
magical barrier with the PSYCO-WAND, Gryz will enhance his ATK, Demi will
create your own barrier, Rika will further amplify this by increasing the
party's DFS, and Chaz will start the ball rolling with an attack.

From this point Zio will begin his offensive. His arsenal includes Hewn,
Corrosion, Black Wave, and a nameless red energy beam. The first two of
these inflict damage on all the characters (although little on Rune), and
Black Wave and the red beam deal a lot of damage to one character.

In the second round, let Chaz use CROSSCUT again, have Gryz attack, get
Rika to use SANER to make sure everyone gets to act before Zio, have Demi
use her PHONON, and let Rune use GIFOI. In the following rounds, Chaz
should continue using CROSSCUT (or GITHU if he runs out), Gryz should
attack constantly, Rika should use GIRES, Demi should attack, and Rune
should keep on firing GIFOI.

Get Rika to heal characters low on HP, taking into account Zio only uses
magic and that Gryz has very poor magic defence and Rune's magic defence is
very good. Always use Demi's RECOVER Skill if her HP gets low, and in dire
circumstances it may be a good idea to use some DIMATEs and let Chaz do
some healing, or even use Demi's MEDIC PW or a STAR-DEW. Whatever happens,
try to keep your characters conscious. Losing a character means that in the
following round both they and the character that revives them lose a turn.

If you watch your health and keep bearing down on Zio, there's no reason
for the battle to last more than eight to ten rounds.
A cut-scene follows the battle that begins with the demise of Zio. He is
confused by this as he was under the impression that he was immortal, but
it seems that Dark Force has abandoned him. Gryz is delighted, and Chaz
thinks about Alys.
```
