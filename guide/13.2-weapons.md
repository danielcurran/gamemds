## 13.2. Weapons

Most of the harm that is inflicted upon your foes in the game will come in the form of a melee or missile attack, by a character wielding a weapon of some kind. Each character has their own preference over which type of weapon they use, and there are a number of different weapons for each character available throughout the game. They all have the same basic purpose, but they vary in price and power. Some are better suited to a particular type of enemy, and some are imbued with special properties. Weapons that can be used as a specific Technique or Skill can only be used in battle. In addition, some Skills require the use of a weapon. This section gives a list of all the weapons in the game, followed by a description of each weapon in order of ATK bonus, split into the seven different types available. Ch:Chaz, Al:Alys, Ha:Hahn, Ru:Rune, Gr:Gryz, Ri:Rika, De:Demi, Wr:Wren, Ra:Raja, Ky:Kyra, Se:Seth Enemy resistance and weakness applies to the affinity of the weapon. Unless otherwise noted, all weapons are force-based. -----------------------------------------------
