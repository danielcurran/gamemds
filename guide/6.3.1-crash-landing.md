## 6.3. Cold New World

<a id="s6-3-1"></a>

### 6.3.1. Crash Landing

> 🥉 **Raja** — Recruit Raja. _(RetroAchievements · 1 pts)_

```
Starting Party: Wren , Chaz , Rika , Rune
Starting Level: 20   , 20-21, 20-21, 21-22
A short introduction to the planet of Dezolis begins this next section of
the game, after which a scene follows where you see the shuttle crashing
onto the snowy surface. The door opens and the party steps out, and Chaz
comments on their flamboyant landing. A green-faced old man approaches,
and jokes that it is raining machines. He seems to be a Dezolisian, and he
asks who they are, pointing out that they've destroyed his temple. Chaz
apologizes, and the Dezolisian notices his unusual-looking companions, Rika
and Wren. He figures they're from Motavia, then makes a joke about how they
came to arrive. Rika seems to find him quite amusing. Chaz doesn't share
her humour. Wren then approaches and informs them that the shuttle is
beyond repair. Rika asks the old man, who tells her that his name is Raja,
if there is anywhere they can fix the ship. He tells her there isn't, but
goes on to say that there is another space ship on the planet. Chaz asks
where, but Raja only agrees to say if they allow him to accompany them.
Chaz doesn't seem to keen on the idea, but Rika thinks he is interesting.
Rune makes the valid point that they have no choice, so they accept his
proposal. Raja then explains that Parmanians landed on Dezolis many years
ago, and that their spaceship is still in Tyler, the town of their
descendants, a short distance to the northwest. They then decide to leave.


Raja joins the party (Level 25)
¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯

Raja is a weak character like Rune, so leave him at the back of the party
where he starts. Keep the rest of the party in the same order they were.
Before going anywhere, equip the PULSE-LASR to Wren. It is less powerful
than his LASR-RIFLE but it damages all enemies in one attack.

Walk out of Raja's Temple, and you'll find yourself outside in the middle
of a massive snowstorm. A short conversation will then follow that begins
with Chaz commenting on the weather. Raja tells them that the storm has
been going on for three months, and even though Dezolis has a cold climate
he has never seen it so bad in all his life. Wren suggests that it must be
the Dezolisian climate control centre, but Raja thinks otherwise. He talks
of a place called the Garuberk Tower, which he thinks is responsible for
the snowstorm. He cracks another joke that only Rika finds funny, and then
you are given control again. Your next destination is Ryuon, so save and
and either get into your Land Rover or stay on foot, then head north.
```
