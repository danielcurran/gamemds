### 6.1.11. Fruit of a Thousand Years

> 🥉 **Rika** — Recruit Rika. _(RetroAchievements · 5 pts)_

```

Starting Level: 9-10 , 10   , 9-10 , 9-10

Make your way through Birth Valley as before, going up and then taking the
second right. Go right until the end, then go up then left and down the
stairs. Head up and then left, and then up past some injured people and
through the door that is now accessible. This will take you to a new area.


  ______________

            Ismounos, Neowhistle, Sensor Bit
  Treasure: CURE-PARAL, GRPT-CROWN, CRMC-SWORD, ANTIDOTE
Go up through the first short corridor. A little way along the next
passage, a red light will flash. Chaz and Hahn are terrified, but Alys
calms them down. Walk over to the panel on the wall and have a look.

"Sterilization Treatment Completed.
 Please follow the line on the floor."

Head through the door on the right, and head up through passage beyond.
Go down in the elevator, then follow the passage down to the central
column, where you should turn right. Follow the passage round upwards and
collect the CURE-PARAL from the end. Head back to the column and then go
down to the junction. Read the panel on the left.

"Elevator W3 To the West Section"

Take the elevator nearby down to the West Section. Go down the corridor,
then all the way to the left, then head up. Ignore the two open chests to
the left, and obtain the GRPT-CROWN from the next alcove on the left. Give
this to Alys, then read the panel a little further along the passage if
you wish.

"West Section  Unauthorized Entry Prohibited Beyond This Point"

This is very true, as the next passage is blocked by a cave-in, so return
back down, right, and then up back to the elevator. Take it to the floor
above, then head right and read the panel on the opposite wall.

"Elevator E3 To the Central Block"

Take this elevator down, then follow the passage right, up, then left. Go
all the way up the corridor between the capsules until you reach a
junction. Turn right here, then descend in the next elevator. You will now
be on a suspended walkway. Follow this all the way to the bottom to procure
the CRMC-SWORD, and equip this to Chaz straight away.

Go back up and take the path to the left. Turn down at the junction, then
go left and down, then take the junction to the right to acquire the
ANTIDOTE. Return to the main walkway and continue down, right, then down
through a door. In the following passage, go down, then left and descend in
the elevator. Go either left or right (it doesn't matter) and then up
through the door, then go around and speak to Professor Holt to begin a
cut-scene. Everyone should be at least Level 10 by now.

The Professor is fine, as he seems to have been aided by a pink-haired
stranger. She introduces herself as Rika, and explains that they are in a
research facility for genetic engineering, built over a thousand years ago.
Hahn asks if the place is still operational, and Rika replies that it is,
and also that it is not the only one of its kind. There are many such
systems that control the planet's ecology, soil and climate conditions.
The Professor asks why, if that is the case, such problems have occurred
recently. Rika explains that the system has run out of control, and then
suggests you speak to 'Seed' for more information.

Rika and Professor Holt disappear through the door above, so follow them
through. Rika will then introduce you to Seed, the control computer for the
Bio-Plant. The scholars are impressed. Seed warns the party that the
monster outbreak was caused here and that if things are left to continue,
the planet may be destroyed. Alys suggests they shut down the system, but
Hahn is dubious about the problems this may cause for the planet. Gryz then
points out, quite wisely, that no system is better than one that is ruining
everything. Chaz notes that to do that will require each system being shut
down, but Rika cuts it by saying that only the control system, Nurvus, must
be turned off.

Seed tells the group that in order to shut down Nurvus they will need to
rescue Demi, an android, who is being held hostage by Zio at Zio's Fort.
This just so happens to be the castle beyond the quicksand near Mile. Alys
is up for it, and Gryz is glad to hear that she's also up for getting rid
of Zio. Seed asks them to take Rika along, and it turns out that she is
Seed's child, an artificial lifeform based on a prototype from before the
'Great Collapse'. Alys agrees, and Rika seems excited about seeing the
outside world. Professor Holt decides to go to academy to let them know
what is happening, and then everyone leaves the Bio-Plant.

Seed knows that he must stem the outbreak of monsters, and so he shuts
himself down. Outside, Rika is overjoyed with all the new sensations. Then,

suddenly, the mountain above Zema explodes. The Bio-Plant is destroyed.


Rika joins the party (Level 1)

agile and fairly strong character, and should go after everyone except Hahn
```
