### 6.5.5. Proving Your Courage

> 🥈 **Towering Courage** — Prove yourself in the Tower of Courage. _(RetroAchievements · 10 pts)_

```
Starting Party: Wren , Chaz , Rika , Rune
Starting Level: 40-42, 43-44, 46-48, 40-41
 // DUNGEON #22 ¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯\
|/  Courage Tower          \_________________________________
   Enemies: Bladeright, BloodSaber, CrminHeads, Dark Witch,
            Hakenleft, Illusionst, Le-Faw-Gan, Twinarms
  Treasure: STAR-DEW, STAR-DEW, GUARD-MAIL, GUARDARMOR,
            GUARD-ROBE, ALGO-RING, RYKR-RING
 \

  \   Boss: Sa-Lews
   \______________________________


Go up and then left, and procure a STAR-DEW from the alcove to the left.
Return to the centre of the chamber and continue upwards until you hit the
top wall. From here, head right and then down to ascend the stairs to the
floor above.

Head up, left and then down the passage, ignoring the path on the left near
the top wall. Keep going down until you see a small path to the right. Go
in and pick up another STAR-DEW, then go left, down and left again, then up
to a junction. Ignore the stairs to the left for a moment, and walk up,
right and down, then take the stairs in this small room.

Go up and left, and climb another set of stairs. Head right and then down,
then pick up the GUARD-MAIL from the alcove to the right and the GUARDARMOR
from the area below you. Equip the GUARD-MAIL to Rika (she needs it more
than Chaz), and equip the GUARDARMOR to Wren. Return back up and left to
the stairs, and take them.

Return right and down and take the next staircase, then go back up, left
and down to the junction you were at earlier. Take the left path this time,
and climb the stairs to the next level. Go right, down, right and up, and
acquire the GUARD-ROBE from next to the top wall. Replace Rune's RFLC-ROBE
with this new protection, then go back down, left, up and left, passing the
stairs as you head up the corridor. Turn right at the top, then continue to
the far wall and take the stairs below.

Head left, up, left, and then down at the corner. When you reach the corner
at the bottom, turn right and climb the final staircase.

When you arrive, you will see two BlindHeads attacking a large being made
blue fire. The StoneHeads are dispatched quite easily, and control returns
to you. Heal the characters, then equip Wren's best single-target weapon
(PLSMLAUNCH or PHOTNERASR). Speak to the fiery being, and it will inform
the party that they must pass his test. This begins a boss battle.

 __________
| BOSS #17 \____________  ____________________
| Sa-Lews               ||                    |
|¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯|| Recommended Level: | 
|  HP: 5499             ||                    |
| EXP: 35000  MST: 1    || Wren: 40-43       /
|                       || Chaz: 43-46      /
| Weak: -               || Rika: 46-49     /
| Res:  -               || Rune: 40-42    /
| Imm:  Fir,Frc,Grv,Lgt ||               /
|                       ||              /
The guardian of the Courage Tower, Sa-Lews is a master of magical combat.
In the first round, have Chaz use CROSSCUT, get Rika to use SANER, let Rune
use LEGEON, and get Wren to set up his BARRIER.

Sa-Lews will use a number of Rune's Skills, including FLAELI, HEWN, TANDLE
and LEGEON. As three of these are hit-all Skills, you may be in for a tough
time in this battle.

In the following rounds, let Chaz continue using his CROSSCUT, get Rika to
use GIRES or GISAR (you may need to use NASAR following some of Sa-Lews
attacks), get Rune to use LEGEON (or NAWAT if this runs out), and keep Wren
attacking (or use RECOVER if he is low on HP).

As usual, have Rika perform DBLSLASH if there is no organic healing to be
done, and revive fallen characters with the fast-slow method, a SOL-DEW or
a REPAIR-KIT (for Wren).

Follow the strategy above, and Sa-Lews will concede in around seven or
eight rounds.
Sa-Lews will admit that the party has done well, and ask that they take
some objects as proof that they have met and passed Sa-Lews. Two chests
then appear below.

Collect the ALGO-RING and the RYKR-RING, and equip the former to Chaz.
Also, replace Wren's PLSMLAUNCH or PHOTNERASR with the PULSEVULCN again.
Now, use HINAS or an ESCAPIPE, or make your way out of the Courage Tower on
foot. Head back to Silence Temple and into the main chamber. If you have
already completed the Strength Tower, follow the walkthrough from '6.5.6.
Genesis of Algo'. Otherwise, head up and use the Regen Platform to fully
restore the party, then leave the Silence Temple.

Next, head directly northwest from the Silence Temple until you find a
secluded area containing red rocks. The largest and most central of these
rocks contains a door at the front. This is the Strength Tower. Save your
game outside, then go in. Follow '6.5.4. Proving Your Strength'.
```
