### 6.3.9. Bi-Millennial Man

> 🥉 **The Millennium Man** — Meet Lutz. _(RetroAchievements · 5 pts)_

```
Starting Party: Wren , Chaz , Rika , Rune
Starting Level: 26-27, 27-28, 29   , 27
Head downstairs and out of the clinic, then stay at the Inn to the left.
Leave Meese and pull out the Ice Digger. Drive north until you reach a
small wooded area, then head east and through a gap in the mountain range.
From here, head north between the mountains and drive straight into the
forest at the top. This immediately takes you out of the vehicle, and
Chaz points out that even with a vehicle they can't break through. Rika
then spots someone being attacked by the trees, and a battle then ensues.

There are three Carnivorous Trees. Individually, they are not particularly
dangerous enemies. The only thing they do each round is attack (sometimes
inflicting Poison). However, each time a Carnivorous Tree is defeated it is
replaced with a new one. This cycle never, ever ends, and eventually the
party will be wiped out. Unlike all other boss battles, you can run from
this battle, and this is exactly what you should do. Don't do anything in
the first round except escape. There is no point fighting at all, as you
cannot possibly win. Just run.
After escaping, a cut-scene begins. The girl thanks you the party for
rescuing her, and introduces herself as Kyra Tierney. She asks who they
are, and Rika explains that they were sent by Kyra's friends to save her.
Chaz says it was reckless of Kyra to go out on her own, but Kyra tells
them that she felt that she had to do something, as she couldn't stand
seeing all those people in pain. Chaz asks if there may be a better way
to get past the carnivorous trees, to which Kyra suggests that they see
the Esper leader, Lutz. She then tells the story of how the wizard Lutz and
a heroine named Alis saved Algo from an evil force two thousand years ago.
Chaz questions how Lutz can still be alive, and Kyra goes on to say that he
is hidden in the deepest depths of Esper Mansion, and that only top-ranking
people may be granted an audience with him. Kyra claims to have seen Lutz
from afar once, and that she could recognize it was him by how intelligent,
classy, graceful and generally wonderful he is. Chaz still can't understand
how Lutz could still be alive after 2000 years, but Kyra explains that he
is an unparalleled magician and a legendary hero, who may have even found
the secret of immortatility. Wren hypothesizes that it must involve
cryogenic hibernation. Kyra then tells them that she can't leave the people
of Meese in misery, and that they should go and meet Lutz. The rest of the
party agree, and then it's back on the road.
```
