### 6.4.7. Slash and Burn

> 🏅 **Limit: Lashiec** — Defeat Lashiec. Combined level limit: 133, party of 5. _(RetroAchievements · 25 pts)_

```
Starting Party: Wren , Chaz , Rika , Kyra , Rune
Starting Level: 35   , 37   , 39-40, 35-36, 34-35
The Air Castle explodes as the Landale heads into space, and Wren warns the
party of a shock wave. It passes unnoticed, then Rune expresses his opinion
of Lashiec. He thinks that Lashiec is pretty pathetic, being seduced by an
enormous power and ending up a mere puppet. Chaz asks Rune who the 'He' was
that Lashiec mentioned, and Rika proposes that it was Dark Force. The party
then return to Dezolis.

Back in Gumbious Temple, the Desolisian priest is very pleased that the
party have recovered the Eclipse Torch, and he has no qualms about lending
it to them. Chaz then announces that they are going to the forest of
carnivorous trees. The ECLPSTORCH will now be in your inventory.

First, remove the PLSMLAUNCH from Wren and reequip him with the PULSEVULCN.
You haven't had the opportunity to heal the party yet, so teleport to Meese
and spend the night at it's hostelry. Also, buy any required items that you
may be lacking from your trek through the Air Castle, such as TRIMATEs and
status-curing medicines. By now, your party should already be as powerful
as it really needs to be for the next area you will visit, so you won't
have to get any more EXP before you continue.

Leave Meese and jump into the Ice Digger. Head north and then east, going
through the gap in the mountains that leads to the carnivorous trees. Head
north and the party will get out of the vehicle just before the forest.

Chaz holds the Eclipse Torch aloft, and its intense light burns the trees
to ash. Rika cheers, and Kyra comments on the long mission you have
accomplished. Chaz suggests that the party now goes to the Garuberk Tower,
so head north and save just outside the tall tree-like structure. This is
the Garuberk Tower, so go inside.
```
