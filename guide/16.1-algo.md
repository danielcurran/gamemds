## 16.1. Algo

The entire Phantasy Star IV game is set in the star system of Algo. It is a fairly small system comprising the star Algo, an asteroid belt formed from the destruction of the planet Parma, the planets Motavia, Dezolis and Rykros, and artificial satellites (there are two that are mentioned in the game, although there could very well be more). The Algo star itself is fixed on a specific location, and the asteroid belt, planets and satellites orbit this star. The asteroid belt is the closest to Algo, as it consists of the debris that resulted from the destruction of the first planet, Parma, a thousand years ago. A little further out from the asteroid belt is Motavia, which shares its orbit with the artificial satellite Zelan. At the outskirts of the Algo system are Dezolis and the artificial satellite Kuran, which occupy the same orbit as well. Finally, the planet Rykros orbits the star in a highly elliptical orbit, passing inside the other planets once every thousand years. The star system has a unique purpose that it has had since its creation many thousands of years ago. After the defeat of The Profound Darkness, The Great Light banished it to a different dimension, and created the Algo system to seal the portal between the dimensions. This is the secret of Algo, and the reason for its existence.
