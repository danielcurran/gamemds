### 13.5.5. Armour

```
Similar to gear, android armour is attached to the body to add increased
protection from external damage. It is extremely heavy and durable, so is
only usable by androids due to their tougher physique.

Both Demi and Wren can wear armour.


TITN-ARMOR - 'Armor for androids. Made of a titan alloy.'
¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯
S            Demi begins with this body protection.
1500 MST
             Although it has a very high DFS bonus compared to other body
             protection, this is the weakest of the android armours.


CRMC-ARMOR - 'Armor for androids made of ceramic.'
¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯
S            Found in Plate System.
2000 MST
             Replace the TITN-ARMOR with this armour as soon as it is
             found, and keep it equipped until Demi leaves the party.


SPACEDARMR - 'Double-plated armor for androids.'
¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯
S            Wren begins with this body protection.
2500 MST
             This tough armour should be kept until it can be replaced by
             the ZIRCOARMER when found.


ZIRCOARMER - 'Armor for androids made of anti-heat-metal.'
¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯
S            Found in Kuran.
3000 MST
             Equip this armour as soon as it is found, and have Wren
             continue to wear it until he acquires the COMPO-ARMR.


COMPO-ARMR - 'Compound armor of sheet of ceramic between titan'
¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯
S            Found in Climate Control Centre.
3200 MST
             A strong armour, it should replace the ZIRCOARMER when found,
             and will protect him for a fair amount of time.


ELST-ARMOR - 'Armor for androids that has high elasticity.'
¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯
S            Found in Weapons Plant.
3900 MST
             This armour reduces shock on the android from attacks, and
             should be kept until the LACO-ARMOR becomes available.


LACO-ARMOR - 'Armor for androids made of high grade Laconia.'
¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯
S            Demi returns with this body protection.
4800 MST
             This highly protective armor should be equipped as soon as
             possible, and should only replaced with GUARDARMOR.


GUARDARMOR - 'Nothing is known about this weapon.'
¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯
S            Found in Courage Tower.
6400 MST
             The toughest body protection in the game, this armour should
             be equipped in place of LACO-ARMOR and kept until the end.
```
