### 6.3.6. Food Fit for a Penguin

> 🥉 **Pengo Land** — Take a walk with a penguin. _(RetroAchievements · 3 pts)_
> 🥉 **Musk Cat, Tusk Cat** — Receive the Silver Tusk. _(RetroAchievements · 5 pts)_
> 🥉 **Penguin Power** — Summon a penguin. _(RetroAchievements · 4 pts)_

```
This Dezolisian village is home to a number of Dezolis Penguins. There is
a man on the west side of the village who rents out Penguins, and for 500
MST you can have the party followed by a Dezolis Penguin. The Penguin
leaves the party if they go in a building or leave the village, and it does
nothing of any value, so there isn't much point renting one.

There is also an item that may be bought in the Tool Store called the
PENGU-FEED. This item can cure Poison, although the result text says that
the character's HP have increased. However, if the party try to leave the
Tool Store with a PENGU-FEED in their possession, a Dezolis Penguin runs
across the front of the Store and steals the PENGU-FEED from them. Again,
a completely pointless thing to do.

On a more practical note, it is time to buy some new equipment for the
characters. You'll want to get a FLAMESWORD for Chaz, two THUNDRCLAWs to
replace Rika's PLSM-CLAWs, and a PHANTAROBE for Rune. You could buy a
PHANTAROBE for Raja, but this is a waste of money, as you shall soon find
out. These few items will set you back 38300, which should be easily
affordable after your visit to Kuran. Equip them, then rest at the Inn if
you need to, and purchase whatever other items you may need.

Once you are done, leave Zosa and get back in the Ice Digger. Continue to
the east, heading a little north until you reach another ice wall. Plough
through it to the northeast, then continue a little east on the other side
and stop just before the metal structure. Get out of the vehicle, swap
Chaz's FLAMESWORD back to the PLSM-SWORD, save the game, then enter.
```
