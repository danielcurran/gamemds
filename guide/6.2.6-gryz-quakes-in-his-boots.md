### 6.2.6. Gryz Quakes in his Boots

```
Starting Party: Demi , Gryz , Chaz , Rika
Starting Level: 12   , 15-16, 15-16, 14-15
The Land Rover is a vehicle that can traverse quicksand and makes travel a
lot faster. It counts as an item in your inventory. While you are outside,
select LAND-ROVER from the item list to use the vehicle, and press the
cancel button to get out again.


Enemies in a vehicle on Motavia land: Forced Fly, GrassHound, DesrtLeach
Now that you have the Land Rover, the second job will be available from the
Hunters' Guild. It won't provide you with any experience points, but you
can earn a little cash. See '7.1.2. Tinkerbell's Dog' for more details.

Head north past Krup, turning right when you reach the entrance to Valley
Maze. Continue east until you reach the quicksand. Drive the Land Rover
northeast across the quicksand and then head a little north and into the
village of Monsen.

Head up and follow the path to the east side of the village. You will
notice a large crack in the ground surrounded by a fence to the south.
There is a sign blocking entry to the crack. It reads:
'Danger: No Trespassing Cracks In The Ground'

Speak to the man on the right, and he will tell you that Monsen has been
suffering from earthquakes recently. As if on cue, the ground then shakes
violently. Gryz gets into a panic, and Rika teases him for being afraid.
Demi supposes that the Plate System, to the north, must be the cause of
the problem, and Gryz proposes that they shut it down. This attracts the
attention of the villagers nearby, and they gather to thank the party for
their help. Although Chaz isn't entirely happy about it, he doesn't seem to
have much choice.

Rest at the Inn and stock up on any items you may require, then head out of
Monsen. It would be best to get Chaz and Gryz up to Level 16, Rika up to at
least Level 15 if you haven't already, and get Demi up to Level 13.


Enemies around Monsen: Caterpillr, Depcen, Fanbite, InfantWorm, Sand Worm
¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯

Be extremely wary of Sand Worms while on foot, as you are yet nowhere near
powerful enough to overcome one of these beasts. If you encounter any, my
advice is to flee.

Once you are prepared, spend a night in Monsen then head northeast around
the mountains to the Plate System. Save and enter.
```
