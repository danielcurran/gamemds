### 6.4.6. Castle Catacombs

```
Starting Party: Wren , Chaz , Rika , Kyra , Rune
Starting Level: 34-35, 36-37, 38-39, 34-35, 34
Return to the Air Castle, and head up through the main entrance door as
before. Continue up through the first room, and then up the passage to the
door that the barrier was blocking earlier. Now that it is gone, head up
through the doorway and find yourself in the small chamber with the Regen
Platform. Head through the top door and cross the area outside to go back
into the castle. Continue upwards and through the top door that the second
group of witch illusions was guarding. In the tiny room that follows, take
the staircase to the basement.

The route through the basement is a simple one. Head a little left and then
all the way down to the bottom of the passage, ignoring the junction to the
right along the way. Turn right and then up at the next junction, and take
the stairs at the top. Go a little up and then right, then take the upper
path at the junction. Turn left at the top corner and then go down at the
junction that follows. Descend the staircase.

Head down to the bottom of the corridor and turn left at the junction. Head
up around the corner and follow the passage all the way to the end to pick
up a MOON-DEW. Return down to the bottom and then go right, continuing
right at the junction. Head up the passage, turning left and then up again,
then take the next staircase to the floor below.

Walk down, then left, and then continue down to the bottom. Head right and
then go up at the junction. Go up a little way and take the stairs. Go up,
left, down and then right around the spiral, then go up at the next corner.
Head up through the small room and then into the next one. Collect the
STAR-DEW from the right of this room, then go up and out of it. Head left
all the way along the passage, then down at the end. At the bottom of this

corridor go left again, then up, right and then down. Ascend the staircase.

Go down to the junction and turn left, heading up at the corner that
follows. Keep going up, then turn right at the top and then up to use the
next staircase. Head down the passage to the junction, taking the right
path this time. Go up when you reach the corner, and follow the corridor
to the end, turning a little left and then up to take another staircase.

Head up a short way, then turn right at the junction. Go down at the next
corner, and continue down to another junction. Turn left here, then go
down and ascend the very last staircase. Follow the passage down and go
left at the junction. Head up when you reach the corner, and continue all
the way to the top, ignoring the path to your right en route. Go through
the door in this small room, then equip Wren with his PLSMLAUNCH.

Go a little up, then walk over to the right and stand in front of the next
doorway. Heal up all the characters, then head through the door. Walk up
across the thin bridge between the chasms, then take the ECLPSTORCH from
the box in the centre of the platform. Chaz realizes it is a fake, and you
are automatically flung into battle (you'll also discard the ECLPSTORCH).

Don't worry - it's only a Spector. Defeat it quickly and painlessly, then
your true adversary appears. This huge, dark figure will hobble towards
the party, and notice that Lutz has finally come. It appears to be Lashiec,
as Rune points out, and Lashiec continues by saying that he has waited for
this moment for so long. Rune asks why Lashiec is reincarnated, and Lashiec
replies that it is for revenge. He then vows that Lutz, and all who are
helping him, will suffer endless pain, and that he will kill them all. He
says that he will make them tortured souls like him, who can never leave
the castle. Chaz asks if that's why Lashiec lured them there, and Lashiec
replies that he has waited two thousand years to cut their throats. This
instigates the next boss battle.

Although he is on his own, Lashiec will be more of a threat than all three
of the Xe-A-Thouls. However, he is just one opponent and therefore very
susceptible to your party's best single-target attacks.

In the first round, get Chaz to use RAYBLADE, have Rika use SANER, let
Rune throw out an EFESS, have Wren set up a BARRIER, and get Kyra to use
WARLA. This should set up a decent defence and also start the offensive
against Lashiec.

Lashiec will use one of a few different Skills to begin with - either
THNDHALBRT or ANOTHRGATE (both of which are powerful hit-all Skills, the
latter being the most damaging), or POSSESSION (a single-target Skill that
inflicts Sleep on one character). In the following rounds, have Chaz keep
using RAYBLADE (or NATHU when this runs out), get Rika to use GISAR or
NARES (depending on who was damaged in the round before), get Rune to
continue using EFESS (or NAFOI when this runs out), let Wren just attack
(or use RECOVER if he needs HP), and keep Krya using NAFOI (or GIRES or
a curative item if you need a little extra healing).

Once Lashiec gets to 1/4 HP, he will use REINFORCE, which increases all of
his statistics. This makes him that extra bit tougher, but indicates that
he's almost down. Keep using the above strategy, putting a little more
emphasis on healing if you need it, or letting Rika use DBLSLASH if there
is no healing to be done.

If any of your characters go down, get a fast character to use REVER or a
MOON-DEW, and follow this up with a slower character using GIRES or a
DIMATE or TRIMATE. Alternatively, just use a SOL-DEW or a REPAIR-KIT.

Keep pummelling Lashiec and maintaining your party's HP levels, and this
ancient king will be dethroned in about six or seven rounds.
Lashiec curses Lutz, raising a bright blue sphere above his head, and adds
that although Lutz has defeated him he will never get the Eclipse Torch.
Quick as a flash, Rika dives over Lashiec and grabs the Torch from him.
Kyra announces that they've done it, and Lashiec begins to dissolve. As
he melts away, Lashiec shouts that even now he will not die, and will
continue to be reincarnated again and again. He adds that he must continue
to serve 'Him', then he turns to dust. Wren reports that the Air Castle is
crumbling, so Chaz gets them out of there and they fly off in the Landale.
```
