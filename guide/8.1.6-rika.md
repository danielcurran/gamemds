Rika is an extremely weak character when she is first encountered at the
Bio-Plant, and she stays with the party until the end. Her poor performance
at the beginning is countered by her extremely fast growth rate, as she
increases in power very quickly. Rika is a unique character, as she is the
product of over a thousand years of genetic engineering advancement. She
was created by Seed using this knowledge, and was taught a great many
things by her 'father' and her tutor, Wren. Her adolescent appearance and
nature, coupled with the fact that she is only one year old, is proof of
her dramatically increased physical and mental growth.

Since her creation in the Bio-Plant, Rika had learned about the world
outside from images and text given to her by Seed and Wren. As she gained
more and more knowledge of the world her desire to experience these things
increased. After Seed asks the party to take Rika along with them, Rika is
upset to be leaving the only friends she had ever had, but her thirst for
seeing the outside world exceeds the pain of this loss. She has a respect
for the world around her that the other characters do not possess, as it is
a new place that Rika does not take for granted. This, combined with her
fear of being alone, is what drives her through their journey.

Rika is a beautiful, strong and intelligent young woman, and cares deeply
for her friends. This sensitivity is sometimes difficult for her to manage
effectively, as she has had no real experience of such things since her
creation. Rika is attracted to Chaz from the start, but her lack of
understanding causes her to translate these feelings into close friendship
for most of the game. She acts as Chaz's emotional anchor, helping him
through Alys' death and other occasions where he is afraid or confused. It
is only at the end, when she is about to part from Chaz, that Rika realises
what her feelings truly mean.

Rika is the soul of the party, often adding innocent, yet sometimes naïve,
insight that only a child could possess. For although she has the physical
appearance and intelligence of a teenager, she begins with only the
experience and emotions of a young child. Like Rune, Rika is able to sense
the Black Energy Wave. However, this seems to come from a type of sixth
sense that gives her the ability to detect emotions in an empathic way.
This helps her to understand other characters' feelings, but also acts
against her when faced with the malign emotions of the Darkness.


Rika's favoured weapons are claws, and she wears mainly crowns and heavy
armours.

Rika has no offensive Techniques, but possesses a considerable number of
curative and assistance Techs.

Her Skills include distraction, special attacks and causing instant death.
