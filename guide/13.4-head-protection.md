## 13.4. Head Protection

Head protection serves to increase the Defence Power of a character. It is not as strong as body protection, but it can make a noticeable difference in the amount of damage a character receives. There are different types of head protection, each suitable for different types of character. This section gives a list of each item of head protection in order of DFS bonus, split into the five different types. The symbols following the head protection names indicate which characters can wear them: Ch:Chaz, Al:Alys, Ha:Hahn, Ru:Rune, Gr:Gryz, Ri:Rika, De:Demi, Wr:Wren, Ra:Raja, Ky:Kyra, Se:Seth -----------------------------------------
